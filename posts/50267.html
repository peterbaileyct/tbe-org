2nd Annual BBGC Color Dask 5K to be held Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50267 Published on 2024-09-01 07:00:00

The 2nd Annual Bristol Boys and Girls Club 5K Color Dash fundraiser will be held Saturday, Sept. 7 beginning the club.

Registration begins at 8 a.m. with the race beginning at 10 a.m. All proceeds benefit the BBGC.

Cost 5K Color Dash Run/Walk is $30 for participants 13 and older; Kids Fund Run for children under 12 is $5. Registration includes t-shirt for the first 300 to register, a sway bag, race program, water station and divisional and overall awards.

An afterparty includes an award ceremony, vendor booths, food trucks, a mobile pub and live DJ.
