A second threat uncovered at Bristol Central High School under investigation by Bristol Police

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=39747 Published on 2024-01-04 19:30:00

The Bristol Police Department will be investigating graffiti of a non-specific threat found in a boy's bathroom at Bristol Central High School this morning at about 10 a.m., a release announced earlier today.

Police investigated a threat via social media yesterday. That threat resulted in the arrest of a minor.

School administrators uncovered and investigated today's incident. Bristol Police are following up out of an "abundance of caution," as reported in the release.

There is no known specific or ongoing threat to the community, the release states.

Anyone with information is asked to contact School Resource Officer Gotowala at (860)584-3215.
