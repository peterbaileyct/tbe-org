Latanya Ferrell performs this evening at last Rockin' Out at Rockwell summer concert

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49505 Published on 2024-08-13 11:30:00

Bristol's own Latanya Farrell will perform this evening at the last concert of this year's Rockin’ Out at Rockwell summer concert series.

Ferrell performs soul and R & B. The concert begins at 6:30 p.m. and runs until 8 p.m.

Attendees are encouraged to bring family and friends, blankets and chairs, and to grab dinner at one of the food trucks, organized by the West End Association. Wednesday’s fare is Solid Rick Grill, Szabo's Seafood and Rich Farm Ice Cream.

The 2024 concert series is sponsored by the West End Association, Xfinity, Thomaston Savings Bank and RV Enterprise LLC and is hosted by the Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services.

For updates, visit BristolRec.com.
