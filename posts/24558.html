State Rep. Mary Fortier will host an Energy Assistance and Policy Forum Wednesday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24558 Published on 2023-04-04 14:26:07

State Rep. Mary Fortier recently announced that she will be hosting an Energy Assistance Forum at the Bristol Public Library on Wednesday night at 6 p.m.

There will be panelists at the forum representing Operation Fuel, HRA of New Britain, and the Connecticut Public Utilities Regulatory Authority.
