Bristol Sports Hall of Fame announces this year’s inductees

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=28397 Published on 2023-07-11 16:00:29

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame Vice-President Bob Kalat has announced the "Class of 2023" for this year’s induction ceremony.

Mark Dunn, Chris Michalowski, Chuck Nolan, Bob Nicoletti, Frank Owsianko Jr., Sara Paradis, Carol Langlais Panigutti, and Tim Washington will be inducted on Friday, November 17 at the DoubleTree in Bristol. 

"These eight individuals continue to represent the best of Bristols' storied sports history,” said Kalat, the co-chair of the Induction Banquet committee along with Hank Lodge.

More detailed information about the induction will be available on the BSHOF website,  bshof.org, at a later date. 

Information about past inductees, scrapbook pages, a video vault, and a way to nominate individuals for future inductions is also available on the website.
