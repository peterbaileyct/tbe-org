Memorial Military Museum will host the premiere showing of “Stories from WWII: Robert Fitz” on Saturday, June 18 at 1:00 p.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13724 Published on 2022-06-09 07:00:00

The Memorial Military Museum, located at the Bristol Historical Society building on 98 Summer St., will show the video production, ”Stories from World War II: Robert Fitz,” at 1:00 on Saturday, June 18.

Robert ”Dick” Fitz will be on hand to meet all who attend.

There will also be a special display of Robert’s memorabilia in the Jack Denehy Gallery.

Admission is free.

Refreshments will be available.
