The Central Connecticut Conference All-Conference teams for the spring scholastic campaign recently released

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=27577 Published on 2023-06-20 07:00:00

By Michael Letendre

The Central Connecticut Conference has recently announced most of its All-Conference teams from the spring campaign on its website.

And, as always, the list is loaded with student athletes from the Mum City.

Just from the Bristol Central and Bristol Eastern boys track and field teams alone, the grouping had 19 athletes earn CCC South, All-conference nods.

Here’s the most complete list of those winners as of Monday, June 19 from the CCC website:
