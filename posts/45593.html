Bristol Eastern girls track triumphs over crosstown rival Bristol Central, 96-54

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45593 Published on 2024-05-16 07:00:00

Bristol Eastern girls track defeated Bristol Central, 96-54, yesterday at Bristol Eastern High School, running their overall record to 6-1. The girls track team's next meet is Tuesday at the CCC Championships.

Among those taking first place are the following:

Amanda Noel: 1st place in 100 Hurdles (16.5) and 1st place in 300 Hurdles (49.8)

Princess Priscilla Dilone: 1st Place in 100m (12.9)

Kaelyn Gonzalez: 1st Place in the 1600 (5:51) and 1st place in the 3200 (13:42)

Ryan Braccia: 1st place in High Jump (5-02), Long Jump (15-09.5) and Triple Jump (34-04)

Lauren Ayotte: 1st place in Javelin (86-09)

Jamayrie McRae Arroyo: 1st Place in Shot Put (31-03) and 1st Place in Discus (87-09)

4x800 (Kaelyn Gonzalez, Punya Mohan, Ace Vu, Calliope Gallagher) 1st Place (10:45)
