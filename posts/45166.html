Bristol Eastern girls track and field defeats Platt on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45166 Published on 2024-05-08 07:00:00

The Bristol Eastern girls edged Platt in track and field on Tuesday by a score of 79-68. That sets up their season final dual meet against Bristol Central next Tuesday with a chance for first place in the division.

Bristol Eastern: 79

Platt: 68

Below are the BE first place winners:

Amanda Noel: 1st place in 300 Hurdles (48.8)

Kaelyn Gonzalez: 1st Place in the 1600 (5:52.56) and in the 3200 (12:49)

Ryan Braccia: 1st place in High Jump (5-00), Long Jump (16-00.75) and Triple Jump (34-04.5), and 100 Hurdles (16.89)

Lauren Ayotte: 1st place in Javelin (78-09)

4x800 (Kaelyn Gonzalez, Punya Mohan, Lauren Ayotte, Calliope Gallagher) 1st Place (10:57)

Bristol Eastern girls are (5-1) on the season and back in action next week Tuesday at home against Bristol Central!
