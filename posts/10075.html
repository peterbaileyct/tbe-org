Free COVID-19 testing at Centre Square- Hours are announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10075 Published on 2021-12-28 12:21:19

Genesys Diagnostics is offering FREE COVID-19 drive-up testing on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 12-7 pm; Monday and Wednesdays from 11 am-6 pm; and Saturdays from 10 am-3 pm in the parking lot at Centre Square across the street from City Hall (111 North Main Street, Bristol).

Welcoming teachers, drivers, healthcare workers, and the general public.

PCR & Rapid Testing available with results in 24-48 hours.

 PLEASE NOTE: the City of Bristol and the Bristol-Burlington Health Department are NOT in charge of this testing site, for more information, contact Genesys Diagnostics directly.
