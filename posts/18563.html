Wake hours for Alex Hamzy announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18563 Published on 2022-10-17 18:08:25

Walk through calling hours for Officer Alex Hamzy will be held Wednesday Oct. 19, from 12 p.m. to 8 p.m. at the Lyceum, 181 Main St., Terryville.

Those attending the calling hours will park at the Terryville Fairgrounds, 171 Town Hill Road, where they will be bussed to and from the Lyceum. The area of the Lyceum hall will be closed to traffic.

Alex's obituary can be found here: https://www.scottfuneralhomeinc.com/obituary/SgtAlex-Hamzy

There have been no calling hours announced for Sgt. Dustin Demonte. The family is having a private wake.
