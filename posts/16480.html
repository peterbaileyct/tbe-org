Parking Lot Patrol

The TBE Parking Lot Patrol inspected the parking lot on Mix Street near the ballfields on Sunday afternoon. After years of neglect and abuse, the lot is finally receiving much-needed attention. Hallelujah!

Ivy Drive School

With all this ballyhoo regarding school tours recently, this is an excellent time to reflect on a previous school tour.

Ivy Drive School, located north of Farmington Avenue, in the northeast section of town, opened in the fall of 1967. On May 26, 1968, for two hours, students provided tours of the elementary school for the public.

However, 14 years later, in December of 1981, school administrators discussed closing the school due to declining enrollment.

It survived and remains open today.
