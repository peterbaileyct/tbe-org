Bristol Public Library receives 2 honors, 1 for excellence in recognizing U.S. Veterans and another for addressing aging well

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51139 Published on 2024-09-19 07:00:00

The Bristol Public Library was recognized by a U.S. Veterans program and a collaborative focused on aging well, it was announced in a release late yesterday.

The Bristol Public Library received recognition as a "Top Performer" as a "Commemorative Partner with the Dept. of Defense's Vietnam War Commemoration."

"Your impressive dedication, energy, and engagement has identified you as a Top Performer, a Significant Partner instrumental in thanking and honoring more than four million of our Nation's Vietnam veterans and their families in twenty=eight thousand events across the country since 2012," the certificate with the honor reads in part.

The certificate is dated July 22, 2024.

The library also was recognized as a "Local Pick" as part of Age Well Across Connecticut, an initiative of the Connecticut Community Care. The certificate honors "places and programs that demonstrate age, dementia and disability inclusivity."
