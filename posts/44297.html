Bristol Central defeats Maloney in boys and girls track on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44297 Published on 2024-04-18 19:57:07

BC vs Maloney at Bristol Central High School

Bristol Central hosted Maloney Tuesday afternoon and came away with a double victory. The Central girls bested Maloney by a score of 100-49.

The Central boys defeated their counterparts from a Meriden by a score of 112-38.
