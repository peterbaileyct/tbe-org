Bristol Police Department seeks public's help in locating missing person

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54284 Published on 2024-11-23 13:10:08

The Bristol Police Department is asking for the public’s help in locating a missing person, Tara Provoyeur, who was last seen driving a gray 2012 Honda CR-V with misuse CT license plate BB76161.

Provoyeur has been missing since Nov. 21, around 4:30 p.m., having yet to return home after stating that she was going to store. 

Provoyeur is a 39-year-old, 5-foot-2-inch White, non-Hispanic female weighing 110 pounds with burgundy hair, hazel eyes, and a light complexion. She was last seen wearing blue jeans, a black jacket and black Nike sneakers. 

Tara Provoyeur. | Photo courtesy of Bristol Police Department

Anyone having information on Provoyeur’s whereabouts is asked to contact the Bristol Police Department at 860-584-3000.
