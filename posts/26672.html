Answer to Tuesday’s trivia question

At Tuesday’s Finance Board meeting, the City of Bristol Tax Collector reported to the board the amount of uncollectable tax income the city will have for the past year due to Bankruptcies, Deaths, Out of Businesses, or Whereabouts Unknowns was — $2,254,270.62 WOW!!!

On this date in history

In 1922, Babe Ruth was suspended 1 day and fined $200 for throwing dirt on an umpire. (He never did that when he was on the Red Sox.)

In 1968, the Rolling Stones release the song "Jumping Jack Flash.”

In 2020, a video of George Floyd’s arrest and murder while restrained in Minneapolis police custody shows he was pinned to the ground by police officer Derek Chauvin's knee for 8 minutes and 46 seconds and ignites widespread condemnation and nationwide protests.

Final thought for today

“It's May! It’s May,

All TBE readers, supporters and donors                

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week. This decision is based on our financial projections and, most certainly, to remind people that TBE is serious about providing accurate, timely and thorough reporting for Bristol.
