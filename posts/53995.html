Today’s City Meeting’s include Real Estate Committee, Police Commission

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53995 Published on 2024-11-19 07:00:00

The monthly meeting of the Real Estate Committee will be held tonight at 5:00 p.m. in Meeting Room 1, at City Hall, located a 111 North Main St.

The Board of Police Commissioners will meet in the City Council at 6 p.m. at City Hall. 

The Sewer Commission and Water Commissioners will meet at 6 p.m., and 6:15 p.m. respectively. Both meetings will be held at the Bristol Water & Sewer Filtration Plant, located at 1080 Terryville Ave.
