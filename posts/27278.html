Bristol Blues to have several promotions this season down at Muzzy Field

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=27278 Published on 2023-06-12 16:00:00

By Michael Letendre

The Bristol Blues of the New England Collegiate Baseball League have a plethora of promotions this year as the season has gotten underway from Muzzy Field.

Here’s what to expect when planning to bring your family down to a game this summer:

June

16 - School’s Out

18 - Father’s Day Celebration

19 - Be Your Favorite Athlete

21 - Pride Night

22 - Latin Night

24 - Little League (Softball) Night

27 - Polish Night

28 - Autism Acceptance

29 - Irish Night

July

1 - Blues, Brews & Cruise

6 - Christmas in July

9 - Military Appreciation

12 - Pink Night

13 - Thirsty Thursday

14 - Little League Night

16 - Bark in The Park

18 - Community Night

19 - 80’s Night

21 - Fireworks Friday

26 - Harry Potter Night (just flick and swish)

29 - Fan Appreciation

Don’t forget, individual tickets are $6 each this season and season passes are $99.

For more information on the Blues, the Blues are on Instagram @BRISTOL_BLUES.

NOTE…Three promotions that did not make the cut this year for the Blues includes ‘Bring your Forklift to Muzzy Field Night,’ ‘Count the Canadian Coins Saturday’ and ‘Math Out Night’ which would have seen all fans dress up as their favorite numbers (Canadian numbers would have been accepted as well, just at a discount).
