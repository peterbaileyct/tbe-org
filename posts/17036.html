Bulldog Cheerleaders hoping for another successful season

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17036 Published on 2022-09-13 10:27:13

The Bristol Bulldogs have 2 cheer teams, D8 & D12 (based on ages- D8 being the
younger team)

Last year the Bulldogs D8 team went all the way to Nationals in Kissimmee Florida while the D12 team fell just short of their opportunity. The teams are looking forward to another successful year with
hopes of both teams making the nationals.

The girls cheer every Sunday for Bulldog teams competing in HOME games.

Meanwhile the competition schedule/season starts in October.

The schedule below is incomplete at this time. The places and times are TBD/ the schedule is subject to
changes:

Oct. 22, 2022: Local competition @ Shelton HS
Nov. 5, 2022: States competition @ Hillhouse HS
Nov. 19, 2022: Regionals @ Rhode Island
Dec. 8-11 2022: Nationals In Kissimmee FL
