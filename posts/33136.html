Press conference to address racism

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=33136 Published on 2023-08-28 12:05:00

Recent white supremacist and racist activity within the city will be the subject of a press conference this afternoon at the site of the Infinite Hope mural on Riverside Avenue.

The conference will include addresses by Lt. Gov. Susan Bysiewicz as well as local speakers and community activists, including members of the state and local NAACP chapters.

The conference, called by Bristol Democratic Town Committee, will be held at 2 p.m. at the mural, which celebrates diversity and features images of author Maya Angelou, Martin Luther King Jr. and Bristolite Barbara Hudson.
