It is time to put away your bird feeder for the spring and summer

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24550 Published on 2023-04-04 14:06:06

It is getting warmer now, and it is advisable to put away your birdfeeder for the summer.
As Barry the Bear always says regarding bird feeders, “Lead us not into temptation.”

So if you don’t want to invite a bear into your kitchen this summer, put the bird feeder away.
