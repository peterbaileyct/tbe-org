Eversource announces streets scheduled for gas line replacement

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10754 Published on 2022-01-20 14:28:57

On January 19, 2022, Eversource issued the following press release:

Eversource Gas is starting replacement of gas lines in the following neighborhoods:

  • Driscoll Drive, Woodard Drive & Norris Drive
  • Oakland Street, Cedar Street, Leominster Road, Westminster Road & Wooding Street
  • First Street, Second Street & Third Street

Work will continue through May of 2022.
