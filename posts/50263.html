Police investigating a missing juvenile complaint

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50263 Published on 2024-08-31 19:06:24

Bristol Police are currently investigating a Missing Juvenile complaint. If anyone has any information on the whereabouts of Tylon Brooks, please contact the Bristol Police Department.

Brooks is a 13- year-old black male, approximately 4 feet 9 inches tall, weighing around 100 pounds. Brooks was last seen wearing black shorts, a white tee shirt and a green hat. Brooks was carrying a black and gold Nike backpack
