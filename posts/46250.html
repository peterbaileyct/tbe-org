Fallen tree limb blocks Broadview Monday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46250 Published on 2024-05-28 07:00:00

Yellow emergency tape and orange cones, each at opposite sides of an enormous tree branch that fell across Broadview Street Monday afternoon alerted drivers to the damage.

The branch from a silver maple that is among the few remaining trees that loom large over the street appeared to have broken away as the inside of the tree hollowed out and weakened.

Public Works responded shortly after being notified. They cleared the road Earlier in the week, work crews were busy replacing gas lines directly under the same tree.
