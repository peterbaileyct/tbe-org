Bristol Central boys track and field dominates against Lewis Mills in 111-39 victory

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44915 Published on 2024-05-02 07:08:10

The Central boys hosted Lewis Mills Wednesday afternoon and maintained their undefeated season with a 111-39 rout of the Spartans.

Standout performers for the Rams were Cassias Breland, with four first place finishes, and Patrick Wininger with three firsts.

Central improves to 6-0 with the win, and will host Berlin next Tuesday.
