Access to local businesses during bridge construction available from both Maple Avenue and Mix Street

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45369 Published on 2024-05-11 07:00:00

Businesses along Jerome Avenue remain open while repairs are being made to the Jerome Avenue Bridge. Edgewood Wine & Spirits, located at 568 Jerome Ave. and The Hidden Café and Greenhouse at the Bristol Adult Resource Center (BARC), located at 621 Jerome Ave. are accessible by way of Maple Avenue or Mix Street.

Repairs are expected to continue through the fall.

All TBE readers, supporters and donors                

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week. This decision is based on our financial projections and, most certainly, to remind people that TBE is serious about providing accurate, timely and thorough reporting for Bristol.
