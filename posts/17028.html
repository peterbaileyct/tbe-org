7th grade Bristol Bulldogs win first home game of 2022 season

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17028 Published on 2022-09-13 09:45:19

The Bristol Bulldogs 7th grade team made an impressive showing in their first home game of the year, defeating Wallingford 15-6 in a grinding defensive battle from the varsity field at Bristol Eastern.

The Bulldogs were led by the tremendous offensive effort of Dwight Smith Jr., who scored a touchdown and also returned an interception for a pick 6.
There was a collective defensive effort helping to keep Wallingford at bay. Bristol bottled up their opponent for the entire game, yielding a solo touchdown and controlling the line of scrimmage with aggressive play.
