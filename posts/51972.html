Bristol Central cross country teams continue to impress in meet vs. Maloney and Middletown

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51972 Published on 2024-10-07 07:07:00

At Rockwell Park on Friday, the Bristol Central cross country teams continued to show great improvement as the end of the season nears and the championship meets start this week.

Winning the races on Friday were BC sophomores Naythan Pellerin and Emersyn Mazzei, which bodes well for the future of the program.

The Rams also celebrated seniors Nick DiMatteo, Braydan Dwyer, Aiden Georgakopoulos, and Ruth Smith at their last home meet at Rockwell Park.
