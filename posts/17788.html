Scoreboard denier

Yesterday’s, NFL scoreboard claimed the Jets beat the Steelers 20-17. The Jets won? No way. Clearly, the scoreboard stole the game from the Steelers. There is no evidence and there is no proof, but everyone was in on it. The Steelers won 17-20.
