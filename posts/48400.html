Bristol Legion baseball wins 7th straight, a 10-4 victory over Torrington

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48400 Published on 2024-07-15 15:43:50

Bristol’s Legion team belted out fifteen hits and scored ten runs again on Sunday in besting Torrington on Sunday afternoon at Muzzy Field.

Bristol had production from the entire line-up against the Torrington pitching staff.

Rocco Spirito had three hits including a double while knocking in three teammates.

Connor Cyr, Jose Feliciano, Brian Grabowski and Thomas Pirog each had two hits, with Feliciano and Pirog getting two RBI’s apiece.

And Caleb Molinsky, Ryan Maglio, Caleb Simard, and Jedric Sutula joined the hit parade with a single each.

Maglio got the complete game win, allowing only three hits and two earned runs while striking out ten Torrington batters.

With the win, Bristol extended their winning streak to seven and improved to 12-6, only 2.5 games behind first place West Hartford.

Tonight Bristol travels to Fuessenich Park in Torrington in a 6:30 contest. They then repeat the same trip again on Tuesday. Same time, same field, same opponent.
