Bristol Police Dept. posts traffic patterns for Duck Race on Sunday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45005 Published on 2024-05-04 13:59:00

The annual Pequabuck River Duck Race will be held in Forestville Center on Sunday, May 5. The event will take place between 8 a.m. and 4 p.m., but a road closure of Forestville Center will occur at 9 a.m. to approximately 3 p.m.

Forestville Center will be closed and these specific roadways will be closed for the event:

  • Broad Street at Broad Place – no eastbound traffic
  • East Main Street at Lincoln Avenue – no westbound traffic
  • Central Street at Church Avenue – no southbound traffic
  • Central Street at Bingham Street/East Main Street – no northbound traffic

A no parking restriction will be posted and in effect in the area through the event. The Pequabuck River Duck Race will have crafts, vendors, food trucks and more.
