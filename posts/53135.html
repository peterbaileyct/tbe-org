'Celebrate Bristol 2025' calendars available at Wheeler

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53135 Published on 2024-10-30 07:00:00

Calendars for 2025 with images from a city-wide photo contest this summer are available for free while supplies last at Wheeler’s Family Health & Wellness Center at One Hope Street, and other locations in the city, including city hall, the Bristol Public Library and Parkside Café.  

The photo contest resulting in "Celebrate Bristol 2025" was held to help mark Wheeler’s move to a new community health center and administrative headquarters at One Hope Street. A panel of community judges evaluated and selected images for inclusion. 

Nearly 80 photos were submitted by people from Bristol and beyond, and images lovingly captured the very best of the city, from its parks and downtown area to its neighborhoods, trails, schools, and more.
