Hydrant flushing continues today in the northwest section of the city

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51696 Published on 2024-09-30 11:50:43

The Bristol Water and Sewer Department will continue its 2024 hydrant flushing season today, Monday, Sept. 30. The following areas may be affected:

  • Sandra Street
  • Ben Street
  • Jacqueline Drive
  • Farmington Avenue
  • Hannah Street
  • Christine Street
  • Gregory Road
  • Jasmine Street
  • Rosewood Drive
  • Stafford Avenue
  • Deerfield Road
  • Arbor Court
  • Ivy Drive
  • Council Ring Drive
  • Rambler Street
  • Kimberly Lane
  • Couture Drive
  • Marcia Drive
  • Sims Road
  • Camp Street, and
  • some of the surrounding areas.

Flushing will continue in this area until the next area is posted.

The process begins at 7 a.m. and ends at 2:30 p.m. Low pressure and discolored water is to be expected during flushing and for short periods afterwards. The recommendation is to refrain from washing clothes during this period. In addition, residents should run an outside faucet or the cold water in a bathtub until the water runs clear.
