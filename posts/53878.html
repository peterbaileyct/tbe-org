Saint Vincent DePaul Hope for the Homeless campaign is on throughout November

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53878 Published on 2024-11-15 07:00:00

The Board of Directors of St. Vincent DePaul homeless shelter have announced that their sixth annual Hope for the Homeless fundraiser/awareness campaign is ongoing throughout November 2024.  

Saint Vincent DePaul is the only homeless shelter in Bristol that provides a bed/food/safety/clothing/case management/referral help/and access to housing resources to move out of shelter.

The sixth annual Hope for the Homeless event will be held on Thursday November 21, 2024. This year's event is a comedy show and buffet dinner (doors open at 5:30pm/show at 7:00pm), at Mulligans Grille, 56 School Street, Terryville.

Tickets are $40.00 per person. For more information about this event or ot learn about the shelter, check us out on Facebook: SVDP Mission of Bristol @missiontoendhomelessness.

There are many ways you can help: Sponsorship opportunities (listed below), donate a raffle item, or simply attend and bring your friends!
