Bristol Central girls swimmers have strong performance in CCC South Championships

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9164 Published on 2021-11-08 08:56:14

The Bristol Central Lady Rams Swim & Dive team took home a lot of hardware and a 3rd place finish at CCC South Swim & Dive Championships on Friday night!  The team had 27 Season best times with 26 of those being personal best!

The following swimmers were All Conference Champions: 

Isabella Cordero, Nyomi Cordero, Rayiah Reinhard, Katelyn Holleran with a 2nd place finish in the 200 Medley Relay with a personal best time of 2:00.05. 

Katelyn Holleran with 2nd place finishes in both the 50 with a time of 27.35 and 100 free with a time of 59.77. 

Nyomi Cordero with a 2nd place, personal best and State L qualifying time of  1:05.29 in the 100 fly, and 1st place and State L qualifying time in the 100 breaststroke 1:13.50.  

Isabella Codero with a 1st place, personal best and State L qualifying time in the 100 back with a time of 1:03.87. 

Medals were presented to the top 3 relays and top 6 finishers in each individual event. 

Madison Pikiell earned 5th place medal with a personal best of 2:18.00 in the 200 free. 

Isabella Cordero 3rd place in the 200 IM with a State L qualifying time of 2:26.72.

Hannah Krueger 6th place and a personal best time in 200 IM at 2:51.91.

Christina Tomcak with 5th place on diving.

Rayiah Reinhard 5th place and personal best 100 fly at 1:08.81. I

Madison Pikiell 4th place in 500 free 6:17.17.

Alayna Lawson 6th place and personal best time of 6:23.93.

Milan Barresi 6th place in 100 breast at 1:28.10.

The Lady Rams finished the evening with 3rd place in the 400 free relay Katelyn Holleran, Madison Pikiell, Isabella Cordero and Nyomi Cordero.
