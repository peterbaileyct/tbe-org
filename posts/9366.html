Watch identification event this Saturday at American Clock & Watch Museum

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9366 Published on 2021-11-17 07:54:02

There is a special event scheduled at the American Clock & Watch Museum this Saturday.


Do you have an old watch that you would like to learn more about?

Bring it to the watch identification from 1:00-3:00 on Saturday, November 20th, at the American Clock & Watch Museum.

Join watch expert, Micah Tasker, as he guides you to discover information about your special American-made watch.* The cost of this service is $10 per watch and includes the price of admission.

For more information about Micah, go to TheVintageWatchMan.com.

*  Please note: museums are restricted by law, so appraisal values will not be given at this event. Depending on the number of patrons attending the event, the number of evaluations may be limited to three per person.

For more information, call 860-583-6070 or go to www.clockandwatchmuseum.org.
