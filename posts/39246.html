Police, on wellbeing check, uncover deceased person

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=39246 Published on 2023-12-25 20:55:00

The body of a deceased elderly female was discovered inside the bedroom of a home at an undisclosed address by Bristol Police with the assistance of the Bristol Fire Department on a wellbeing check earlier this morning.

The circumstances at the scene appeared suspicious and as a result the incident is being investigated as a potential crime, according to a release this evening by the Bristol Police Department.

The Bristol Police Criminal Investigations Division is currently being assisted by the Connecticut State Police Major Crimes Squad, the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner and the New Britain Superior Court’s State’s Attorney’s Office.

The name and address of the deceased woman is being withheld at this time, due to the ongoing nature of the investigation.

Further information and updates will be released by this agency as they become available.
