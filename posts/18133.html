Bristol dentists make annual Connecticut magazine listing

Five Bristol dentists are included in this year's Connecticut magazine's Top Dentists issue this October. They are Michael R. Ellis, general dentistry; Arabella Christian, endodontics; James D. Affenito, oral surgeon; David Fenton, oral surgeon and Robert Treado, periodontics.
