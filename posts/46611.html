Annual senior center fashion show and auction this Wednesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46611 Published on 2024-06-04 07:00:00

Contributed by Sharon Bachand

The Bristol Senior Center's yearly fashion show and auction will be held, Wednesday, June 5, at 1:30 p.m. at in the dining room at the senior center.

Among the music that models will be walking the cat walk to include the following: "I want to dance with somebody," chosen by Joyce Sergeant, "Girls just want to have fun," chosen by Rose Pelletier,Â "Billie Jean," chosen by Jean Boada, and "Shape of you," chosen by Carol Kallberg.

The fashion show stems from the senior center Boutique. It will be narrated by Sharon Bachand, and auctioned by Joan Vallee, starting at a $5 bid for each outfit.

The event is free and light refreshments will be serviced.
