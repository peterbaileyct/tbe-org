St. Paul announces the hire of Tony Mazzarella as head coach of the Falcon’s football program

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=40837 Published on 2024-01-29 07:00:00

St.. Paul Catholic High School recently announced the appointment
of Tony Mazzarella as their new head football coach.

A graduate of the St. Paul class of 1973, Mazzarella brings with him a wealth of experience and a deep connection to the school.

With a coaching journey spanning over two decades, Mazzarella brings a wealth of experience
in high school varsity football and lacrosse. His coaching career began in 1996 as an assistant football coach at Southington High School, where he contributed to the team's success until 2005. He joined St. Paul as an assistant coach in 2005, serving until 2022.

“We are thrilled to welcome Coach Tony Mazzarella back to St. Paul Catholic High
School. His dedication to our school, combined with his proven coaching expertise, will
undoubtedly have a positive impact on our football program and the entire school
community. We look forward to a successful and exciting chapter under his leadership,”
said Cary Dupont, St. Paul’s President.

Tony Mazzarella expressed his enthusiasm about the opportunity, stating, “Returning to
St. Paul Catholic High School as the head football coach is an honor and a privilege. I
am excited to work with the talented student-athletes, and I am committed to fostering a
culture of sportsmanship, discipline, and achievement. Together, we will strive for
excellence both on and off the field.”

After graduating from St. Paul, Mazzarella graduated from Trinity College. He has been the
president of Mazzarella Media, Inc. since 1982.
