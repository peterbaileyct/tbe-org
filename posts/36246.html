Dedication ceremony for 'Traveling Wall Memorial,' former Mayor Ward to be honored Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=36246 Published on 2023-10-27 07:00:00

A presentation ceremony honoring Vietnam veteran and former Bristol Mayor Arthur "Art" Ward in recognition of his efforts to bring the Traveling Wall to Bristol will be one of two events featuring the Traveling Wall at the Bristol Public Library at 2 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 28.

Paul Fitta, who served with Ward, will be featured speaker.

The second event is the dedication of a new exhibit of memorial and votive items left at the Traveling Wall/Wall that Heals during the time it was installed along Memorial Boulevard.

The permanent display honors members of the Bristol community who served in the Vietnam War.
