Speaking of wasting money

The chances of winning the Mega Millions is 1 in 176 million. The CDC says you are 30,000 times more likely to experience a bathroom injury than you are to win the Mega Millions jackpot.

According to LendEDU, the average American spends $219.54 on lottery tickets a year. In Connecticut, that amount is $321.41.

In 2017, Americans spent a total of $71.8 billion on the lottery.
