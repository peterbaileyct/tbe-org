Shots fired at Rockwell Park earlier today under investigation

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50760 Published on 2024-09-10 20:00:00

The Bristol Police Dept.'s Patrol and Detective Division is conducting a joint investigation following the undercovering of evidence that shots were fired inside Rockwell Park earlier today.

Bristol Police officers responded to a report that shots were being fired at the park at approximately 2:45 p..m.

While they officers did not find any injured person at the location and a check of area hospitals did not turn up any recent gunshot victims, the officers did find evidence that shots were fired.

Anyone with information is asked to contact the Bristol Police Dept. at (860) 584-3011 or via email at BPDCrimeWatch@Bristolct.gov.
