Bristol police fundraiser to be held Oct. 28

Jordan Oaks will be featured performer at a Bristol police fundraiser/concert, "Back the Blue," at Cadillac Ranch in Southington on Friday, Oct. 28. The event was announced in a release by the Bristol Police. All proceeds from the event will go to the Bristol Police Dept. in honor of recently fallen officers.
