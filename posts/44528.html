Bristol Eastern track teams split with Middletown on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44528 Published on 2024-04-24 10:17:27

The Bristol Eastern Track teams hosted Middletown on Tuesday and ended with a split result. The Lancer girls had a big day in gaining a 94-56 win over the Blue Dragons team.

The girls are 3-1 on the season and back in action next week at home on Tuesday against Berlin.

The Eastern boys were very competitive, but dropped a close decision to Middletown by a 83-67 score.

The boys move to 2-2 on the season and also are back in action next week at home on Tuesday against Berlin.
