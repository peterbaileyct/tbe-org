Voting at West Bristol? Use the Matthews Street entrance.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53472 Published on 2024-11-05 11:15:00

Voters at West Bristol School are being redirected from the Clark Avenue entrance to the Matthews Street entrance, causing a bit of a jam since cars finding out about the redirection are turning around in the parking lot, according to TBE reporter JoAnn Moran who is onsite.

In addition, voters at Northeast Middle School are being directed to use the Felice Road entrance, since the Stevens Street entrance is closed while construction on the new school is underway.
