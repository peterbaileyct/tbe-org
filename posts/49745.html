Zoning Committee meets at 6 p.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49745 Published on 2024-08-19 07:00:00

The sole meeting on the city calendar today, Monday, Aug. 19 is a special meeting of the Zoning Commission at 6 p.m. in the council chambers at city hall.

Among the items on listed at that meeting under public hearings are plans for a special permit for a residential development on Earl Street followed by the site plan for that property, the plans for an outdoor pavilion on the Bristol Boys and Girls Club property and a special permit for the removal and processing of earth materials off Barlow Street.

New business includes a revision to an approved site plan for a restaurant on the former Larson property along Farmington Avenue.
