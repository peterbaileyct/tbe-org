Bristol Police searching for missing juvenile

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=27596 Published on 2023-06-19 21:21:50

Bristol Police are attempting to locate a missing juvenile, Aubriana Cummings, age 14, who was last seen on Sunday, June 19, at approximately 9 a.m. when she willingly left her residence.

Cummings is a white female with blonde hair and brown eyes. She is approximately 5 ft. tall and weighs around 100 lbs. Cummings is believed to be wearing red shorts.

If anyone has any information regarding Aubriana, please contact the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3000.
