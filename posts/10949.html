Cynthia L. (Norton) Usher

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10949 Published on 2022-01-30 19:39:32

Cynthia L. (Norton) Usher, 69, of Plymouth, beloved wife of Ronald Usher, died on Thursday, Jan. 27, at home.  

Cindy was born on Oct. 17, 1952, in Southington and was a daughter of the late Frederick Norton, Jr. and Lena (Marotto) Norton. She was raised in Southington graduating from Southington High School. She attended Computer Processing Institute and worked in the office at Economy Spring until her early retirement.  She loved caring for her plants, sewing, and cooking.  

In addition to her husband of 35 years, Cindy is survived by her son and daughter-in-law: Scott and Stacey Hall of Bristol; two brothers: David Norton of Meriden and Paul Norton of Terryville; two grandchildren: Jacob and Lexi; a stepdaughter: Lisa Usher of Oriskany, N.Y.; three step-grandchildren: and nieces and nephews.
