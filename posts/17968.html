RHAM volleyball deals Bristol Eastern 2nd defeat of the season

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17968 Published on 2022-10-06 16:18:46

The RHAM volleyball program is a perennial state power. They won a state championship last year, their eighth since 2002, and they have averaged 20 victories a year since 2002. So the Lancers of Bristol Eastern knew they were in for a battle when the Raptors walked into the Eastern gym on Wednesday night.

But Eastern has a pretty good volleyball heritage of its own, and the Lancers gave RHAM everything they had, but they eventually fell to the Raptors in four sets. The scores were:

Set 1: 18-25, Set 2: 16-25, Set 3: 25-23, and Set 4: 22-25.

RHAM had some big hitters at the net, as Lani Fecho had 20 kills, and Ally Hadley added 19 kills. Both players showed all-around skills, as Hadley also had 15 digs, while Fecho was all over the court with 27 digs.

The Lancers were led by Lauren Bender, who had 20 kills and 12 digs. Emily Valentine chipped in with an ace, a kill, and 14 digs. Gabby Caucci also had a strong night with 3 aces, a kill, 2 blocks, and 7 digs.

RHAM's record is now 10-1.

Eastern fall's to 9-2, but there is a real possibility these two might meet again.

Bristol Eastern travels to AIC College on Monday, 10/10, to take part in the Volleyhall Classic. They then travel to Glastonbury on Wednesday, the 12th.
