School bus involved in accident on Farmington Avenue

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21545 Published on 2023-01-05 17:32:43

A school bus was involved in an accident on Farmington Avenue Thursday afternoon.

The driver, who was traveling eastbound on route 6, crashed into a utility pole.

The driver was on their way to pick up morning pre-school students from school; no students were on board the bus at the time.

No other details have been released about the accident at this time.
