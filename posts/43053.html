Old American flags may be deposited in drop box outside American Legion Post 2

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43053 Published on 2024-03-27 07:00:00

Anyone who would like to discard an old American flag may now drop the flag at American Legion Post 2 on Hooker Court in a new drop box constructed by a local scout.

The new drop box is part of an Eagle Scout project by Mason Chartier. | Post 2 photos

As part of his Eagle Scout project, Mason Chartier of Troop 23 & 29 constructed the blue and white droop box, which now stands outside the American Legion Hall by the main entrance. The box is available 24/7.
