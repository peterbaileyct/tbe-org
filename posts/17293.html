Bristol Police announce road closures for this weekend for Mum Festival events

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17293 Published on 2022-09-20 07:00:00

The Bristol Police Department has announced the following road closures for the upcoming weekend due to the Mum Festival.

The Memorial Boulevard will be closed from Friday, Sept. 23, at 1 p.m. for the Mum Festival until Sunday, Sept. 25, at 9 p.m.

On Sunday, Sept. 25, at about 1 p.m., the Mum Festival will be holding their
annual parade in the downtown area. The following areas and their intersecting streets
will be affected;

  • South Street
  • North Main Street
  • Main Street
  • West Street
  • Race Street
  • Riverside Avenue extension

Detours will be posted to help guide motorists around the affected areas. The parade is
expected to end at 4 p.m. Please plan accordingly.
