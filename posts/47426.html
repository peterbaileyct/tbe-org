Bristol Historical Society to conduct walking tours of Federal Hill this weekend

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47426 Published on 2024-06-21 13:59:00

Historic homes, churches and the green will be the topics of walking tours of Federal Hill being offered by the Bristol Historical Society, this Sunday, June 23.

Tours will leave from the Bristol Historical Society parking lot every half hour, beginning at 1 p.m. through 2:30 p.m. Each tour lasts 35 to 45 minutes.

Tour participants will receive a booklet with information about the sights so they can recreate the tour on their own.

No registration is required. Parking is available in the rear lot. Suggested donation for the event is $5 for BHS member and children under 14 and $10 for nonmembers. 

In addition, participants may explore the Bristol Historical Society, before or after events, where there will be refreshments and exhibits.

Provisions can be made for those who would like to skip walking the hill and join at green. Inquire at the historical society prior to the tour.
