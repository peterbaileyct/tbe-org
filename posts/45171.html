Sole city meeting today is Persons with Disabilities Committee

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45171 Published on 2024-05-08 07:00:00

Persons with Disabilities Commission meets today, Wednesday, May 9, in Meeting Room 3-1 at 7 p.m. on the third floor of city hall.

Among the business will be a presentation by Scott Heth, executive director of the Environmental Learning Centers of Connecticut on Harry C. Barnes Memorial Nature Center programs, updates and nature trail news.

The commission will also hear an update regarding Substitute House Bill No. 5241 – An Act Establishing a Bureau Coordinating Services for Deaf, Hard of Hearing or Deafblind Persons.

All TBE readers, supporters and donors                

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week. This decision is based on our financial projections and, most certainly, to remind people that TBE is serious about providing accurate, timely and thorough reporting for Bristol.   • Donors and financial supporters will have unlimited access as long as they log in.                

Note: Donors may have to contact TBE if they find they are being limited, since we will need to set up a membership account for you. Email editor@bristoledition.org for instructions.
