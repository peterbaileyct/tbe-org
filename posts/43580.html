Community Bagon Day is here!

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43580 Published on 2024-04-06 10:45:00

By Drew a.k.a ZypherFusion

Hello Trainers!

It’s Drew again! I hope you all had a great weekend with family and friends! There is a lot in this article to cover, but I’ll do my best to highlight the local scene exclusively here.

Community Bagon Day! It’s starts tomorrow, Sunday, April 7. Just like Litten Day, you can catch the dragon Pokémon Bagon, and the shiny form too! It’s from 2 to 5 p.m. I will be doing this event over in Rockwell Park, so if you are there, you can meet me in person!

If you want to check out the other hotspots for this event, look below:

Federal Hill Green, 33 Queen St.
Page Park, 651 King St.
Rockwell Park, 238 Jacobs St.
Bushnell Park, Hartford

If you guys purchased the Wonder Ticket, Part 2 is coming out! If you are a fan of Eevee and their evolutions, this will be perfect for you. Each evolution type gets a new costume, and this part is loaded with extra features. It’s $9.99, and it does grant you access to Part 1 and Part 3 coming soon.

Mega Charizard X is also coming to raids near you! Make sure you use your best dragon-type Pokemon to beat this one. This one can come in a shiny form, but the chances are 1 in 60.

This will gear us up for the Kartana, the Ultra Beast Pokemon! This grass and steel type is super weak to fire, and Charizard happens to be fire, and a strong fire type at that. Have a least four trainers with you for Charizard, and seven people for Kartana. The raids for Charizard and Kartana start at 10 a.m. today. Kartana ends on April 12, and Charizard ends on April 14, both at 10 a.m.

If you can’t make it to Bagon day, the Europe International Championships are here! The event started on Friday, April 5, and ends on Sunday, April 7.

This weekend, the biggest tournament of the year so far will host some of the most experienced players in Pokemon Go! If you want to watch them, go to this link below.

You also have a chance to get a Timed Research for watch 30 minutes of the event: Twitch.tv/PokemonGO, youtube.com/Pokemon. The start time is 1 a.m. PST.

Be sure to gear up for next week, where we prepare for the next World of Wonders event! For more information, visit www.pokemongolive.com. Until next time Trainers!
