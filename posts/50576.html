2nd Annual 5K Color Dash brings together hundreds for a fun run and block party

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50576 Published on 2024-09-08 07:00:00

The 2nd Annual 5K Color Dash Run/Walk brought hundreds of runners, walkers and volunteers downtown Saturday, with a finish line that filled West Street with rainbow hues and clouds of pinks and yellows and blues that participants ended up wearing.

John Ducham of Thomaston was the top finished in the male category and Brittany Telke of Bristol was the top finisher in the female category.

The daylong event began with registration at 8 a.m. and continued through the day after the event with a block party featuring food trucks, a bouncy house, live DJ, vendors and giveaways.

The event, which benefits the Bristol Boys and Girls Club, had over 275 participants.
