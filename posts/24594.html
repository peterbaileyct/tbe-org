Bristol Eastern's Model UN put its leadership skills to the test at recent gathering

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24594 Published on 2023-04-05 09:17:21

By Michael Letendre

The Bristol Eastern High School Model UN recently conducted a mock conference with its members.

Each team member partnered up to represent a country as they worked through numerous controversial international issues.

The group was lucky to have two local speakers at the event.


ESPN Security Director Mike Heimbach and Bristol Mayor Jeffrey Caggiano each talked about their respective backgrounds to the assembled group.

Heimbach retired from a long career in the FBI as Assistant Director of Counterterrorism.

He shared his experiences in law enforcement, dealing with international crime.
