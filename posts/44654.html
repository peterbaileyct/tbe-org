Bristol Police seek help in locating missing juvenile

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44654 Published on 2024-04-27 09:32:52

The Bristol Police Department is seeking the public’s help in locating a missing juvenile, 14-year-old Chase Sherman. Sherman is described as a white male, brown eyes, brown hair, 5 feet 5 inches tall, weighing 200 lbs., last seen wearing a gray and red hoodie sweatshirt, tan sweatpants and gray and blue Nike's.

Sherman was last seen at about 5 p.m. on Thursday, April 25, when he left his house in Bristol. A Silver Alert has been issued for Sherman.

Anyone having any information on Sherman’s whereabouts are asked to please call the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.
