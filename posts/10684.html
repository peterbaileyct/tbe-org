Bristol Lawmakers to host virtual pre-session legislative update

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10684 Published on 2022-01-18 18:43:49

For Immediate Release: January 18, 2022

From Jason Pheasant, Press Secretary: (860) 240-1347

Jason.Pheasant@cga.ct.gov

State Representatives Cara Pavalock-D'Amato (R-77), Whit Betts (R-78), and State Senator Henri Martin (R-31) will be holding a Virtual Town Hall to hear from residents prior to the start of the 2022 legislative session on Wednesday, February 9.

Date: Tuesday, January 25

Time: 6:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.

Zoom Meeting Link:

https://us06web.zoom.us/j/86216160330

The lawmakers would like to encourage residents who want to share their thoughts or concerns before the 2022 session to attend the meeting. In order to ensure any and all questions are answered, Representatives Pavalock-D'Amato, Betts, and Senator Martin are requesting questions be submitted to Cara@housegop.ct.gov,Whit.Betts@housegop.ct.gov, or Henri.Martin@cga.ct.gov prior to the event.
