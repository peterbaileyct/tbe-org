Bristol Eastern girls and boys sweep Lewis Mills in track on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44293 Published on 2024-04-18 19:17:21

Bristol Eastern’s track team travelled to Burlington on Tuesday to take on Lewis Mills and came away with a double victory. The girls team dominated in many events and came away with a 109-36 victory.

The boys had a closer meet but still emerged victorious by a score of 82-68.

Below are the Eastern victors for the afternoon:

Girls Track and Field

Bristol Eastern: 109

Lewis Mills: 36

Princess Priscilla Dilone: 1st place in the 100m (12.9), 200m (27.6), 400 (65.6) and 4x400

Kaelyn Gonzalez: 1st Place in the 800 (2:38), 1600 (5:49), 3200 (13:27) and 4x800

Ryan Braccia: 1st place in High Jump (5-01), Long Jump (15-01.5) and Triple Jump (33-04)

Jamayrie McRae Arroyo: 1st Place in Shot Put (31-03)

Rayven Duquette: 1st Place in Discus (68-09)

Lauren Ayotte: 1st Place in Javelin (85-04) and 4x800

4x100 (Madison Dixon, Rabiatu Bah, Anna Marrero, Jaryanet McRae Arroyo): 1st place (54.9)

4x400 (Madison Dixon, Princess Priscilla Dilone, Emily Rogers, Punya Mohan): 1st place (4:37)

4x800 (Kaelyn Gonzalez, Punya Mohan, Lauren Ayotte, Calliope Gallagher): 1st place (10:53)

Bristol Eastern girls are 2-1 on the season and back in action next week at home on Tuesday against Middletown!
