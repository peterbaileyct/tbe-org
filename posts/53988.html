Bristol Fire Department calls for the week of Nov. 4

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53988 Published on 2024-11-17 19:00:00

The Bristol Fire Department responded to 78 incidents the week of Nov. 4, including the following:

  • 18 unauthorized burning calls
  • 6 lockouts
  • 5 motor vehicles accidents with injury
  • 4 smoke detector activation (no fire/unintentional) calls
  • 3 each of the following: motor vehicles accidents without injury, good intent calls, medical assists EMS crew
  • 2 each of the following: powerlines down, service calls, electrical wiring/equipment problems, trash or rubbish fires contained, unintentional transmission of alarm calls
  • 1 each of the following: smoke scares/odor of smoke calls, Building fire, Cooking fire (confined to container ), Chimney or flue fire (confined to chimney or flue), Fuel burner/boiler malfunction (fire confined), forest, woods or wildland fire, Motor vehicle/pedestrian accident, medical assists (other), Removal of victim(s) from stalled elevator, Gasoline or other flammable liquid spill, Gas leak (natural gas or LPG), Oil or other combustible liquid spill, overheated motor, ring or jewelry removal, Assist police or other governmental agency, 622 - No incident found on arrival at dispatch address, Smoke scare odor of smoke, false alarm or false alarm call, alarm system activation (no fire-intentional)
