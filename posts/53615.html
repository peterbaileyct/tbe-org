Jukebox Bingo, sponsored by American Legion Post 209, at Gloria Dei this evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53615 Published on 2024-11-08 12:30:00

Jukebox Bingo will be held Friday, Nov. 8, from 6 to 8 p.m. at Gloria Dei Church, 355 Camp St.

The evening, a fundraiser for American Legion Post 209, promises a fun evening for families. Light refreshments will be served. Tickets are available at the door. Cards are $15 each for adults and $10 for children 12 and under.

For information, contact Bruce Barton (860)810-8160, Bob Getz (869)919-0305 or Brian Avery (860)384-5626.
