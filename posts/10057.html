The 42nd Annual Telly Awards honor the City of Bristol and MazzMedia for “Made in Bristol” video series

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10057 Published on 2021-12-27 15:22:00

The City of Bristol Economic & Community Development Department in partnership with MazzMedia have announced that they have been awarded a 2021 Silver Telly Award in the General – Promotional Video category for the “Made in Bristol” video series.

The videos were produced in the spring and summer of 2020 and released in the fall to promote several successful small businesses located in the City of Bristol. The videos were intended to encourage others who may have an idea about opening a small business in the City to do so, as well as to highlight the great places Bristol has to visit, instilling a sense of pride for those who live and work in the All Heart City.

“Interviews with the business owners were the foundation of the videos,” stated Producer Tony Mazzarella. “We were able to capture the genuine emotion and passion each of the owners has for their business. Their enthusiasm and respect for the city of Bristol was authentic. In the end, it’s emotion and authenticity that makes a video engaging.” 

Justin Malley, Executive Director of Economic & Community Development for the City of Bristol stated, “The ‘Made in Bristol’ videos were created to promote Bristol through the eyes of some of our most unique and successful independent small businesses. We thank all the businesses that have taken part and look forward to creating new videos in the future.”

The videos can be viewed on the Bristol All Heart website: https://bristolallheart.com/doing-business-in-bristol/made-in-bristol/#slivertelly

About the Telly Awards

The Telly Awards is the premier award honoring video and television across all screens. Established in 1979, The TellyAwards receives over 12,000 entries from all 50 states and 5 continents.

Entrants are judged by The Telly Awards Judging Council—an industry body of over 200 leading experts including advertising agencies, production companies, and major television networks, reflective of the multi-screen industry The Telly Awards celebrates. Partners of The Telly Awards include NAB, Stash Media, Production Hub, and Independent Filmmaker Project.
