Special Meeting of the Aquifer Protection Agency to be held tonight

A special meeting of the Aquifer Protection Agency will be held tonight at 6:30 p.m. at City Hall, located a 111 North Main St.

On the agenda is a registration request to transfer existing registration for Frank’s Transmission (APA2-2011-01 – Expiration Date: December 2025) located at 881 Farmington Avenue Assessor’s Map 53, Lot 1; BG (General Business) zone to 881 Farmington Associates Corp.

The Zoning Commission will meet immediately before the Aquifer Protection Agency at 6 p.m., at City Hall.
