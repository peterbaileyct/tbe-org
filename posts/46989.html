Thomaston Savings Bank across from Bristol Plaza remains open after car strikes building this morning

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46989 Published on 2024-06-10 14:48:10

The Bristol Building Dept. has evaluated the damage done to the Thomaston Savings Bank branch at 120 Farmington Avenue and cleared the scene, declaring it safe, according to a release from the Bristol Police Dept. The bank is open to the public.

At 9 a.m., Bristol police responded to a call, where a Mercedes sedan had struck the bank. The Mercedes suffered extensive damages, but there were no injuries, the report states.
