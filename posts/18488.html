Funeral services at Rentschler Field announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18488 Published on 2022-10-16 18:00:00

Funeral services for Sgt. Dustin Demonte and Officer Alex Hamzy will be held Friday, Oct. 21, at 11 a.m. at Pratt and Whitney Stadium at Rentschler Field in East Hartford, a press release from the Bristol Police Dept states.

The Bristol Police Department requests that members of the community wishing to attend the funeral services arrive no later than 9:30 a.m.

Sgt. Dustin Demonte and Officer Alex Hamzy were killed responding to a domestic disturbance call on Redstone Hill Road on Wednesday. Sgt. Demonte was a 10-year veteran of the Bristol Police Department and Officer Hamzy was an 8-year veteran of the Bristol Police Department.
