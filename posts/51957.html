Bristol Police identify motorcyclist killed on Mountain Road

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51957 Published on 2024-10-06 19:35:00

The motorcyclist who died in an accident on Mountain Road earlier today has been identified by Bristol Police.

Peter Speranza, 35, from Bristol died Sunday morning when the motorcycle he was operating failed to negotiate a curve and collided with a Honda CRV, police said.

The motorcycle was traveling westbound on Pine Street and continued on Mountain Road where the collision occurred, according to the initial investigation by the Bristol Police Department's Serious Traffic Accident Reconstruction Team.

Speranza was pronounced dead on the scene. The CRV operator was treated and released at an area hospital.

The roadway was closed while the scene was being processed. It is now reopened.
