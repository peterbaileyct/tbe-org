Rosa, Huskies to face Marshall in the Myrtle Beach Bowl

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20953 Published on 2022-12-19 07:00:00

Bristol football sensation Victor Rosa will join his UConn teammates in the Myrtle Beach Bowl today.

The Huskies (6-6) will face the Marshall Thundering Herd (8-4) in their first bowl appearance since 2015.
