Pavalock-D’Amato, Betts and Martin hosting legislative town hall on Tuesday, June 21

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14136 Published on 2022-06-17 16:00:00

State Representatives Cara Pavalock-D'Amato (R-77), Whit Betts (R-78) and State Senator Henri Martin (R-31) are inviting area residents to join them for a Legislative Town Hall to discuss any item addressed during the 2022 session. They want to hear from residents about their top concerns about bills that were passed, changes they'd like to see, or issues the state should address.

Date: Tuesday, June 21

Where: Bristol Public Library, 5 High St., Bristol
Time: 6:00 p.m. - 7:30 p.m.

If you are unable to attend but would like to get in touch with them, please feel free to contact Reps. Pavalock-D'Amato and Betts via phone at (800) 842-1423 or by email at Cara.Pavalock@housegop.ct.gov orWhit.Betts@housegop.ct.gov. For Sen. Martin call (800) 842-1421 or email him at Henri.Martin@cga.ct.gov
