Applications for those interested in energy field are being accepted by BristolWorks!

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52097 Published on 2024-10-10 07:00:00

Applications for a course exploring careers in the energy industry this spring are now being accepted at BristolWorks!

The specific area of interest is energy management, which offers opportunities in commercial business, government and utilities. Among the jobs being explored are those including consultants, advisors, auditors and project managers.

"Foundations of Energy Management" will expose those curious about careers to cutting edge technology and innovation. The course has been created to sync with degree and certificate programs in state schools, especially the Tunxis satellite campus here in Bristol.

To learn more about the class, contact BristolWorks! by phone at (860)634-5333 or via email at info@bristolworksct.org.

The course is $150, with financial assistance for those who qualify. The classes are held here at the BristolWorks! offices on North Main Street.

To apply, click here.

BristolWorks! is funded wholly or in part through federal funding awarded to the City of Bristol under the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
