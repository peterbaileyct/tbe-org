On this day in history

In 1906, the Wright brothers' flying machine is patented. The American aviation pioneers are credited with having performed “the first sustained and controlled heavier-than-air powered flight.” (Fédération Aéronautique Internationale)

In 1980, the arcade game Pac-Man is released.

(The editors of TBE will leave up to you, the reader, as to which of these two events is more important. But, because we are afraid of flying, we go with Pac-Man).

Central vs. Eastern

Tonight, at 6 p.m., Bristol Central and Bristol Eastern play at Muzzy Field. It will be Senior Night for both teams. As the great Yankee catcher, Yogi Berra said, "Baseball is 90 percent mental. The other half is physical." That will definitely be true at Muzzy Field tonight.

And whoever loses tonight, remember the immortal words of Tom Hanks (Jimmy Dugan), "There's no crying in baseball!"

Moon phase

Our final item is that tonight the moon will be beginning its waning crescent phase. Surgical operations are recommended during the Waning Moon. However, avoid surgeries of organs under the influence of the Moon Sign, specifically, the feet, toes, and the pineal gland.
