Pepper is a purebred Havanese dog with a sweet personality. She traveled over 3,000 miles as a puppy when her family adopted her from Germany. The sweet natured girl currently lives with her dog parent, Rich, in the Federal Hill neighborhood.

Pepper has naturally long, silky hair, which was recently cut and styled at the salon. She comes from a line of show dog champions which include her Grandmother and Father.
