Bristol Police seek missing youth

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50392 Published on 2024-09-04 08:15:00

The Bristol Police Department received a missing person complaint regarding Elijah Bermudez on Tuesday, Sept. 3.

Elijah Bermudez is reported missing. | BPD

Bermudez is a 16-year-old Hispanic male, standing 5'3" tall and weighing 130 pounds with black hair and brown eyes. Bermudez has a scar above his left eyebrow. He was last seen wearing a black sweatshirt, brown pants, and white shoes.

If anyone has any information about Bermudez's whereabouts, please contact the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3000.
