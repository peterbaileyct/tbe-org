William L. 'Bill' O'Neil

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21085 Published on 2022-12-22 07:00:00

William L. “Bill” O’Neil, of Bristol, beloved husband of Jeannine (Gervais) O’Neil, died unexpectedly on Friday, Dec. 16, at John Dempsey Hospital. Bill was born on Dec. 7, 1936, in Bristol and was the only child of the late William J. and Helen (Zipp) O’Neil. 

He was a lifelong Bristol resident and graduated from Bristol High School. He attended the Morse School of Business and served in the Connecticut Army National Guard. He enjoyed a 40-year career at Pratt and Whitney Aircraft before retiring. Bill was an avid skier until the age of 84, his favorite place being Mt. Snow. He also enjoyed playing racquetball, playing his last match on Dec. 15, the day before his passing. 

His love of good food resulted in him becoming a grill-master. His friends and family requested his famous chicken wings and his grilled chickens. He made his own rubs for the meats that needed them. He loved cooking for others and took great pride in his final product. His love of family was his greatest treasure.

In addition to his wife of 62 years, Bill is survived by two daughters: Sharon McLaughlin and husband, Charles of Farmington and Jill O’Neil of Burlington; four grandchildren: Meredith and Owen McLaughlin, Forrest Russell and fiancé Josh Dumas, and Jack Russell; and his beloved dog, Delilah.

The family will be holding a celebration of Bill’s life in the spring. In lieu of flowers they encourage you to donate to Brians Angels, PO Box 2111, Bristol, CT 06011-2111 or at BriansAngels.org.

Please visit Bill’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com
