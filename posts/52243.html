Broadview fire destroys garage and damages cars and homes

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52243 Published on 2024-10-13 07:00:00

A garage was totally destroyed by a mid-afternoon fire Saturday at 5 Broadview on the corner of Broadview and Goodwin streets. The blaze drew three fire engines, that responded within minutes to calls from neighbors.

The intensity of the flames melted windows on a house on the next lot up Goodwin, damaged several cars in the garage's driveway and the driveway next door and damaged property to the house next door on Broadview.

A neighbor captured the blaze in this short video, prior to the arrival of Bristol Police, who cordoned off the surrounding streets and Bristol Fire Department trucks and personnel.
