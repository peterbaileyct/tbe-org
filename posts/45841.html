CCC lacrosse tournament opens today with BE girls hosting Lewis Mills; boys co-op hosts Enfield on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45841 Published on 2024-05-20 07:00:00

The CCC post-season lacrosse tournament kicks off with divisional games for the Bristol Eastern girls and Bristol boys Co-op. Both teams grabbed the 2-seed via late-season wins and thus have home games. 

The Lady Lancers will have a rematch with Lewis Mills at Alumni Field on Monday night at 5:30. The Lancers and Spartans had a battle a few weeks ago with BE emerging with a 16-14 victory.

The Co-op team has a third match-up vs Enfield after picking up a 15-3 home win and 9-5 away win. Bristol will play Enfield Tuesday night at 6:00 at Alumni Field at BE.
