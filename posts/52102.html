Caminiti to be celebrated this Saturday at the American Legion and honored on Monday, 'Joe Caminiti Day' in Bristol

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52102 Published on 2024-10-10 07:00:00

Joe Caminiti, World War II veteran last known Iwo Jima survivor as acknowledged by the Iwo Jima Association of America, will be honored Saturday, Oct. 12, at the American Legion Post 2, in a program from 1 to 3 p.m. All are welcome.

Monday, Oct. 13, Caminiti's 100th birthday, has been designated "Joe Caminiti Day" in Bristol. At Tuesday's city council meeting, Mayor Jeffrey Caggiano read the proclamation acknowledging Caminiti and presented him with a ceremonial key to the city.

At the American Legion program, it has been announced, will receive a certificate from U.S. Sen. Richard Blumenthal.
