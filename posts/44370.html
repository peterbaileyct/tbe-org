Eastern girls lacrosse loses to Bethel on Thursday; Emma Stavens notches goal number 200

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44370 Published on 2024-04-20 11:24:24

The Lady Lancers went to Bethel Thursday night and lost 12-5 to a very strong team from Bethel.

Eastern was very competitive through the first 3 quarters.

The score was 8-5 at the half with Emma Stavens getting four goals and Leah Roy one for the Lancers.

The Bethel team tightened their defense and won the 2nd half 4-0 for the 12-5 final.
