Upcoming events for Veterans Day scheduled

Nov.  7

 6:00- Wolcott’s Annual Veterans Vigil and Luminary Ceremony to Honor all Veterans,
Location: Wolcott Town Hall, 10 Kenea Ave, Wolcott
This Ceremony will serve to honor local Veterans, Servicemen, and Servicewomen and educate about the Green Light A Vet Advocacy Campaign.
To purchase Luminaries email patriotsdayct@aol.com

10 a.m. Ceremony and Veteran Coffee House at Manross Library, Forestville

5:30 American Legion Auxiliary Luminary at Post 2. A special event honoring WW II Veterans and Joe Caminiti.

5:30 Bristol ELKS Lodge holds a Veteran Dinner.

8a.m. to 11 a.m. Veterans Day Pancake Breakfast, Southington ELKS Lodge. Veterans eat Free.

11 a.m. Veteran Day Ceremony at Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School (BAIMS)

 5 p.m. Veterans Dinner at Hill Side Community Church. Meatloaf dinner - Veterans eat free. 

Nov. 13

8:00 - 10:00 a.m.- The West End Association is sponsoring a breakfast for local Veterans at Jimmy's on the River.
