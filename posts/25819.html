'Broadway, Classics and All That Jazz' program Sunday at the Rockwell Theater

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25819 Published on 2023-05-05 07:00:00

The Bristol Chorale will perform a wide variety of music from Broadway at its "Broadway, Classics, and All That Jazz" concert at the Rockwell Theater on Sunday, May 7, at 3 p.m.

Among the offerings will be “This Is Me,” “Beauty and the Beast;” choral classics — “Let There Be Music,” “Let the River Run;” jazz — “Take Five,” “Ain’t Misbehavin;” and a live Jazz combo playing the Blues.

Musical director Rich Theriault and assistant musical director Roberta Verbyla lead the chorale, with Jane Theriault on piano. The theater is located at the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School on Memorial Boulevard.

The chorale will honor the memory of Bristol’s fallen policemen Dustin DeMonte and Alex Hamzy with a special performance of “Amazing Grace.” Also on the program is a rendition of Irving Berlin’s “God Bless America.”

Tickets are $15 for adults and $10 for children 10 and under, and are available from members of the Chorale, at Michaels Jewelers, or online by visiting the group’s website bristolchoralsociety.org.

This concert is funded in part by contributions of program advertisers and sponsors, including D’Amato Construction.
