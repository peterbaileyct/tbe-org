3rd annual 'Conversations in the Park' set for Aug. 3

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14916 Published on 2022-07-14 16:00:00

Kamora Le’Ella Herrington, founder and visionary of Kamora’s Cultural Corner, will be featured speaker at the 3rd Annual Community Conversations event sponsored by the Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services, and Bristol All Heart.

Herrington has worked with many organizations and groups committed to marginalized communities, according to her website.

The Community Conversations will be held at Rockwell Park Amphitheater on Wednesday, Aug. 3, at 5:30 p.m.

The Diversity Council will be present with newly appointed chair, Jaymie Bianca and two new members, Ronaldo Ortiz and Denise Powell.

“We led conversations in the past about diversity, equity and inclusion in the city and we're super excited to be back,” said Bianca.

Every year the Community Conversation provides an open forum for discussions among community members and leaders of the City of Bristol, while promoting a diverse, equitable and inclusive community, according to the Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services website.

 “I always come away with something really positive,” said Bianca. It's something that's really beneficial for our community.”
