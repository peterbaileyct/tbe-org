Hello Trainers!

It’s Drew with some brand-new Pokémon Go news for you!

The World of Wonders event has taken off, and this week, it’s about the Paldean Showcase!

In case if you hadn’t noticed, Sprigatito, Fuecoco and Quaxly, are spawning more frequently as we gear up for the next fun-filled week catching Pokémon and getting into raids. Make sure you look carefully; if you win these showcases, (which are located at various Poké Stops across town,) you could get premium rewards which are challenging to come by!

The events, like many events, start at 10 a.m. For this event, it started on Wednesday, March 6, ends on Friday, March 8, at 8 p.m. Listed below are places the showcases will take place:

33 Queen St. (The Bristol Green)
651 King St. (Page Park)
238 Jacobs St. (Rockwell Park)

Another tip for trainers catching Pokémon, Ditto has now changed its disguise yet again! Here are some known encounters to catch this Kanto favorite, and it also can show up as a shiny Pokémon:

Oddish Koffing Rhyhorn Goldeen Spinarak Numel Bidoof Gothita Solosis Bergmite Stufful

Many Special Research (tasks the game assigns you to do while you play) require this Pocket Monster to be caught to complete your tasks. This Pokémon can be very useful in the upcoming raids battles because it has an attack known as Transform. It copies the Pokémon you are battling against, along with its moves and stats! Try looking into various parks this weekend and use items like Incense to lure more Pokémon out this weekend.

These are some tidbits for you guys this week. Be sure to gear up for next week, where Community Day will be all over Bristol looking for Litten! For more information, visit www.pokemongolive.com.
