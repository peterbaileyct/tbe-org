Erick Rosengren sworn in as city councilor for First District open seat at Tuesday’s City Council meeting

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24866 Published on 2023-04-12 16:00:00

At the City Council meeting Tuesday night, Erick Rosengren was sworn in as a city councilor to fill the position vacated by Jolene Lusitani, who resigned recently.

Rosengren has served the city as a board member on the Board of Assessment Appeals and as an alternate on the Housing Code Appeals Board. He will finish out Lusitani’s term which ends in November of this year.

Rosengren replied to congratulations on Facebook by saying, “I’m just honored to be able to serve the community I love with a great team. Thank you all.”
