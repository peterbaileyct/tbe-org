According to the Hartford Courant, Miss Clark travels to West Hartford

According to the Hartford Courant, on this date in 1935, Bristol resident Miss Ida M. Clark, a member of the Women’s Christian Temperance Union of Hartford County, led a discussion at the West Hartford Baptist Church on a wide variety of topics.
