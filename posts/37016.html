Bristol, once blue, now red

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=37016 Published on 2023-11-08 13:59:00

In this recent election cycle, the Democrats levied numerous charges against the Republicans, from out-and-out racism to antisemitism to the misuse of a city credit card and to standing in front of them to take bows.

And when the votes were tabulated Tuesday night, none of it mattered. Simply put, Bristol responded with an overwhelming yawn.  Incumbent Mayor Jeffrey Caggiano and the Republican party clobbered the Democrats in an overwhelming and message-sending victory.

Bristol, once a blue, is now most certainly red. One could say it is now decidedly MAGA.

With this, what can only be considered a mandate, the Republicans can now put their thumbprint on downtown development and other matters of concern.

And for the Democrats, they need not go gently into the night. They can learn a lesson from the Republicans of 2019 by regaining their footing and remaining a vocal opposition.
