Bristol Co-op swim team remains perfect at 9-0 with victory over Plainville

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11369 Published on 2022-02-16 21:10:27

The Bristol Co-op swim team won their meet at Plainville Tuesday afternoon, 96-53, bringing their record to a perfect 9-0. 

The Bristol swimmers achieved 7 best times and many first place finishes.

First place finishes were won by:

200 Medley Relay - Luke Losacano, Jacob Wilson, Thomas Masotti and Quentin Hernandez. 

200 Free - Aidan Colaianni not only placed first, but took 21 seconds off his best time.  Colaianni also placed first in the 100 Breast. 

Thomas Masotti took 1st place in both the 200 IM and the 100 fly.  

In both of his events, 50 free and 500 free, Konrad Truszkowski took first place and had a best time.

Jedric Sutula first place and had his best diving score. 

Bristol swims next against Berlin at home on Friday at 3:45.
