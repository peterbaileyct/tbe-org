Jimmy & The Parrots concert at Downtown Live Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50511 Published on 2024-09-06 07:00:00

Jimmy & The Parrots, one of the most requested Jimmy Buffett cover bands in the country, will perform at the Rockwell Theater on Memorial Boulevard, Saturday, Sept. 7. Doors open at 6:30 p.m. and the show starts at 7 p.m.

The group sings Buffett favorites as well as new and classic rock 'n roll, including originals songs writte by lead singer/guitarist Jimmy Maraventano.

Their cover songs include favorites by the Beach Boys, Zac Brown, Jerry Jeff Walker, Harry Belafonte and Bob Marley.

The evening includes a Jimmy Buffet Look Alike Contest. The winner takes home a gift card to Monterrey Mexican Restaurant.
