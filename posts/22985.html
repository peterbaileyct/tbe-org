When Tyler Martin and Jack Stavens took a trip to Washington DC this past week, it was all about tasty tacos

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=22985 Published on 2023-02-13 07:00:00

By Michael Letendre

A couple of Bristol Eastern’s finest alumni were busy down in Washington DC last week.

UConn sophomores Tyler Martin and Jack Stavens partnered with three other business school students for the Kogod Case Competition. 

The group had to create a business plan for Chaia Tacos in the Georgetown section of Washington DC.

According to its website, ‘Chaia is an unconventional shop serving insanely delicious tacos’ which seemed like a delicious prospect for the crew from Connecticut to tackle.

Martin, Stavens and crew advanced all the way to the national competition and flew into Washington over February 9-11.

American University was the host school for the final round of competition.

Martin and Stavens enjoyed three days’ worth of events in our nation’s capital and put a tremendous amount of work in to earn the trip to Washington DC.
