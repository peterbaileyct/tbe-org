North Main Street closed for Candle Light Vigil

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=35356 Published on 2023-10-11 07:00:00

The North Main Street area in front of the Bristol Police Department will be closed at 6 p.m. on Thursday, Oct. 12 for the Candle Light Vigil honoring Lt. Dustin DeMonte and Sgt. Alex Hamzy.

The candlelight vigil is expected to be from 7 p.m. to 7:45 p.m. The public is asked to bring candles and to park in lots and businesses in the surrounding area, including, but not limited to, those across the street from the police department.

Parking is also available in the parking garage behind the police department, accessible from Meadow St.
