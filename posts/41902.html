Bristol Police make narcotics arrest

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41902 Published on 2024-02-29 07:00:00

Two Bristol residents were charged with intent to sell narcotics as the result of a Bristol Police Department Narcotic Enforcement Team and Drug Enforcement Administration investigation that began in January, according to a Police Dept. release yesterday.

A search and seizure warrant was obtained and served on Feb. 27 for 53 Mark St., with the result that the two individuals, Dean Lapointe and Kaitlyn Shoplick, were found to be in possession of illegal narcotics and large sum of money.

They were both charged with possession with intent to sell.
