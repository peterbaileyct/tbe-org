Bristol Police seek help in locating a missing 14-year-old

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51817 Published on 2024-10-02 07:45:00

A Silver Alert has been issued for 14-year-old Solmary Leet, a mixed-race female, 5 feet, inch tall, weighing approximately 140 pounds, with dark hair tinged with red/burgundy coloring.

Lee was last seen wearing a beige skirt and matching beige crop top and white sandals. She left her home on the morning of Oct. 1. She is not believed to be endangered, according to the release from the Bristol Police Dept.

Anyone having any information on Lee's whereabouts is asked to please call the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.
