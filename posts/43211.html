Misfit Moms' egg hunt part of day-long event Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43211 Published on 2024-04-05 07:00:00

The 2nd annual Spring Egg-Stravaganza! at Rockwell Park will feature a handful of events throughout the day on Saturday, April 6, beginning at 8 a.m., as part of a fundraiser for and sponsored by Misfit Moms. The event requires pre-registration and the purchase of tickets from the Misfit Moms website in advance. Not tickets will be on sale Saturday. Click here.

Among the activities are the following:

  • 100,000 hidden egg hunt through the day
  • Rubber duck races throughout the day
  • Breakfast with the Easter Bunny 8 - 10 a.m.
  • Car Show 8 -11 a.m.
  • Bounce Houses & Jumbo Slides 
  • Kids Zones
  • Train Rides
  • Live performances, local vendors, food trucks & more!

The Misfit Mamas Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
