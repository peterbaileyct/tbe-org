15-year-old reported missing

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50030 Published on 2024-08-25 19:30:00

Daron Brown, a 15-year-old black male standing 6 feet 2 inches tall and weighing 140 pounds, was reported missing Sunday, Aug. 25, according to Bristol Police.

Daron Brown | BPD photo

Brown was last seen wearing a gray Nike hooded sweatshirt and white Puma basketball sneakers.

If anyone has any information about Brown's whereabouts, please contact the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3000.
