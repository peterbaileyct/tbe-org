Interfaith Thanksgiving Service scheduled for Wednesday evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20174 Published on 2022-11-22 07:00:00

The Bristol Interfaith Coalition is inviting all Bristol residents to join them for the Annual Bristol Interfaith Thanksgiving Service on Wednesday evening at 7:30 p.m..


It is an opportunity for the people of Bristol to come together as a community of many faiths, joined in gratitude for each other and for the blessings all share.

The 2022 theme is Feeding Our Neighbors.

Here are the details:

Wednesday, November 23, 7:30 p.m., at Prospect United Methodist Church, 99 Summer Street

The service will also be streamed on the Prospect United Methodist Church Facebook page.
