Today's farmers market features New Cambridge Project

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47791 Published on 2024-06-29 07:00:00

The featured vendor at this Saturday's Bristol Farmers Market is the New Cambridge Project, located at the former site of Firefly Hollow.

The market is open Saturdays, through Nov. 2, at its new location in the parking lot at 10 Main St., from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m., rain or shine.

Among the vendors at each week's market are Better Baking By Beth, Continuum Distilling, Chicken Frosty, Gresczyk Farms, CBG Gurus, Amazing Love, Soothing Ways, Ridge Runner Soaps, the Ancient Flavors, Perkins Pure Maple Sugar House, Pure Goodness Bakery, Tonn's Marketplace and Twin Pines Farm.

The venue features free musical entertainment, accepts Senior Farmers Market Nutrition Vouches and WIC as well as Double Snap and EBT.
