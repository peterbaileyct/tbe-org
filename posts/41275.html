Name of Middle Street fatality victim released by Bristol Police, services Saturday in Plainville

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41275 Published on 2024-02-10 07:00:00

The name of the pedestrian struck and killed crossing Middle Street earlier in February was released yesterday by the Bristol Police.

The female pedestrian has been identified as Felicia Emily Yetke, 36, from Bristol, who was struck late on the evening of Feb. 1, and after receiving medical and transported to an area hospital, was pronounced deceased early on the morning of Feb. 2.

Yetke leaves behind two children and her husband, Kyle David Yetke, and parents Silvestre and Stephanie (Henry) Santos, in-laws David and Cheryl Yetke, cousins and many aunts, uncles and friends, according to the obituary.

A 2005 Bristol Central High School graduate, Yetke's "hard work ethics, strength and determination were evident in every aspect of her life ... Felicia was a loving and selfless mother. Her children were the center of her universe." Her obituary can be found here.

A Celebration of Life, for Yetke, will be held Saturday, Dec. 10 at 12 noon at Plainville Funeral Home, 81 Broad St., in Plainville. Calling hours will be from 11 a.m. to 12 noon, preceding the services.

A GoFundMe fundraiser is being conducted to help provide the family "some financial relief during this unimaginable time." As of Friday evening, 360 donations have been received. For a link, click here.

This crash is currently under investigation. Anyone with information is encouraged to contact Officer Spencer Boisvert at 860 584-3036 or spencerboisvert@bristolct.gov.
