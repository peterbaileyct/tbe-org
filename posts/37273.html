It's Tuesday, a little to the right, a little to the left. We have arrived, people.

Weather

In the AM, overcast. Later, sunny around 2 PM, with temps near 45. Come on. Wind 1-12 mph, with gusts to 26 mph.

Some city meetings

Joint Board at 6:45 p.m. with first City Council meeting post-election, following in new city council chamber. Special Bristol Board of Education meeting at 6 p.m,, BoE auditorium.

What kind of day for the movies

Movies released on this day through the years: "Dumb and Dumber To," "Ryan and Dean's Excellent Adventure" and "Christmas in the Heartland." But then again, there is also "Love Actually" and "Quantum of Solace."

So much for the ground game

On this day in 1943, the Chicago Bear's Sid Luckman of the famous jump pass leads his team to a 56-7 win over the New York Giants, 56-7, becoming the first professional quarterback to pass for over 400 yards (433) and seven touchdowns.

My advice

Tuck in your chin, Bristol. Brace yourself.
