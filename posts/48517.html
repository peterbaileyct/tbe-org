Bristol American Legion defeats Torrington, 11-3

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48517 Published on 2024-07-18 10:00:00

Tom Pirog allowed two hits over five and three quarters innings and Jose Feliciano scored three runs with four RBIs as Bristol American Legion baseball defeated Torrington 11-3 on Tuesday in Torrington.

Rocco Spirito added a two-run homer. Connor Cyr and Ryan Maglio each scored two runs in the victory.
