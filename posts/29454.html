On this date in Bristol

1965

The city of Bristol announced that as part of a new highway project, which resulted in two pieces of land being purchased near Pine and Emmett streets, a brand spanking new street was planned from Lake Avenue to East Road. The purpose of the new street was to relieve traffic congestion in the Middle Street section resulting from increased industrial activity.

1975

Before 786 fans on a Friday night at Muzzy Field, the Bristol Red Sox split a doubleheader with the Waterbury Dodgers. In Game One, the BriSox won 2-1 in 10 innings with Ed Ford's two-out home run.

Game Two went to Waterbury 2-0. Lanny Phillips led the way with two hits for the home team.
