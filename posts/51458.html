Bristol Central boys and girls cross country both split at 1-1 in action against Lewis Mills and Berlin

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51458 Published on 2024-09-25 17:35:09

The Bristol Central cross country teams ran against Lewis Mills and Berlin in Burlington on Tuesday.

The Central boys defeated Berlin by a score of 18-45, but lost to Lewis Mills, 22-34.

Central’s girls also went 1-1 on the day, defeating Lewis Mills 15-50, but losing to Berlin, 19-44.

Coach Tamara Stafford-Kirk stated, “I’m happy the teams are at .500 on the season. We continue to be led by sophomores on the boys and girls sides, which is promising for us as we rebuild.”

Stafford-Kirk pointed to Captains Nick DiMatteo and Braydan Dwyer as positive leaders who are doing a great job encouraging an overall young team to believe in themselves and put in the hard work. She also praised Sophomore Captain Emersyn Mazzei, who continues to step up in workouts and races to lead a young girls team as well.

Both teams are now 2-2 on the season.
