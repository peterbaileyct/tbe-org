Tuesday, May 30, forecast

Sunny, with patchy clouds in the afternoon. Highs in the mid 70’s. Northeast winds at around 5 m.p.h., becoming southeast in the afternoon.

What’s going on?

CIAC state tournament action begins today.

  • In baseball, Bristol Central will host Daniel Hand at Muzzy Field at 3:15 p.m.
  • In softball, Bristol Eastern visits Nonnewaug at 3:30 p.m. (To get to Nonnewaug, take a left at Polliwog.)
  • Bristol Central, ranked #2 in softball, has a bye and will have a home game tomorrow.
