Bristol Eastern boys cross country clinches CCC South championship; girls take 3rd place in conference

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52383 Published on 2024-10-16 07:55:43

Boys CCC Championships

The Bristol Eastern Boys finished an undefeated season with an outstanding showing in the CCC Championships at Wickham Park on Tuesday.

Jase Koczur took first place with a time of 17:12 and is the South Conference Champion. Brendan Tartarelli earned all-conference honors with a time of 17:12.

“The boys overcame every obstacle that came in their way and ran their hearts out. I couldn’t be prouder of this team,” said Coach Paul Ryskowski. “Jase and Brendan were backed with great performances from Kyle Kelley, Jonathan Casarola, and Cole Beland.”

CCC BOYS CROSS COUNTRY CHAMPIONSHIP

Bristol Eastern times:

1) Jase Koczur: 17:12

2) Brendan Tartarelli: 18:14

3) Kyle Kelley: 18:16

4) Jonathan Casarola: 18:49

5) Cole Beland: 19:00

6) Aidan Gezelman: 19:19

7) Christian Ferraro: 19:37

8) Szymon Winiarczyk: 19:38

Girls CCC Championships

The Bristol Eastern Girls finished 3rd in the CCC South.

Kaelyn Gonzalez finished as the Conference Champion Runner-Up and earned All-Conference honors with a time of 21:32!

Calliope Gallagher, Tesla Coello, Maryn Primus, Ace Vu, and Maiya Gibson all ran well too. Ryskowski stated, “This is a young team that showed a lot of promise this year - I am very proud of them!”

3) Tesly Coello: 26:17

4) Maryn Primus: 26:22

5) Ace Vu: 26:26

6) Maiya Gibson: 29:36
