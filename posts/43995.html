Bristol Eastern girls lacrosse opens season at 2-2, big cross-town battle with Central on Monday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43995 Published on 2024-04-14 16:26:49

The Bristol Eastern girls lacrosse team is 2-2 after four games with wins at Rocky Hill and home vs RHAM, and losses at Canton in the season opener and at home in the last minute to Watertown.

Freshman goalie Erin Martin took on the challenge after having never played the sport before. She had eight saves in the most recent win over RHAM.

The defense is led by seniors Jayna Hatcher, Meg Krok and Gabby McLay.

The offense has been paced by senior Vanessa Drury with eight goals, freshman Harper Dauphinee with six goals and senior Emma Stavens with 25 goals.

Junior Leah Roy has been critical on ground balls and winning control off the draw.

The girls travel to Bristol Central Monday night for the first cross-town match-up of the year. Game time is at 6 p.m.
