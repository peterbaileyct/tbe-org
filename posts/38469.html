Organizational meeting open to all willing to help plan Connecticut's 250th anniversary celebration

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=38469 Published on 2023-12-12 07:00:00

An organizational meeting to plan Bristol's celebration of the state's 250th anniversary will be held at the Bristol Historical Society on Thursday, Dec. 14 at 6:30 pm. All are invited and encouraged to attend to help plan events.

During the meeting, the following will discussed:

  • review what the CT 250 Commission is and what it has been up to
  • discuss who should be involved in the planning of events
  • brainstorm ideas for events
  • explore how to pay for events
  • set up a schedule for future meetings

Lee McFadden, vice regent of the Conn. Daughters of the Revolution and the Bristol Historical Society are organizing the meeting. For information, contact McFadden at lee@gomcf.com.
