Bristol Legion wins over Meriden, then loses to West Hartford on Wednesday and Thursday nights at Muzzy Field

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47478 Published on 2024-06-23 07:00:00

6/19/24 Meriden at Bristol

Bristol’s American Legion team had an easy time of it Wednesday night against Meriden, winning by a score of 10-1 at Muzzy Field.

Ryan Maglio paced the offense with a three hit night, including a double and two RBI’s. Noah Molinsky went two for two, and Rocco Spirito had two RBI’s to keep the attack rolling.

Gavin Colvin overpowered the Meriden line-up, allowing no hits while striking out nine in five innings on the mound. (Meriden’s box score was not available)

6/20/24 West Hartford at Bristol

On Thursday, West Hartford visited Muzzy and dealt Bristol a 6-2 setback.

Bristol struggled against West Hartford pitching again on Thursday, scratching out only two hits in the seven-inning affair.

Ryan Maglio pitched well in defeat, giving up only two earned runs, as five Bristol errors led to four unearned runs crossing the plate. Maglio limited West Hartford to six hits in his complete game loss and deserved a better fate.

Bristol currently sits in fourth place in Zone 1 with a record of 4-3. West Hartford occupies first place with a 4-0 mark.

The two teams will meet again on Monday at Muzzy in a 5:45 p.m. start.
