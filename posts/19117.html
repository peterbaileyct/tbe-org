Bristol Sports Hall of Fame selling tickets for November Induction Dinner

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=19117 Published on 2022-10-26 11:09:07

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame's annual Induction Ceremony will be held on Friday, Nov. 18, from 6 to 9 p.m. at the DoubleTree in Bristol. This years' inductees are:

  • Leon & Gussie Breen
  • Kim Corbin Varasconi
  • Mario Marrero
  • Tracy Pomerenke Regan
  • Clem Roy
  • Reinaldo Soto
  • Chris Strahowski
  • Peter Wininger

Tickets can be purchased by contacting Pat Parente at 860-919-8087 or on the Hall of Fame website at bshof.org.  No tickets will be sold at the door.
