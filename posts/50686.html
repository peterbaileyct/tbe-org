Dale E. Crowe, 80

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50686 Published on 2024-09-10 07:00:00

Dale E. Crowe, 80, of Bristol, passed away on Tuesday, Sept. 3. He was born on April 7,1944, in New Britain, son of the late Mr. and Mrs. Ernest Crowe.

Dale was the loving husband of the late Cheryl (Barrnett) Crowe. He served in the US Army and later retired from General Electric. Dale enjoyed golfing, watching baseball and loved his family and friends dearly.

Dale is survived by his sons Greg Crowe and his wife Beth Gallant of Plainville; Jeremy and his wife Elizabeth of Monroe; grandchildren Paige, Brendan and Lauren Crowe and great grandchildren Charlotte and Cooper.

Dale is also predeceased by eight brothers and sisters.

A burial will be held privately.

To leave an online message of condolence, please visit Dale’s memorial webpage at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
