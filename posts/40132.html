AAA and Majors Little League registration closes Jan. 27

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=40132 Published on 2024-01-13 16:00:00

Registration for Little League AAA and Little League Majors closes Jan. 27. Any child not signed up by then will be placed on a waitlist. Visit www.bristolctll.com to register today. On the page, select the "Baseball Programs" tab and on the pop-up menu, click on "online registration."

Both seasons run from April 6 to June 29. Majors is for players 10-12 years old and is a more competitive environment that follows the traditional rules and strategies, while AAA is for players 9-11 years old and focuses on player development.

Registration for each program is $150. Scholarships are available from the T-Mobile Little LeagueÂ® Call Up Grant Program dedicated to helping families in need by covering registration fees associated with their local Little League.
