Post 209 annual walk-a-thon supporting St. Vincent De Paul of Bristol and Prudence Crandall Center set Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52108 Published on 2024-10-10 07:00:00

A "Suicide & Domestic Awareness Walk-A-Thon" will be held this Saturday, Oct. 12, from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m. at Rockwell Park.

The event, sponsored by American Legion Post 209, Forestville, not only draws attention to these important issues but also fundraises for the St. Vincent De Paul Mission of Bristol and the Prudence Crandall Center.

Among the items being sought are hoodies, sweatshirts, socks, single serve snacks, paper plates, laundry soap, coffee, powered creamer and sugar.

The event provides a forum for information sharing and several speakers.

The event will be held rain or shine.
