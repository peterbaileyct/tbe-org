'World of Wonders: Taken Over!' runs through Sunday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43162 Published on 2024-03-28 12:00:00

By Drew a.k.a ZypherFusion

Hello Trainers! 

It’s Drew with some brand-new Pokémon Go news for you! 

The World of Wonders: Taken Over! event began Wednesday, March 27 at 12 a.m. and runs through Sunday, March 31, at 11:59 a.m.

Team Rocket has once again taken over another event, and it’s up to us to stop it. If you were not able to catch Groudon in the previous event, here is your chance to get the shadow version of this Pokémon.

Be sure to have these types available to take it down: grass, ice, and water Pokémon will do the trick with ease. Make sure you complete the missions this week to get your chance to get it!  

Shadow Mewtwo is coming this weekend! The legendary Pokémon has come back in its shadow form looking to escape the clutches of Team Rocket. This raid is special because you can only catch this Pokémon locally in a Team Rocket Raid. Mewtwo is weak to ghost, dark, and bug types.

My personal picks to beat Mewtwo this weekend would be Mega Gardevoir with the charged move Shadow Ball, Darkrai with the Charged move Dark Pulse, and Mega Tyranitar with the charged move Brutal Swing.

Make sure you have at least eight people minimum in your group to have a better chance of catching it. Here are the spawn locations for this event starting at 10:30 a.m. Saturday and ending Sunday at 5 p.m.: 

  • Federal Hill Green, 33 Queen St., Bristol 
  • Page Park, 651 King St., Bristol 
  • Rockell Park, 238 Jacobs St, Bristol 
  • Bushnell Park, Hartford 

This weekend is Easter, so happy Easter! Before I go, there is one more event happening next weekend. It’s Community Bagon Day! It starts April 8. Just like Litten Day, you can catch the dragon Pokémon Bagon -- and the shiny form too! More details about this on next time! 

Be sure to gear up for next week, where we prepare for the next World of Wonders event! For more information, visit www.pokemongolive.com. Until next time Trainers!
