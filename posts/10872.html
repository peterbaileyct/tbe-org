St. Paul girls dominate from the start in an easy 80-34 cruise over Crosby of Waterbury

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10872 Published on 2022-01-26 12:56:51

St. Paul dominated play in the first half Tuesday night and cruised to a 80-34 win over Crosby of Waterbury.

From the opening tap, the Falcons jumped all over their outmatched opponents, scoring 33 points in an overwhelming first stanza, while holding Crosby to 6. The destruction just continued in the 2nd period, and St. Paul led 53-15 at intermission.

Coach Joe Mone took the opportunity to give playing time to his entire roster, and every St. Paul player contributed, with 15 Falcons breaking into the scoring column.

Nadia Reimer and Izzy Leggett led the scoring parade with 20 and 17 points respectively.
Kelsea Tefoe was the Falcon’s leading rebounder with 9 boards, while Zoey Rubins dished out 7 assists.

St. Paul now jumps to 8-1 on the season. Crosby fell to 2-9.

The Falcons get right back to work tonight with a home game against Seymour at 7:00.
