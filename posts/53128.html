Manross Memorial Library to host veterans Nov. 9

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53128 Published on 2024-10-30 07:00:00

The F.N. Manross Memorial Library's 2024 Veterans Appreciation Social will be held on Saturday, Nov. 9, at 10 a.m. This program was originally hosted and organized by Bob Montgomery who writes for the TBE.

The program is free. Veterans and their guests are invited to attend, join in the fellowship and enjoy refreshments. Registration is recommended online at F. N. Manross Memorial Library - Bristol Public Library (bristollib.com), by calling (860)584-7790 or stopping in.
