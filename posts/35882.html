Annual interfaith Thanksgiving service to be held at St. Joseph Church on Federal Hill Nov. 22

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=35882 Published on 2023-10-20 07:00:00

This year's annual Bristol Interfaith Thanksgiving Service, with "Gratitude" for its theme, will be held at St. Joseph Church, Wednesday, Nov. 22, at 7:30 p.m.

The annual service is organized by the Bristol Interfaith Coalition, a multi-faith community of people from all spiritual paths who connect to one another by embracing similarity, while celebrating differences.

During the service a collection will be taken for the Prudence Crandall Center, which has for its mission reducing the impact of domestic violence. Services include providing an emergency shelter, 24/7 crisis hotline and more.
