Polls are open at BAIMS for today's special election in Council District 3

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48950 Published on 2024-07-30 06:00:00

Voters from City Council District 3 -- that is Federal Hill, Forestville and downtown -- will be casting their ballots in today's special election at Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School on Memorial Boulevard.

Polls open at 6 a.m. and close at 8 p.m.

Voters will have a choice between Democrat Mark Dickau and Republican Sal Mazzarella. For information about each, refer to the TBE stories at the following links listed below:
