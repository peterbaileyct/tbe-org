A Mum Festival Parade wends its way through a steady rain and still thrills -- more photos from the day

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51794 Published on 2024-10-02 07:00:00

Sunday's 62nd Mum Festival took place under cloudy skies and what started as a fine mist and turned to a steady rain--and the participants brought their best smiles and moves, from band members to dancers and pols.

Bristol photographer Ron Tessman was on hand chronicling the event. More photos from the parade can be found on his website, BristolCT.net.
