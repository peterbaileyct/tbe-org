Board of Public Works, Animal Control Building Committee among today's city meetings

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54108 Published on 2024-11-21 07:00:00

City Hall will host several city meetings of throughout the day from 8:30 a.m. through 7 p.m.

The Banking & Audit Committee will meet at 8:30 a.m. in Meeting Room 3-1 at City Hall, located a 111 North Main St.

The Commission on Aging will meet at 11 a.m. in Room 103 at the Bristol Senior Center, located at 240 Stafford Ave. 

Citation Hearings will be held at 3 p.m. at City Hall.

The Animal Control Building Committee will meet at 5 p.m. at City Hall 

The regular monthly meeting of the Purchasing Committee will meet at 5:15 p.m. at City Hall 

The Board of Public Works will meet at 6 p.m. in the City Council Chambers 

A special meeting of the Board of Fire Commissioners will be held t 6 p.m.at Headquarters Station, located at 181 North Main St.

The Energy Commission will meet at 7 p.m. in meeting room 116 at City Hall.
