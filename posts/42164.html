BBGC gala announcement ...

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42164 Published on 2024-03-06 20:41:27

On Saturday, April 20, 2024 the Boys & Girls Club of Bristol is hosting our Golden Ticket Gala to be held at the Aqua Turf Club in Plantsville, CT from 5-10pm. Guests will enjoy fine dining with dinner and dessert, an open bar, and live music. A silent and live auction featuring a wide array of exciting items will be held.

For information on sponsorship opportunities, please contact Keleigh Courtney at 860-540-3101 or KCourtney@bbgc.org.

Proceeds from the event will support the transformational youth programs that we provide to the thousands of children and families across our network of 13 sites in our community.

We will be hosting our third annual Black Tie Gala Event to be held at the Aqua Turf Club in Plantsville, CT from 5-10pm. Dedicated to our Imagine Nation, A Museum Early Learning Center division this event will be a truly awe inspiring delve into the world of Willy Wonka! Guests will enjoy fine dining with dinner and dessert, an open bar, and live music. A silent auction featuring a wide array of exciting items will be held and there might even be a golden ticket or two at each table! Tickets are available as Sponsorships, Tables, or Individual Tickets at www.BBGC.org/Gala or via phone at 860-540-3101. For further information please contact Keleigh Courtney at 860-540-3101 or KCourtney@BBGC.org. 

All TBE readers, supporters and donors                

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week. This decision is based on our financial projections and, most certainly, to remind people that TBE is serious about providing accurate, timely and thorough reporting for Bristol. To do this we have devised a financial support structure that makes unlimited access extremely affordable, beginning with a $6 monthly donation.                

  • Non-members will be able to access four (4) articles per week.                
  • Free readers and people who have subscribed by email will be able to access four (4) articles per week.                
  • Donors and financial supporters will have unlimited access as long as they log in.                

Note: Donors may have to contact TBE if they find they are being limited, since we will need to set up a membership account for you. Email editor@bristoledition.org for instructions. Sorry for any inconvenience. People with financial difficulties may write editor@bristoledition.org to be considered for free access.
