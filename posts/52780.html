Special city council meeting to consider purchase of property off Perkins Street abutting Hoppers-Birge Pond Preserve

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52780 Published on 2024-10-24 07:00:00

On the calendar for city meetings, today, Thursday, Oct. 24, is a special meeting of the city council, which will convene to executive session to discuss the purchase of property off Perkins Street until being considered for a 16-unit open space housing development.
