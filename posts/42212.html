Bristol police update on Wednesday's shooting on Union Street and East Road

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42212 Published on 2024-03-07 17:30:55

The Bristol Police Department issued an update on yesterday's shooting incident which reads as follows:

On Wednesday, March 6, members of the Bristol Police Department Patrol and Detective Divisions responded to a reported shooting in the area of East Road and Union Street.

Officers located a male who had sustained several gunshot wounds. The male was transported to an area hospital.


A red Infiniti G37 bearing Connecticut registration BK77317 was sought by police. The vehicle was located shortly afterward.

Anyone with information is encouraged to contact Detective Matthew Godbout at 860-584-3052.
