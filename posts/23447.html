Covid 19 booster shots to be offered at Wheeler Family Health and Wellness Center on Wednesdays in March

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=23447 Published on 2023-02-25 11:11:26

Wheeler Health issued the following announcement on Feb. 23:

COVID-19 booster shot clinics will be offered to the public at Wheeler's five Family Health & Wellness Centers on Wednesdays in March, as well as the Plainville Senior Center through
Wheeler's Mobile Family Health & Wellness Center, on March 20 and April 10. No appointment or referral is needed, and insurance cards and IDs are recommended.

The Wednesday clinics will be offered on March 1, March 8, March 15, March 22 and March 29 from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m.

·       Wheeler’s Bristol location is: Wheeler Family Health & Wellness Center, 10 North Main Street

Medicaid, Medicare, and commercial health insurance are welcome. No patient will be denied health care services due to an individual's inability to pay for services.
