St. Paul cruises to a 51-33 victory over Naugatuck in girls basketball Tuesday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21741 Published on 2023-01-11 14:01:47

The Naugatuck girls did not pose much of a problem against St. Paul Tuesday, as the Falcons led from the start and coasted to a 51-33 win.

Rebecca Kelly and Audrey Tice led the scoring parade for St. Paul with 15 and 11 points apiece. Tice added 10 nifty assists to her stat line.

Kelsea Tefoe led the Falcons on the boards with 8 rebounds.

Naugatuck was led by Aryn Bomberry with 13 points. No other Greyhound hit double figures.

St. Paul improves to 5-3 on the season and plays at Torrington Friday night at 7:00.
