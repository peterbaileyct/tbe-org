Robert J. Avolt, 80

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=38161 Published on 2023-12-06 07:30:00

Robert J. Avolt, 80, beloved husband of Nancy (Knibbs) Avolt, died on Saturday, Dec. 2, at the Hospital of Central Connecticut, New Britain. Bob was born July 30, 1943, in Bristol and was the youngest of four children of the late John Avolt, Jr., and Evelyn (Semrow) Avolt.

He graduated from Bristol Eastern High School then served in the United States Army Reserves. He worked primarily in construction, laying underground piping. He also owned and operated the former B & L Oil. 

Bob had a love of farming and tended his farm for many years on Hart Street where he also kept his Belgian Horses. He enjoyed the years of camping with family and friends and especially adored his grandchildren and great-grandchildren. 

In addition to his wife of 60 years, Bob is survived by three children: Lori Marquis of Bristol, Jeffrey Avolt and wife, Tricia of Terryville, and Kathy Schilling and husband, Ken of Rocky Hill; eight grandchildren: Brian Schilling and wife, Angelia, Eric Schilling, Daniel Marquis and wife, Kalee, Kyle Marquis and wife, Jessica, Robert Robertson, Hannah Avolt and twins, Levi and Erin Avolt; four great-grandchildren: Scarlett, Mason, Jack, and Luke; and nieces and nephews. 

Calling hours will be held on Thursdayds, Dec. 7, between 10 a.m. and 12 noon at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol, followed by words of remembrance at noon. Burial, with military honors, will follow in West Cemetery, Bristol.  

In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the American Heart Association, PO Box 840692, Dallas, TX 75284-0692 or to St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, 501 St. Jude Pl., Memphis, TN, 38105.

Please visit Bob’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
