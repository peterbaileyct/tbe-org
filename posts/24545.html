Hilda Jane Fiorillo, 85

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24545 Published on 2023-04-04 07:00:00

Hilda Jane Fiorillo, 85, widow of Harry J. Fiorillo, of Bristol, passed away on Friday, March 31.

She was born on Nov. 13, 1937, in Springfield, Tenn., the daughter of the late Onie and Pearl Dorris.

Hilda is survived by her children and their spouses, Carol (Maury) McDermott, John Fiorillo, Janet (Joseph) McGrath, Jeffery (Pam) Fiorillo and Robert Fiorillo; her grandchildren Charles, Casey, John Joseph, Sarah, Kellie, Krista, Kimberly, Jordan, Jennifer, Michael and Jon Matthew; her great-grandchildren Owenn, Griffin, Sophia, Charlotte, Dylann, Isla, Sayer, Lachlan, Bristyl, Austin, Elizabeth, Lincoln, Charlotte and Grace; and several nieces and nephews.

In addition to her husband, she was predeceased by her brothers and sisters.

Calling hours will be held on Tuesday, April 4, from 5 p.m. until 7 p.m. at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave., Bristol. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Wednesday, April 5, at 10 a.m., directly at the Church of St. Anthony, 111 School St., Bristol followed by a procession to St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.

To leave an online message of condolence, share a memory or a photo, please visit Hilda’s tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
