Frances Teti, 77

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21395 Published on 2023-01-01 15:00:00

Frances (Manocchio) Teti, 77, of Bristol, widow of John Teti, died on Friday, Dec. 30, at Apple Rehab in Plainville. Frances was born in Bristol on June 20, 1945, and was a daughter of the late Domenic and Frances (Nestico) Manocchio.

A lifelong Bristol resident, she worked as a bookkeeper and was a parishioner of St. Gregory the Great Church, Bristol.  She enjoyed working in her garden tending to her flowers. She also enjoyed jigsaw and crossword puzzles and especially cooking; known for her cheesecake, cookies, and her baking.

Frances is survived by two daughters: Frances R. Teti of Tiverton, R.I., and Gina Teti of Bristol; three sisters: Anne Tonon, Kathleen Baldaccini and husband, Daniel, and Theresa Zukowski and husband, James; a granddaughter: Sophia Velles; and several nieces, nephews, great-nieces, and great-nephews. 

Family and friends are invited to gather on Wednesday, Jan. 4, between 9:30 and 10:30 a.m. at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Avenue, Bristol.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 11 a.m. at St. Gregory the Great Church, 235 Maltby St., Bristol. Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol. 

In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 3 Landmark Square, Stamford, CT 06901.

Please visit Frances’ memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
