Bristol Central Track Team members travel to Oregon to compete in the Nike Outdoor Nationals

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47371 Published on 2024-06-19 12:22:01

Eighteen student-athletes from Bristol Central went out to Eugene, Ore., last weekend to compete in the Nike Outdoor Nationals. They had a great experience and enjoyed every minute of the competition.

Coach Tamara Stafford-Kirk had the following comment about the weekend experience- "It was an amazing trip and opportunity for our program. We'd like to thank the BC and Bristol community for supporting the kids and their dreams."
