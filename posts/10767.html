O'Neil Bellefleur

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10767 Published on 2022-01-21 08:06:49

O’Neil Bellefleur, 56, lifelong resident of Bristol, died on Tuesday, Jan. 18, at home. O’Neil was the beloved son of Fernande (Plourde) Bellefleur of Bristol and the late Alfred J. Bellefleur.

He graduated from Bristol Central High School and worked for Roberge painting for 25 years.  He enjoyed exercise and walking his English Bulldogs, Stella and Louie. In addition to his mother, O’Neil leaves his two sisters, Suzanne Howard and husband, James, and Michelle Desmarais and husband, Robert Desmarais: and five nephews: Austin and Dillon Howard, Robert, Jake, and Daniel Desmarais. He was predeceased by a brother: Alan Bellefleur. 

Relatives and friends are invited to a visitation at Funk Funeral Home 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol, on Monday, Jan. 24, between 6 and 8 p.m closing with a prayer service at 8 p.m. Burial will be at the convenience of the family. 

Please visit O’Neil’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com
