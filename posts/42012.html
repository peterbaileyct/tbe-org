'The Everything Expo' to return for second year to DoubleTree on March 21, vendor space almost full

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42012 Published on 2024-03-04 07:00:00

Limited space for vendors is still available for the 2nd annual "The Everything Expo," combining the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce's Health Fair, Business Expo and Career Fair, to be held for the second year at the DoubleTree by Hilton on Thursday, March 21, from 2 to 7 p.m.

“This will be a fun day, with something for everyone that we know will be beneficial to both the vendors and the public,” chamber president and CEO D’Agostino said in the announcement from the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce. “There was a good turnout throughout the day last year, and we’re hopeful for an even better showing this year.”

Among the almost 75 vendors incuded are BristolWORKS!, Advanced Window Systems, Conquest, The Palace Theater, Prudence Crandall Center, Ahlgren Landscaping, Pinnacle Dental, Keller Williams, Barron Financial Group, Casanova Remodeling, Hartford HealthCare, GoNetspeed, Capture the Concept, Bristol Health, Energy Resources USA, AECOM and Fox 61.

“For the public, to be able to gather information on really everything you’re looking for now in one day, we think is a total win,” said chamber vice president Johnny Burnham in the same announcement.

The event is free for the public. Attendees will have a chance to talk with professionals from a number of industries, grab some free swag and win some great raffle prizes from vendors, according to the announcement.

Vendors may visit centralctchambers.org for information. The cost is $250 per booth and includes lunch provided by the DoubleTree.

For more information on the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce, visit www.CentralCTChambers.org or call (860) 584-4718.
