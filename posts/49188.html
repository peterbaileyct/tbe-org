Bristol Auto Club 'Cruise Night' today at 10 Main St.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49188 Published on 2024-08-03 07:00:00

This month's first Saturday 2024 Cruise Night presented by the Bristol Auto Club returns to 10 Main St. this afternoon, from 3 to 7 p.m.

The Cruise Nights are free and feature Oldies Music, food from 10 Main Cafe and Deli, ample parking and raffles, including a 50/50. The events are held the first and third Saturdays of each month through Oct. 5.

There is no cut off for vehicles, however, the Bristol Auto Club reserves the right to refuse any vehicle. Each night there are three awards for vehicles on site.

The shows are alcohol and pet free. No bicycles are allowed and burnouts are prohibited. For information, click here.
