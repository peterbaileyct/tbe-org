Earth Day Walk and Celebrate today at 12:30

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44382 Published on 2024-04-21 08:00:00

An Earth Day celebration and walk will be held today, Sunday, April 21, beginning at 12:30 p.m. at the "Hill" in the old section of the Hillside Cemetery in Thomaston.

The walk will include viewing sacred ceremonial landscapes with Night Hawk Flying and will commence after 10 a.m. services at Good Shepherd Episcopal Church on 851 Stafford Ave.

Parking is limited at the walk site, so the organizers suggest carpooling. In addition, the walk is uphill and the ground may be muddy. The path is not paved but it is not rugged.

More information is available on Facebook at Good Shepherd Episcopal Church, Bristol/Plainville site or contact Father Link at linkhullar@gmail.com.
