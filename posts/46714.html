Bristol Eastern seniors sign letters of intent to play for New England colleges

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46714 Published on 2024-06-07 13:02:11

Tuesday afternoon saw Bristol Eastern senior athletes sign letters of intent to athletic programs across New England.

Lauren Ayotte

Lauren Ayotte will attend Western New England University and compete in Track and Field.

Ryan Braccia

Ryan Braccia will attend Southern Connecticut State University and compete in Track and Field.

Isaiah Lawrence-Bynum

Isaiah Lawrence-Bynum will attend Southern Connecticut State University and compete in Track and Field.

Ben D’Amato

Ben D’Amato will attend Eastern Connecticut State University and compete in Baseball.

Caleb Molinsky

Caleb Molinsky will attend Western Connecticut State University and compete in Baseball.

Adrianna Reay

Adrianna Reay will attend Franklin Pierce University and compete in Volleyball

Lukas Sward

Sward will attend Vermont State University- Johnson and compete in Basketball.
