Bristol Central girls tennis defeats Bristol Eastern 6-1 in a city series bout on Friday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13458 Published on 2022-05-22 15:56:04

By Michael Letendre

BRISTOL - The Bristol Central and Bristol Eastern girls tennis teams held their annual city series battle on Friday afternoon.

And while the Rams tallied up a 6-1 victory over the Lancers from the tennis courts at Bristol Central High School, Eastern showed grit throughout competition.

Eastern’s top singles player, Brooke Treannie, was able to snare a win in her match but Central swept through the rest of line-up.

And then the home team rolled through doubles play with a 3-0 win.

But Treannie was able to top Alex Rivera by scores of 7-5 and 6-2. 

At No. 2 singles, Central’s Sam Chapman defeated Adrianna Reay (6-3, 6-3) and at No. 3, the Rams Ava McCann swept Vanessa Cyr 6-0 over set one.

In set two, Cyr pushed McCann to the brink but the athlete from Central held on to win, 7-6.

And in the fourth singles match, Central’s Emely Sosa Requena and Eastern’s Trish Mohan went the distance.

Mohan roped up the first set, 6-2, but Sosa Requena bounced back in set two for a 6-3 victory - forcing an overtime situation as the match was all tied up.

And Sosa Requena rolled out a 10-4 third set victory to snare the win.

In doubles play, Central’s top duo of Alexa and Brooke Rinaldi roped up wins of 6-2 and 6-0 against Eastern’s Olivia Dossias and Ava Gesner.

The number two doubles combo of Liz Martin and Hannah Krueger spun Eastern’s Maria Perrone and Kiana Ouellette (6-2, 6-2) and at No. 3, the Rams saw the team of Sydney LaRosa and Angella Avila defeat the Lancers Sam Rivard and Taylor Berube (6-1, 6-1).

Central moved to 9-4 over the winning effort while the Lancers fell to 1-12.

The Rams complete their regular season on Tuesday with a home match against Berlin (3:45 p.m.) while on Wednesday, the Lancers travel to Wickham Park to battle East Catholic - also at 3:45 p.m.

BRISTOL CITY SERIES GIRLS TENNIS – CCC South action 

BRISTOL CENTRAL 6, BRISTOL EASTERN 1

from the tennis courts at Bristol Central High School 

Singles 

  1. Brooke Treannie (Bristol Eastern) defeated Alex Rivera: 7-5, 6-2 
  2. Sam Chapman (Bristol Central) defeated Adrianna Reay: 6-3, 6-3 
  3. Ava McCann (Bristol Central) defeated Vanessa Cyr: 6-0, 7-6 
  4. Emely Sosa Requena (Bristol Central) defeated Trish Mohan: 2-6, 6-3, 10-4

Doubles

  1. Alexa Rinaldi/Brooke Rinaldi (Bristol Central) defeated Olivia Dossias/Ava Gesner: 6-2, 6-0
  2. Liz Martin/Hannah Krueger (Bristol Central) defeated Maria Perrone/Kiana Ouellette: 6-2, 6-2 
  3. Sydney LaRosa/Angella Avila (Bristol Central) defeated Sam Rivard/Taylor Berube: 6-1, 6-1

Records: Bristol Central 9-4 overall; Bristol Eastern 1-12
