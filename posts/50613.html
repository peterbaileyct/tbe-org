Silver alert issued for missing juvenile

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50613 Published on 2024-09-08 09:35:00

A Silver Alert has been issued for 14-year-old, Kenneth Rivera, Jr. the Bristol Police Dept. announced in a release earlier.

The Bristol Police Department is seeking the public’s help in locating the missing juvenile.

Kenneth Rivera Jr. is missing. | BPD

Rivera left his house at approximately 7 p.m. on Friday, Sept. 6. Rivera is described as a Hispanic male, 5 feet, 5 inches tall, weighting 110 pounds, with brown eyes and brown hair, and a light complexion.

His exact clothing description is unknown, although he is likely dressed in all black clothing.

Anyone having any information on Kenneth Rivera Jr's whereabouts is asked to please call the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.
