It could get a little slippery this morning. Snow is likely, about 2-4 inches with a chance of some freezing rain in the afternoon. The high will be about 29 with winds 5-10 m.p.h.

With a forecast like that, watch for the no-school announcements, and if we get 4 inches of snow (the modern definition of blizzard), schools may be closed for the rest of the week.

This day in history

The final episode of Bonanza aired on NBC in 1973. Bonanza was rated the 43rd greatest TV series by "T.V. Guide Magazine." Many observers of The Bristol Edition have mentioned that TBE’s Dave Fortier reminds them of Ben Cartwright, while others have noticed the uncanny resemblance of Ben’s son Hoss and TBE’s own Rit Carter.

On this day in 1899, the infamous gangster Al Capone was born. Capone was actually mentioned just yesterday by former President Donald Trump. Trump “bragged” that he was indicted multiple times, but Capone only had one indictment. What a slacker!

Basketball showdown

Bristol Central and Bristol Eastern will tangle in girls’ basketball tonight at the Rams’ gym at 6:45 p.m. Both teams are having great seasons. Be there or be square.
