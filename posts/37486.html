Police seeking assistance locating missing youth

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=37486 Published on 2023-11-19 09:35:00

The Bristol Police Department is seeking the public’s help in locating a missing juvenile, 14-year-old Chase Sherman. Chase is described as a white male, with brown hair and brown eyes, fair complexion, 5 ft. 3 in. tall and approx. 165 lbs. He was last seen wearing a maroon t-shirt, Bristol Eastern sweatshirt hoodie, black sweatpants and red/black Nike sneakers.

Chase was last seen at approx. 4:30 p.m. on Nov. 18, when he left his house on foot. A Silver Alert has been issued for Chase.

Anyone having any information on Chase’s whereabouts are asked to please call the Bristol Police Department at 860-584-3011.
