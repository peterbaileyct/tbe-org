Bristol Eastern defeats Maloney in girls volleyball, runs record to 7-1

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17723 Published on 2022-09-29 12:29:31

Bristol Eastern swept Maloney in straight sets Wednesday night, winning sets one and two easily by scores of 25-13 and 25-12. Maloney put up more of a fight in set three, but the Lancers still prevailed by a score of 25-20. A large crowd went home happy as Eastern ran their record to 7-1.

Emily Valentine played a strong floor game for the Lancers, serving up 5 aces, and adding 8 digs. Alyssa Valentine had 15 assists, and Andrea Martir-Perez chipped in with 9 assists on the night.

Peyton Pulaski led Maloney with 5 kills.

Eastern is now 7-1, while Maloney drops to 3-4.

The Lancers will travel to Newington Friday night for a 5:45 encounter.
