Board of Education hires new athletic director

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=23725 Published on 2023-03-05 07:00:00

The Bristol Board of Education has hired a new director of athletics. Cera Orner begins in her new position on March 27.

Orner fills the position that has been vacant since the former director of athletics Chris Cassin retired. The announcement was made on Wednesday at the board's monthly meeting.

Bristol Superintendent of Schools Catherine Carbone provided Orner's bio at the meeting. It includes the following:

Orner earned a bachelor's degree in English and literature, a master's degree in secondary education and teaching, and a sixth-year certificate in educational leadership all from Quinnipiac University.

Orner was a member of Quinnipiac's Division I acrobatics and tumbling team for four years and was a senior captain of the NCAA National Runner-Up team. She has taught English for the past eight years at North Branford High School, where she was head coach of North Branford's girls volleyball team.

Previously, she coached Sacred Heart Academy's competitive cheerleading team, which placed in the top 10 in state competition. Orner is a certified personal trainer as well as a certified nutrition coach.

Editor's note: Orner's start date is March 27, which was incorrectly stated in the original.
