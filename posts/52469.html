Voter backs Rydingsward in 77th

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52469 Published on 2024-10-18 07:00:00

To the editor:

The 77th district needs a positive change. Our current State Rep has been in office for 10 years and has a scant to zero record of aiding or improving our needs. She collected blankets and towels for an animal rescue facility in Waterbury but has not done much to make my life here in Bristol any better. It's time for a change. 

Mary "Mim" Rydingsward has lived in the district for over 35 years, has worked to protect our Kern Park wooded wetlands for over 18 years, has lobbied legislature on behalf of clean air and water, served two terms as Registrar of Voters and eight years as President of the Pequabuck River Watershed Association.  

As a democracy champion, freedom fighter, healthcare protector, and environmental steward, she will safeguard elections and voting rights as well as offer sustainable, creative solutions to improvements that are needed. No matter what your affiliation, she will work for you.  

Say NO to hatred and vote Mary "Mim" Rydingsward for State Representative! Go to fighting4freedom.net and click on the picture to learn more about Mim. Be the change. Vote Nov 5th line 5C or 5D.

Patty Claffey

Bristol
