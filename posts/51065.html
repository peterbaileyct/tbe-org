Bristol Eastern sweeps three straight sets in volleyball from Manchester Monday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51065 Published on 2024-09-17 16:31:43

MANCHESTER -- Bristol Eastern played some close sets in their road game with Manchester Monday night, but they were able to win the big points when crunch time arrived and swept to victory by scores of 25-22, 25-17 and 25-23.

The Lancers had big performances from Harper Dauphinee, who had 12 kills and 14 digs; Naseem Hennouch, who led BE with 23 assists and added 6 aces, 2 kills and 6 digs; and Kamila Mateo Rivera who contributed 15 digs and 6 kills.

Bristol Eastern 3 Manchester 0

Game scores: BE-M 25-22, 25-17, 25-23

Records: Manchester, 2-4; BE, 3-3
