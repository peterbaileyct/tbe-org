Sports trivia question

On this date in 1972, the Cincinnati Reds defeated the Oakland A’s 1-0 in game 3 of the 1972 World Series.

The Reds had four hits, while the A’s managed three. Each team committed two errors and combined for 20 Ks.

The game took two hours and 24 minutes, it was played on natural grass and there were 49,410 spectators in attendance. Name them.
