Salvation Army to hold benefit concert next week at Chippens Hill Middle School

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=26649 Published on 2023-05-24 11:04:09

The CAM Academy of the Bristol Salvation Army will be holding a benefit concert at Chippens Hill Middle School next Wednesday, May 31. The concert will include vocal and instrumental pieces along with dance and drama scenes.

Adult tickets are $10 each. Anyone under eighteen can attend for no charge.

For more information or to order tickets in advance, contact:
