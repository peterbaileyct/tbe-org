Attention night watchers

All this month after sunset, look for Jupiter above the eastern horizon.

Also, in the wee hours of the AM, when people who engage in problematic behavior are scrambling home following an evening of lurid improprieties, an hour or two before the sun dusts itself off, look for Mars. 

Speaking of the movies

The box office winner last week was Barbarian with $10.543,948. Distributed by 20th Century Fox, it played in 2,340 theaters. What will the box officer winner be this weekend?

The Weekend

Saturday morning, who will ring the opening bell at the Farmers Market? Last week it was Tom Dickau.

Bristol Trivia Answer

1951 was the first year of the Page Park Fishing Derby. Held at the Page Park Pond/Lagoon, the derby is named after Perry J. Spinelli, a longtime employee of the Parks Department.

That’s going to do it for Good Morning, Bristol. Everyone have a great day and a grand weekend. Be good to one another, and we will meet here on Monday.

All TBE readers, supporters and donors        

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week. This decision is based on our financial projections and, most certainly, to remind people that TBE is serious about providing accurate, timely and thorough reporting for Bristol.   • Donors and financial supporters will have unlimited access as long as they log in.
