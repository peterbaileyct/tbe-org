Fatal accident on Jerome Ave. and Fox Den Rd.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10749 Published on 2022-01-20 13:49:56

The Bristol Police Department has reported the following concerning a fatal accident Wednesday evening on Jerome Ave. and Fox Den Rd.:

On Wednesday, 1/19/22, at around 7:45 P.M., members of the Bristol Police Department responded to Jerome Avenue and Fox Den Road to investigate a car accident where a vehicle struck a pedestrian.   

The female pedestrian was pronounced dead shortly after being transported to the hospital.  

The Bristol Police Department’s Serious Traffic Accident Reconstruction Team was called to the scene to investigate.  

The Bristol Police Department is currently seeking witnesses that may have been driving in the area of Jerome Avenue and Fox Den Road in Bristol on 1/19/22, around 7:45 P.M. and saw this accident occur.  

Anyone with information should contact Officer Verillo at 860-584-3031.
