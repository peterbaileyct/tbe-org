Bristol Eastern boys track and field scores win over Platt Tuesday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45162 Published on 2024-05-08 07:00:00

The Bristol Eastern boys track team won their fourth dual meet of the season in a 93-59 verdict over Platt on Tuesday. Next week the Lancers host their cross-town rivals from Bristol Central on Tuesday.

Bristol Eastern: 93

Platt: 59

Below are the Lancer 1st Place winners:

Jase Koczur: 1st place in the 1600 (5:06) and in the 4x800

Hunter Scott: 1st place in the 400 (53.25) and in the 4x400

Bryce Bacote: 1st place in the 300 Hurdles (47.86) and in Long Jump (20-01)

Cole Beland: 1st place in the 800m (2:07), in the 4x800, and in the 4x400

Kyle Kelley: 1st place in the 3200 (11:26) and in the 4x800

Lincoln Bashaw: 1st place in Shot Put (39-07) and in Discus (112-03)

Ethan Cockayne: 1st place in Pole Vault (10-06)

4x400 (Cole Beland, Hunter Scott, Kobe Senethong, Benjamin Kozikowski): 1st place (3:44)

4x800 (Jase Kozcur, Cole Beland, Kyle Kelley, Jonathan Casarola): 1st place (9:40)

Bristol Eastern boys move to (4-2) on the season and are back in action next week Tuesday at home against Bristol Central!
