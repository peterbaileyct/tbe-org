Moped rider injured seriously in accident on Riverside Avenue Saturday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46893 Published on 2024-06-09 07:00:00

A male injured Sunday afternoon in moped accident on Riverside Avenue was treated at a nearby hospital and then transferred by LifeStar for possible life-threatening injuries, undergoing surgery, police reported earlier this morning.

On Sunday, June 8 at around 4:30 p.m., officers discovered a moped with a seriously injured male operator and notified the Bristol Communications Division of the crash. Officers performed medical aid until medical could be taken over by EMS, the report states.

The operator was transported an area hospital and then transferred to a trauma unit hospital by Lifestar for possible life-threatening injuries and underwent surgery.

The initial investigation showed that the male operator was eastbound on Riverside Avenue and appeared to fail to negotiate a curve in the roadway, laying the moped down where it would come to a rest on the male operator near a curb line on Riverside Avenue by East Street.

The serious traffic accident reconstruction team was later contacted, who also responded to the scene to investigate. This crash remains under investigation.

The name of the operator is not being released at this time, pending next of kin being notified.

Officers conducted a canvas of the area but could not locate any witnesses. If you witnessed this crash or have any further information, place contact Officer Boisvert at (860)584-3036 or at spencerboisvert@bristolct.gov.
