Visit Connecticut libraries in April as part of Passport to Connecticut Libraries 2024 and get entered in drawing

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43661 Published on 2024-04-08 07:00:00

Bristol Public Library and Manross Memorial Library will be participating in Passport to Connecticut Libraries 2024, which offers four individuals the chance to win $200 Visa gift cards from a random drawing of all program participants.

If a person, adult or child, visits at least five (5) participating libraries and returns their Passport to a participating library by May 10, they will be entered into a state-wide drawing for a chance to win a $200 Visa gift card for adults (18 and up) and a $200 Visa gift card for children (under 18).

This is a state-wide contest where four winners will be selected: one adult and one child from a random drawing from all returned Passports with at least five separate library stamps, and the one adult and one child who visited the most libraries/received the most stamps.

The program began April 1 and continues through April 30. Not all Connecticut libraries are participating libraries.

To begin in Bristol, visit either the Bristol Public Library or Manross memorial for a passport. The librarian will stamp passport (and possibly add a token gift). Then, take a passport along to other participating libraries where, after producing a library card, the librarian will stamp the passport (and possibly hand over another small gift).

For more details and a full list of participating libraries are available at the library.
