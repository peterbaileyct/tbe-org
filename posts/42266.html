Award-winning author to speak at Bristol Public Library during school break this April

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42266 Published on 2024-03-09 07:00:00

Newbery honor-winning author Rajani LaRocca will speak at the Bristol Public Library during spring break. The event is free, lunch will be provided and a Q&A session and book signing will follow.

LaRocca will talk about the inspiration behind her verse novels, "Red,White, and Whole" and "Mirror to Mirror," at 2 p.m. on Thursday, April 11. Doors will open at 1 p.m. and lunch will be served at 1:30 p.m. The event not only takes place during school break, but also National Library Week.

This event is recommended for ages 9 and older. Registration is required. All children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Families are welcome. To register, call (860)584-7787, ext. 6. Seats are limited.
