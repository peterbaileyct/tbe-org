Rockwell Festival now accepting vendor applications for this year's event

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=40541 Published on 2024-01-23 07:00:00

The West End Association is now accepting vendor applications for the 2024 Rockwell Festival, Vice President Jesse Jablon announced yesterday in an email.

"We had a record turnout in 2023 with beautiful weather and we hope you want to join us again," Jablon writes. The Rockwell Park Summer Festival for 2024 will be held Aug. 17.

At the same, he announced that for the first time in the 12-year history of the festival, the association is raising the vendor fee by $10. Each vendor space is now $50 for a 10 by 10 area. Food vendor spaces have a different charge depending on space size.

The festival features live music on two stages, arts & crafts, displays, games, food and a Kids Zone.

For a vendor application or any other information, contact Jesse Jablon by phone at 860-620-2771 or via westendbristol@gmail.com.
