Bristol Central runners continue to improve with strong showing at Wickham Invitationals

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51652 Published on 2024-09-30 08:40:27

The Bristol Central Cross Country teams competed in the Wickham Invitationals in Manchester on Saturday, and they continued to show growth and improvement as they have all season.

Coach Tamara Stafford stated, “It was great to be back at the Wickham Invite after it was canceled due to weather last year. Our boys team is definitely starting to step up. Sophomores Naythan and Noah Pellerin continue to come up big for us at the front and our pack of 3-6/7 is looking stronger each week.”

The Girls team is extremely young, so only one of the Ram girls ran in the varsity race. Stafford commented, “Our girls team is young and working hard and we’ve kept all but one in the JV races at invitationals. The goal is to move some of them up to the varsity race at the CCC Championship meet.”
