Reward being offered for information about vandalization of Quinlan Park memorial

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=38199 Published on 2023-12-06 15:30:00

A reward is being offered for anyone with information that leads to an arrest and prosecution of the party responsible for stealing the eagle that was part of the Quinlan Memorial Park Bell and Eagle monument.

Left, what remains of the eagle that once perched above the bell on the monument honoring veterans at Quinlan Memorial Park in Forestville. | Brian Avery
