St. Paul girls cruise to victory Friday over Kennedy

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9927 Published on 2021-12-19 21:41:38

The St. Paul girls jumped out with a 22 point first quarter and were never really threatened as they coasted to a 73-47 victory Friday night over Kennedy of Waterbury.

Nadia Reimer and Haileigh Orzel led the Falcons with 17 and 16 points respectively. Zoey Rubins and Emily Ronalter chipped in with 7 points apiece, and Abbey Stanford added 6.

Reimer added 6 assists to her impressive performance, and Zoe Rubins and Izzy Leggett each had 5 assists on a night that featured crisp ball movement throughout.

Alexis Tierpack led St. Paul in rebounds with 8.

Kennedy was led by Niyah Brown with 20 points and Meryoh Caraballo added 15.
