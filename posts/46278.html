Bristol Farmers Market announces opening date for 2024 season

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46278 Published on 2024-05-28 13:00:00

The Bristol Farmers Market will hold its grand opening Saturday, June 22 at 10 a.m., rain or shine, said a release from the new board under which the farmers market has formed.

The board is led by chair Dawn Nielsen, secretary Lindsey Rivers, treasurer Cheryl Thibeault with members including the following: vendor liaison Lorry LeMaire of Twin Pine Farm, business liaison Carolyn Verikas of the Bristol Bazaar, nonprofit liaison John Cassidy of the Bristol Rotary Club and community liaison Mickey Goldwasser.

Aside from operating under the new nonprofit board, the farmers market also will be at its new location, 10 Main St. The farmers market will meet Saturdays, June 22 through Nov. 2, from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m., rain or shine.

The board is seeking volunteers and sponsorships.

Community volunteers assist with setup, staffing the welcome tent and clean up at the end of market day. Anyone interested in volunteering should contact Goldwasser via email at BristolCTFarmersMarket@gmail.com.

Among this year's sponsors are D'Amato Construction, Barnes Group Foundation and Main Street Community Foundation. Sponsorships are still available. For a sponsorship packet, email BristolCTFarmersMarket@gmail.com.

The farmers market also accepts SNAP/EBT, WIC and Senior Farmers Market Nutrition vouchers. SNAP/EBT funding has doubled this year with financial support from End Hunger Connecticut and the Thomaston Savings Bank Foundation grants.

The Bristol Farmers Market also has a presence on social media, where the latest developments, vendors and musical entertainment is available. The Facebook and Instagram address is @bristolctfarmersmarket.
