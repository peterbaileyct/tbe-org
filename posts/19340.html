Bristol Bulldog cheerleaders head off for competitions

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=19340 Published on 2022-11-03 07:00:00

In 2021, the Bristol Bulldogs cheerleaders D8 team advanced to the AYC National Championship in Florida, in which they placed 6th out of 19 teams. They are hoping to have the same type of success this year.

This year, the D8 & D12 cheer teams kicked off their competition season with the local competition at Shelton High School. The younger D8 lady Bulldogs jump-started the competition with the first performance for Bristol, and the older girls (D12) came on later in the afternoon to finish out the day with another strong performance. Both teams finished with a 2nd place award! 

The Lady Bulldogs are looking forward to another successful season. Their next competition will be states on Nov. 6.
