The NECBL All-Star Game is upon us...

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=15276 Published on 2022-07-24 16:00:00

By Michael Letendre

The New England Collegiate Baseball League All Star Game is upon us.

And expect a tremendous contest between two very competitive squads.

The Martha’s Vineyard Sharks host the 2022 NECBL All-Star Game on Sunday from The Shark Tank. 

According to a release from the league "Players participating will be able to make their mark in front of scouts from every Major League Baseball (MLB) organization."

The 2022 NECBL All-Star festivities began at 2 p.m. with the Home Run Derby. 

The All-Star Game was slated to start at 4:30 p.m.

Here is the Southern Division Roster -- set from July 17 saw four players from the Blues picked for the squad.
