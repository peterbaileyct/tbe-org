Chorale's Sunday performance will be last one for Theriault before he retires

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45010 Published on 2024-05-04 14:01:29

Director Rich Theriault will be leading the Bristol Chorale for the last time as he will be retiring after Sunday's performance of "Americana ... Jazz & Broadway at the Rockwell Theater on Memorial Boulevard at 3 p.m.

Cost for adults is $20; for children 10 and under, $10. Tickets are available at Michael’s Jewelers or on the website, by clicking here.

The Bristol Chorale is a nonauditioned, volunteer community singing group comprising men and women from the Greater Bristol area. We are a 501(c)3 not-for-profit organization that presents two major performances a year, generally in May and December.
