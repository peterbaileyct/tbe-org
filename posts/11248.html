Bristol Co-op defeats Bulkeley Co-op 90-70 Wednesday afternoon; runs record to 7-0

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11248 Published on 2022-02-10 14:22:47

Bristol had 12 best times and 3 additional State LL qualifiers.

The Bristol Co-op swim team ran their record to 7 - 0 with a win over the Bulkeley Co-op, 90 - 70, on Wednesday from the Dennis Malone Aquatic Center in Bristol.

Nathan Stellmach qualified for States LL finishing in first place, with a best time in the 200 free. 

Mikel Palaj qualified for States LL, finishing in first place, with a best score in diving.

Aidan Collins qualified for States LL finishing in first place with a best time in the 100 back.

Davis Kroll had a best time in the 200 free, finishing 2nd and in the100 fly, finishing 1st. 

Aidan Colaianni had a best time in the 200 IM. 

Konrad Truszkowski had best times in the 50 free and the 500 free. 

Jedric Sutula had a best time in the 50 free. 

Luke Losacano had a best time in the 100 fly. 

Julian Alvia had best times in the 100 free and 100 back. 


Bristol's next meet is Friday 2/11/22, home against EO Smith.
