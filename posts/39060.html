American Legion Post 2 gifts local nonprofits

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=39060 Published on 2023-12-23 07:00:00

American Legion Post 2 commander Richard Schreiner presented donations to several local non-profit organizations earlier this week, according to a press release from the post.

Schreiner presented a check to local Salvation Army Captain Emmanuel Echavarria. A few days later Schreiner presented the Salvation Army Echavarria with five turkeys for the soup kitchen that is run from the church basement on Stearn Street.

Schreiner, later in the week, presented Brian's Angels executive director Pat Stebbins and board chair Lisa Whitney with a check intended to help clients of the nonprofits outreach to the homeless.

A third donation in the form of a check was presented to Veterans Stronger Community Center to assist veterans with their needs.
