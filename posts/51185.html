Bristol Central Cross Country teams defeat Platt, lose to Plainville in CCC tri-meet

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51185 Published on 2024-09-19 15:13:57

Plainville

The Bristol Central boys and girls cross country teams followed a similar route on Tuesday afternoon, defeating Platt but losing to Plainville.

The boys ran by Platt by a score of 15-49. The race with Plainville went the other way, 21-35 Plainville.

The girls from BC also beat Platt by a 15-50 score, while Plainville defeated the Rams, 20-43.
