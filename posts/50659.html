Bristol Sports Hall of Fame announces 2024 inductees to be honored at Nov. 22 Induction Dinner

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50659 Published on 2024-09-09 12:41:28

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame has announced that their annual induction ceremony will be held on Friday, Nov. 22, from 6 to 10 p.m. at the Double Tree in Bristol.

The inductees this year are Jeff Bashaw, Russ Carter, Bob Fallon, Taylor Losey, Bunty Ray, Beth Rotko, Rick Schmaltz and Cliff Snow III.

Tickets for the induction are $50 each and can be purchased on the HOF website at bshof.org or by calling ticket chairman Pat Parente at (860) 919-8087. 

No tickets will be sold at the door. 

Also, anyone interested in supporting the induction booklet should call Bob Kalat at (860) 877-0403 for more information.
