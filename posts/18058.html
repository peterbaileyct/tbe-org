Annual AIDS vigil held on Federal Hill Green Thursday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18058 Published on 2022-10-10 07:00:00

The annual AIDS Vigil was held on the Federal Hill Green, Thursday, Oct. 6. Bristol-Burlington Health Director Marco Palmeri and Mayor Jeffrey Caggiano were among those present for the candle light vigil.

Members of the Bristol Eastern High School Madrigals performed, joining among others, speakers BBHD Public Health Nurse Tina Tanguey and Bristol Health Chaplin Rev. Karen Hale.
