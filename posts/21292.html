St. Paul dominates in big win over Staples in Fairfield Holiday Tournament consolation game

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21292 Published on 2022-12-29 10:30:47

After a cold-shooting performance in the opener of the Fairfield Tournament, St. Paul exploded against Staples from the start, outscoring their opponents by 29 points at the half.

With a 42-13 advantage, the entire Falcon roster saw action throughout the second half, as eight different players hit the scoring column. The final on the night was an easy 57-27 St. Paul win.

Audrey Tice, who was named to the all-tournament team, led the Falcons with 17 points in the contest. Rebecca Kelly was close behind with a 16 point performance.

Kelsea Tefoe and Haileigh Orzel chipped in with 9 and 8 points apiece. Orzel also added 7 assists and 8 steals, exhibiting an impressive floor game.

St. Paul is now 2-1 on the season and plays at Wolcott Friday in a 7:00 varsity contest.
