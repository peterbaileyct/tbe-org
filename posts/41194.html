Podcast: Board of Education chair discusses priorities over the remaining school year

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41194 Published on 2024-02-07 07:00:00

By David Fortier

Dante Tagariello, who became the chair of the Bristol Board of Education in the fall of 2023, discusses priorities for the remainder of the school year, reorganizing the committee structure, the investment in a new middle school and the arrangement with the city over the sale of alcohol at The Rockwell Theater at the Bristol Artis and Innovation Magnet School.

The 25-minute conversation was held over the phone earlier in the week on Monday evening. The next Board of Education meeting is this evening at 7 p.m. in the Board of Education Auditorium. For the agenda, click here. For a link to the meeting, click here.
