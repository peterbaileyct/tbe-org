Bristol Eastern girls tennis falls to Manchester on Monday in Bristol by a 7-0 final

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12761 Published on 2022-04-26 18:10:42

By Michael Letendre 

BRISTOL – The Bristol Eastern girls tennis team fell to Manchester on Monday, dropping a tough 7-0 decision in Bristol.

But the Lancers showed some gumption, pushing several of its opponents to the brink.

Manchester’s top player, Chayse Mercer, defeated Brooke Treannie (6-2, 6-3) while at No. 2 singles, Eastern’s Adrianna Reay battled Dara Song but the Kingstreeter fell in consecutive sets, 6-3 and 6-2.

No. 3 Carolina Mancini defeated Eastern’s Kianna Ouellette (6-0, 6-0) and Manchester’s Leila Affini completed the singles sweep with a victory over Vanessa Cyr at No. 4 singles.

But in doubles play, the Eastern combination of Olivia Dossias and Ava Gesner gave Manchester’s number one duo of Maddie Scott and Grace Rowe a real run for their money.

After falling 6-1 in set one, the Lancers’ combination pulled off a 6-4 win in set two - forcing a tie breaking third set.

But the top Manchester twosome hung on to win, 11-9. 

At No. 2 doubles, Eastern’s Maria Perrone and Taylor Berube gave Manchester’s Ohanna Boahen and Aiyana Moore some on court heartburn as well.

Manchester won 6-4 and 7-5 and the No. 3 team of Feeha Amir and Sabrina Lam defeated Jazmein Pryce and Bella Davis (6-0, 6-1) to end the scoring. 

Eastern, now 0-5 on the season, battles Berlin in Friday in a CCC South confrontation from the tennis courts at BEHS.

BRISTOL EASTERN GIRLS TENNIS – CCC Interdivisional action 

MANCHESTER 7, BRISTOL EASTERN 0 

from the Tennis Courts at Bristol Eastern 

Singles 

1. Chayse Mercer defeated Brooke Treannie: 6-2, 6-3 

2. Dara Song defeated Adrianna Reay: 6-3, 6-2 

3. Carolina Mancini defeated Kianna Ouellette: 6-0, 6-0 

4. Leila Affini defeated Vanessa Cyr: No score 

Doubles 

1. Maddie Scott/Grace Rowe defeated Olivia Dossias/Ava Gesner: 6-1, 4-6, 11-9 

2. Ohanna Boahen/Aiyana Moore defeated Maria Perrone/Taylor Berube: 6-4, 7-5 

3. Feeha Amir/Sabrina Lam defeated Jazmein Pryce/Bella Davis: 6-0, 6-1. 

Records: Bristol Eastern 0-5 overall
