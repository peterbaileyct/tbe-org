St. Paul girls looking forward to the 2022-23 season

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20876 Published on 2022-12-15 07:53:21

The St. Paul girls basketball team begins the new season with some significant challenges this year. Several key players from last year have graduated and some others have encountered season-ending injuries.

Nevertheless, Coach Joe Mone has a group that is ready and eager to get this season going. Here is the Season Preview for the 2022-23 Falcons. The season begins tomorrow night as the Falcons host Kennedy from the Rev. Brian Monnerat Gymnasium.

St. Paul Girls Basketball

2021-22 record: 18-6

Coach Joe Mone is in his 28th season as head coach. He has a career record of 421-211

Key returning players: 

Seniors – Kelsea Tefoe, Abby Stanford, Haleigh Orzel, Sophie Bogdanski Natalia Reimer

Juniors- Olivia Dahn, Audrey Tice (All Division), Rebecca Kelly

Sophomores- Aniyah Moore, Maddy Bielecki, Iliana Chaplinski

Freshman- Zola Cudjoe, Carli Cerruto

Outlook for this season

Coach Mone said, “We have a bunch of players out with season ending injuries. We had 7 players gone from last year so building chemistry will be a priority in the beginning of the season and hopefully it translates to a much stronger team come February and March.”

Outlook for league:

Mone- “Holy Cross looks like the team at the top. I think there’s quite a few teams that can challenge for a league championship. I think we’ll be a better team in the second half of the season so hopefully we’ll be playing our best basketball then.”
