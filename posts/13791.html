David Parker

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13791 Published on 2022-06-03 19:40:47

It is with great sorrow we share the passing of Dave Parker. He passed peacefully with his loving family by his side on Sunday, May 22. He was a lifelong resident of Bristol and was well known by many. His vibrant smile would light up a room and make everyone comfortable. He was full of life with a huge heart. He would give you the shirt of his back and help you any way he could. He would also be the first to rise up and protect anyone that needed his help.

He is the son of Maureen Griffin Parker and the late David F. Parker. He leaves his most beloved children Zachery and Zoey, the loves of his life. His Brother Daniel and better half Brenda and his Brother Kevin. Nephews and Nieces Daniel, Matthew, Alex, Miranda and Katelyn. A very large extended family living all across the Country and Canada.

Family and Friends are invited for a celebration of life and wish him well and God Speed as he rides off into the sunset on his birthday at O’Brien’s Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave., Bristol, on Tuesday, June 7 from 5 to 7 p.m. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, June 8, at St. Joseph Church, 33 Queen St. Bristol, at 10 a.m. 

To leave an online message of condolence, share a memory or a photo, please visit Dave’s tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
