Timmy Ronald Daigle, 47

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16884 Published on 2022-09-09 07:00:00

Timmy Ronald Daigle, 47, of Bristol, died on Monday, Sept. 5. Timmy was born on August 22, 1975, in Hartford, Conn. He graduated from Bristol Central High School and worked as a self-employed carpenter. He was a man of many skills and talents when it came to carpentry. He was one that could take on a variety of different challenging projects and never disappoint. 

In his younger years he enjoyed playing rugby in Switzerland. He skied many mountains throughout the world as his parents traveled for work. He was an avid foosball player who loved to challenge some of his closest friends and family including Pat, Mario, and Paul amongst others. He was a devoted dog owner, care giver and best friend to his pit bull Ripley for many years. He had a heart of gold that would do anything for anyone at any point in time.

Timmy is survived by his mother and stepfather: Ginette (Benoit) and Chris Freiler of Bristol; his father and stepmother: Ronald and Christine Daigle of Inverness, Fla.; brothers: Tommy Daigle and wife Tina of Clarksville, Tenn., and John Freiler of Berlin, Germany; his nephew and godson: Zander Daigle; godson: Nathan Benoit; and several aunts, uncles, cousins, and many close friends. He was predeceased by a brother: Dane Freiler. Funeral services will be held on Monday, Sept. 12, at 9 a.m. from Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol, to St. Ann Church, 215 West St., Bristol, for a Mass of Christian of Christian Burial 10 a.m.

Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol. Relatives and friends may call at Funk Funeral Home on Sunday between 2 and 5 p.m. Please visit Timmy’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
