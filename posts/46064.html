Police Dept. announces traffic closures for Memorial Day Procession on the Boulevard

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46064 Published on 2024-05-24 16:36:43

As usual, Veterans Memorial Boulevard and Park will be closed for the entire Memorial Day holiday weekend, beginning at 7 p.m. this evening Friday, May 24, however, Monday's Memorial Day Procession will include additional road closures, including the following:

  • North Main Street from Hope Street to School Street
  • School Street from North Main Street to Memorial Boulevard
  • Main Street from Riverside Avenue to School Street
  • Riverside Avenue from North Main Street to Main Street

The road closures will begin on Monday, May 27, at approximately 9:30 a.m. The procession is scheduled to begin at 10 a.m. and continue for about an hour. The above listed roadways will be opened shortly after the end of the procession.
