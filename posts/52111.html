Bristol Police investigating shooting early this evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52111 Published on 2024-10-09 21:45:00

Bristol Police are investigating a shooting that occurred earlier today in the area of Conlon Street.

Officers responding to 911 calls of shots being fired around 7 p.m. located a single victim suffering from non-threatening injuries, according to a release.

The report states that the incident appears to be isolated and no threats to the public have been identified.

Since the investigation is active, identities are not being released at this time.

Anyone with knowledge of this incident is encouraged to contact the Bristol Police Dept. at (860) 584-3011.
