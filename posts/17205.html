B.E. Lancers volleyball handles Lewis Mills in straight sets

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17205 Published on 2022-09-18 18:10:49

Bristol Eastern had little trouble in handling Lewis Mills Friday night, winning three straight sets without much of a challenge.

The scores went as follows:

Set 1: 25-9
Set 2: 26-15
Set 3: 25-11

The Lancers were led by Lauren Bender with 14 kills and three aces, and Andrea Martir-Perez with 15 assists, six kills and four digs. Ashley Adorno added an ace and two kills.

For Lewis Mills, Emma Fallon had four blocks and Peyton DesJardin added an ace and five digs.

BE runs their record to 4-0.

Lewis Mills record is 3-1.

Bristol Eastern goes on the road all this week with games on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at Berlin-- 6 p.m., Simsbury--5 p.m., and Plainville--6 p.m., respectively.
