City announces leaf collection dates

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17737 Published on 2022-09-30 07:00:00

The Public Works Department has announced that they will begin Fall Leaf Bag curbside collection on Monday, October 17, 2022. Leaf collection will run through Friday, December 2, 2022 (weather-permitting, subject to change). 

Leaf bags are not collected on a specific day, but will be collected once a week. 

Please bring your leaf bags curbside as soon as they are filled and leave them curbside until collected. Leaf bag collection is available to all city residences eligible for curbside rubbish and recycling services. 

Leaves are only collected in brown, recyclable, paper leaf bags that are available at most home and garden stores. There is no maximum limit to leaf bag collection at a residence. Loose leaf piles will not be collected.
