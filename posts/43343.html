Jerome Avenue Bridge Project to begin next Monday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43343 Published on 2024-04-02 11:25:38

Detours are being put in place at the Jerome Avenue Bridge in preparation for the construction project, a release from Bristol Public Works states.

Public Works is asking for motorists to avoid the area for the duration of project.

A map of the streets around the Jerome Avenue Bridge project from the site plan. Leading up to Monday, April 8, the city will be preparing information about detours. | Public Works

Road closures will begin April 8. Local access will be provided on either side of the bridge.

Questions and/or comments regarding the project may be directed to Nancy Levesque, P.E., City Engineer by phone at 860-584-6125 or via email at nancylevesque@bristolct.gov.
