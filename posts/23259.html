Scholarship Established in Memory of Craig Yarde Accepting Applications

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=23259 Published on 2023-02-20 10:28:44

Main Street Community Foundation recently announced that they are accepting applications for The Craig Yarde Scholarship Fund from residents of Bristol who are graduating high school seniors, currently enrolled students, or adult learners seeking financial assistance for further post-secondary education or career training goals.

Established in 2022, The Craig Yarde Scholarship Fund honors the memory of Craig Yarde, a well-known
community leader and the co-founder of Yarde Metals, Inc., which began in Bristol in 1976. Craig personified entrepreneurial spirit, continual learning and self-achievement, and his family established this scholarship to inspire students who have the same work ethic and passion he displayed throughout his lifetime.

One $5,000 scholarship will be awarded. The deadline for all application submissions is March 10, 2023.
