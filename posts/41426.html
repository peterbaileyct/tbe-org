6 Bristol students named to Ben Bronz Academy honor roll

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41426 Published on 2024-02-15 07:00:00

Six Bristol students have made the Ben Bronz Academy 2nd quarter honor roll. They include the following: Melissa Calais Martins, 12th grade, honors; Mason Clevenger, 8th grade, high honors; Grayson Lamont, 3rd grade, honors; Brandin Roland, 7th grade, honors; Rocco Spirito, 11th grade, honors; and Evan Vallieres, 10th grade, high honors.

Ben Bronz Academy is a state-approved private special education school in West Hartford servicing students grades 2-12 from around Connecticut. Students have been identified with various learning disabilities, including: Dyslexia, Dysgraphia, Dyscalculia, Executive Function Difficulties, ADHD, CAPD, and ASD (mild/Asperger's).
