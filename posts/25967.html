St. Paul improves to 13-2 with five inning romp over WCA

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25967 Published on 2023-05-08 10:57:51

Pitchers Tyler Kiluk, Logan Rembish, Cole Raymond, and Isaac Leon threw a combined no-hitter and led St Paul to a 18-0 win over WCA in Bristol Friday afternoon.

The four pitchers combined to record twelve of the fifteen outs via strike out and walked none. Starter Tyler Kiluk picked up his first win of the season. Starter Ronier Martinez took the loss for WCA.

St Paul send seventeen men to the plate in the bottom of the 1st inning. The first fourteen reached base safely, and the first eleven scored. Logan Rembish led off the inning with a walk. Cole Raymond followed with a double to the left center field fence scoring Rembish all the way from first. Devin Kulas walked in front of RBI singles by Cal Chance and Ryan Kilpatrick. Jonathan Remillard reached on an infield error, and AJ Corrado followed with a two RBI single. Then Tony DeSena walked to load the bases, and Keaton Kroll followed with an RBI single. Pinch hitter Dom Napoletano was hit by a pitch driving in a run, and pinch hitter Carson Sklenka walked driving in another. Pinch hitters Bryce Adamski and Carlos Hernandez had RBI singles driving in two and one respectively, and St. Paul led 11-0 after one inning.

The Falcons took advantage of an infield error to score 7 runs (6 unearned) in the bottom of the 2nd inning. The inning featured key RBI hits by Chance, DeSena, and Napoletano. 

St Paul loaded the bases on walks and errors in both the 3rd and 4th innings, but failed to score another run. The final was 18-0.  

For St Paul

Keaton Kroll was 2 for 2 with 2 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

Cal Chance was 2 for 3 with 2 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

Cole Raymond was 1 for 1 with a double, 1 RBI, and 1 run scored.
