Board of Park Commissioners, Salary Committee and Code Enforcement Committee to meet today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54103 Published on 2024-11-20 07:00:00

Today will be a busy day at City Hall which will host several meetings from a variety of committees.   

The Code Enforcement Committee will meet at 3:00 p.m. in the Mayor’s Office, at City Hall, located a 111 North Main St.

The regular meeting of the salary committee will be held on 5 p.m. in the Human Resources Conference Room at City Hall, third floor.

The monthly meeting of the Board of Park Commissioners will meet at 6 p.m. in the City Hall Meeting Room 1-2.

A public meeting to learn more about the Pequabuck River Study will be held at 6 p.m. in the City Council Chambers.

A Special Meeting of the Fire Station 3 Building Committee will be held at 6 p.m.at Headquarters Station, located at 181 North Main St.
