Anthony Felice

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12256 Published on 2022-04-05 20:25:10

It is with great sadness, that on April 2, we lost our loving father, husband and grandpa, Anthony Felice at the age of 92.

He was born on April 11, 1929, in Bristol and was the last of eight surviving children born to Antonio and Maria (Ciccio) Felice, Sr. He was predeceased by his siblings, Josephine, Joseph, Lucy, Angelina, Teresa, Carmella and Rose; his wife Irene and his daughter Kathleen.

He is survived by his wife Audrey and his four surviving daughters that he had with Irene, Nancy, Sharon (Mark), Marcia (Kenneth), Laurie (Sue). He also leaves behind his seven grandchildren, five great-grandchildren and several nieces and nephews, including his godson Robert Krystofolski. The family would like to thank Sheri Tomchik for her excellent and loving care she provide for Anthony, she is a True Angel! He will be greatly missed by his daughter Nancy who cared for him daily along with Sheri.

Anthony was a graduate of Bristol High School and a retired self-employed carpenter and builder.

Calling hours will be held on Wednesday April 6, at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave., Bristol, from 8:30 a.m. until 9:30 a.m. followed by a procession to St. Matthew Church, Bristol, for a Mass of Christian Burial at 10AM then to St. Joseph Cemetery/Holy Family Mausoleum, Bristol, for the committal with military honors.

In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: St. Jude’s Children’s Research Hospital www.stjude.org/
