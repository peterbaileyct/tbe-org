49ers-Lions playoff history

Sunday, at 3:30 p.m., the 49ers and Lions meet for the third time in the playoffs.

The teams split their previous games. In 1957, the Lions won 31-27 to advance to the NFL Championship game, and in 1983, the San Francisco’s returned serve with a 24-23 win to move on to the NFC Championship game.
