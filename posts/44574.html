Bristol Central boys track dominates against Plainville Wednesday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44574 Published on 2024-04-25 09:55:22

Bristol Central boys track travelled to Plainville on Wednesday and came away with a 107-43 victory. The Rams collected first place finishes in fourteen out of the eighteen events. Here are a few highlights:

Rowan Hennessy took three firsts, in the 800 Meters and the 4x400 and 4x800 relays.

Julieen Lopez collected four firsts, in the 400 Meters and the two relays, and also took the high jump.

Patrick Wininger swept the distance races at 1600 and 3200 Meters, and helped the 4x800 relay team take first.
