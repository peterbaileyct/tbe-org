St. Paul performing arts presents 'Shrek The Musical'

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42066 Published on 2024-03-05 13:00:00

"Shrek The Musical" will be this year's spring production of the St. Paul Catholic High School arts department with shows beginning March 22.

This modern take on the classic tale of an underdog who learns the meaning of friendship, inner beauty, and that everything has layers is the perfect stage adventure for any age, according to the announcement from the school

"'Shrek The Musical' teaches us that everyone can find love, beauty isn’t always pretty, and that nothing is handed to you,” said director Mark Mazzarella, in the release. "This show presents a challenge to inhabit, physicalize, and explore these magical characters as an actor on stage.”

Performances will be Friday, March 22 at 7 p.m., Saturday, March 23 at 7 p.m. and Sunday, March 24 at 2 p.m.

Tickets are available online at www.spchs.com or they can be purchased on a first-come, first-served basis at the door.

All seats are reserved seating $20 for general public, $15 for students 18 and under and $15 for seniors 65 and older.
