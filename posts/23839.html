St. Paul’s hoops circa 1973

St. Paul’s won back-to-back state titles in men’s basketball in 1974 and 75. However, many forget that in 1973 they made it to the semifinals.

Despite controlling the first half (up 12-8 after 1 and 18-14 at the half), the Falcons (15-8) lost 53-35 to Northwest Catholic (20-2) ending the upstarts run. In just twelve months, though, they would go on a historic march to a state championship.
