Michael B. Rimcoski, 87

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21149 Published on 2022-12-24 07:00:00

Michael B. Rimcoski, 87, of Bristol, beloved husband of Diana (Tomcheski) Rimcoski, died on Monday, Dec. 19, at Bristol Hospital. Michael was born in Terryville on Aug. 19, 1935, and was a son of the late Michael and Wanda (Gotlibowski) Rimcoski.

He was raised in Terryville where he graduated from Terryville High School. He went on to attend Central Connecticut State University. A Bristol resident since 1960, he worked for Emhart Corporation for over 40 years before retiring. He then helped establish Quality Wire with his son, Jeff. He was active in Bristol politics as a former councilman and commissioner of the zoning board. He was a parishioner of St. Gregory the Great Church, Bristol.

In addition to his wife of 67 years, Michael is survived by a son and daughter-in-law: Jeffrey and Danielle Rimcoski of Bristol; a brother: Tyrone Rimcoski and wife, Mary of Deland, Fla.; two grandchildren: Michelle Fleckner and husband, Zachary, and Melissa Andrade and husband, Jeffrey; two great-grandchildren: Olivia Fleckner and Jeremy Andrade; and several nieces and nephews. He was predeceased by a daughter: Linda Ann Rimcoski.

Family and friends are invited to gather on Wednesday, Dec. 28 between 9:30 and 10:30 a.m. at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Avenue, Bristol.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 11 a.m. at St. Gregory the Great Church, 235 Maltby St., Bristol. Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.

Please visit Michael’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
