Republican primary for U.S. Senate candidate today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49490 Published on 2024-08-13 06:00:00

Polls throughout the city will be open from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m. today, Tuesday, Aug. 13, for the Republican primary for U.S. Senate candidate who will face incumbent U.S. Senator Chris Murphy in November.

The candidates are Gerry Smith, of Beacon Falls, who is the endorsed candidate, and Matthew M. Corey, of Manchester, who is the challenger.

For polling places, consult the following table. Note: Only registered Republicans can vote. In addition, Northeast Middle School is undergoing construction so the Stevens Street entrance will be closed. Voters must use the Felice Road entrance.

There is an assisted voting machine at all locations for voters who are hearing/sight impaired, and every privacy booth has wheelchair accessibility as does each polling location entrance per state statute.
