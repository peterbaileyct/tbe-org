The Adult Summer Reading Program begins Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46432 Published on 2024-06-01 07:00:00

The annual Adult Summer Reading Program returns to the Bristol Public Library and Manross Memorial Library this Saturday, June 1. 

Bristol residents aged 18 and over have the option of participating in the 2024 Summer Reading Program online at ReadSquared.com. Those who would prefer to use a paper summer reading log and packet may pick them up at the Bristol Public Library or Manross Library.  

Participants in the annual event will have the opportunity to earn tickets that may be put towards prizes. This year, there will be optional "missions" available to earn extra chances at winning prizes, as well as a special movie-lover's prize for checking out DVDs.  

Funding for all of the prizes in the Adult Summer Reading Program is provided by the Friends of the Bristol Public Library, a volunteer-based organization that formed in 1980. The nonprofit group works to promote the library as a cultural, educational and recreational asset to the city of Bristol. 

The Adult Summer Reading Program begins June 1, and continues through Aug. 16. For more information on the program, ask a librarian at the Bristol Public Library or Manross Library.

The Bristol Edition will be limiting the number of stories non-members and free readers may access each week.
