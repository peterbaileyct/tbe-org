Veterans Day Concert set Sunday, Nov. 5, at Rockwell Theater

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=35614 Published on 2023-10-18 07:00:00

A "Duty, Honor, Country: Salute to our Veterans" concert, presented by the Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble and directed by Richard Theriault, in honor of veterans will be held Sunday, Nov. 5, at the Rockwell Theater at BAIMS at 3 p.m. The concert is free and open to the public.

Assisting Theriault will be Kyle Camerato and the Bristol Chorale will be featured.

The Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble and the Bristol Chorale will perform well known favorites including "Liberty Fanfare" and "Hymn to the Fallen" by John Williams, "Armed Forces -- The Pride of America!"
"God Bless America" and the "Battle Hymn of the Republic," as well as other selections.

There will also be a reading of excerpts from the "Duty Honor Country" speech given to the cadets at the West Point Military Academy by Douglas MacArthur and a pictorial tribute to local veterans.

Admission is free; however monetary donations are always greatly appreciated and accepted. A portion of all donations received will go to the Bristol veteran's council. Visit the Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble website at www.bbandwe.org for more information.

The City of Bristol provided funding for the program.
