Rock-folk jam collective to perform at New Cambridge Project Saturday evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48729 Published on 2024-07-24 07:00:00

By Maria Caceres

Singer-songwriter Benny Mikula will be performing at the New Cambridge Project brewery this Saturday, July 27, at 6 p.m.

Mikula, a Bridgeport native, is lead singer and chief songwriter for The Alpaca Gnomes, a rock-folk jam collective.

“We find that people come more when they are having fun and listening to music while having drinks,” NCP brewery owner James Ferro III said.

The event will also have catering from the Red Apple Pizza Truck with plenty of parking and a patio outside that is open to pets.

Weekend events are regular features of the new brewery which is located at the location of the former Firefly Hollow on Center Street.
