Aroma Joe’s celebrates grand opening on May 25th

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13612 Published on 2022-05-30 12:08:58

The FIRST Aroma Joe’s location in the state of Connecticut celebrated its grand opening with a ribbon cutting ceremony on May 25. City of Bristol Mayor Jeffrey Caggiano and Tamara Carlson, CEO of the Bristol Boys & Girls Club, were onsite along with Aroma Joe’s franchisees, staff and other local dignitaries.

The location opened on April 4 and saw record-breaking first week sales.

In celebration of the opening and to honor the Bristol community, all Aroma Joe’s Bristol profits from May 27, 2022, were donated to the Boys & Girls Club of Bristol.
