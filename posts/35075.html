BEHS volleyball team dominates E.O. Smith, improving record to 8-3

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=35075 Published on 2023-10-07 07:00:00

The Bristol Eastern volleyball team made quick work of E.O. Smith on Thursday night, rolling to an easy three-set victory. The set scores were 25-9, 25-14, and 25-8, as the Lancers excelled in all aspects of the game. The only positive for the visitors was they got back to Storrs pretty early, as the match was over almost as soon as it began.

Stats for BE: 
Samara Paranzino had three kills and two digs.

Harper Dauphinee contributed with two kills and three aces.

And Katie Beaulieu chipped in with three aces and a dig.

Stats for EO Smith:
Bronwyn Mott had five kills.

Records:

Bristol Eastern 8-3
EO Smith 1-8

The Lancers don’t play again until next Wednesday, Oct. 11, hosting Farmington at 6:00.
