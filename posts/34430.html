Bristol police announce traffic closures for Mum Festival

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=34430 Published on 2023-09-22 13:50:02

The Bristol Police Department has announced the following road closures for this weekendâ€™s Mum Festival today:

The Memorial Boulevard will be closed from Friday, Sept. 22 at 11 a.m. for the Mum Festival until Sunday, Sept. 24, at 9 p.m.

On Sunday, Sept. 24, at about 1 p.m., the Mum Festival will be holding their annual parade in the downtown area. The following areas and their intersecting streets will be affected;

  • South Street
  • North Main Street
  • Main Street
  • West Street
  • Race Street
  • Riverside Avenue extension
    Detours will be posted to help guide motorists around the affected areas. The parade is expected to end at 4 p.m.
    Please plan accordingly.
