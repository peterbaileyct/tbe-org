Reps. Pavalock-D'Amato, Betts, and Sen. Martin to host Pizza and Politics Legislative Event

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12284 Published on 2022-04-06 12:01:00

In an announcement today, State Representatives Cara Pavalock-D'Amato (R-77), Whit Betts (R-78), and State Senator Henri Martin (R-31) are inviting area residents to join them on Tuesday, April 12 for a Pizza and Politics legislative event.

WHEN: Tuesday, April 12th from 6:30 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
WHERE: Max Pizza 4, 454 Middle Street, Bristol

The lawmakers will provide the latest updates from the State Capitol on the 2022 legislative session and answer any questions residents may have on issues related to state government. Complimentary pizza will be provided at the event.

All residents are encouraged to attend. If anyone is unable to make the event but would still like to speak with their representatives, the House Republican Office can be reached at 800-842-1423 or Senate Office at 800-842-1421.
