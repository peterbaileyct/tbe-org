Big Dawg Custom Apparel and Team Redman's take Fall Parks and Rec softball championships

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=37064 Published on 2023-11-09 07:00:00

Team Big Dawg Custom Apparel took home the championship title in the 2023 Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services men’s 40-and-over Fall Softball League.

Team Redman’s swept the 2023 Co-Ed Fall Softball League to be named champion.

After finishing the regular season with a 17-2 record, the #1-seed Big Dawg Custom Apparel secured the league championship with an undefeated playoff run, ultimately defeating Misfits in the championship game.

Redman’s finished the regular season as the #1-seed after going undefeated with a 14-0 record. The team continued their dominance with an undefeated playoff run, ultimately defeating Tune Squad in the championship game.

For Spring Softball League team registrations, register online at www.BristolRec.com. For individuals looking to join a fall team, contact League Director Shawn Mirmina at shawnmirmina@bristolk12.org.

For more information, visit www.BristolRec.com.
