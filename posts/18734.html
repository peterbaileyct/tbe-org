Eastern volleyball loses to Conard Wednesday night, 3-1

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18734 Published on 2022-10-20 08:28:25

Bristol Eastern knew they were in for a tough night Wednesday when Conard, rated number four in the state, invaded the Lancer gym. The Lancers played the Red Wolves tough throughout the evening, but eventually fell to the powerful Conard hitters, 3 sets to 1.

The score in sets was:

Set 1: 18-25
Set 2: 18-25
Set 3: 25-16
Set 4: 16-25

Conard had several strong hitters, and Zahara Quraishi led the Red Wolves with 14 kills, 10 digs, and 2 aces. Nina Buezynski had a tremendous floor game with 32 assists, 6 digs, and 2 aces.

The Lancers’ Emily Valentine had a strong night with 4 aces, 1 kill, and 10 digs. Lauren Bender had 12 kills and 11 digs, Gabby Caucci had 2 aces, 4 kills, and 6 digs, and Adrianna Reay had 2 aces, 6 kills, and 7 digs.

BE’s record drops to 10-5, while Conard improves to 13-2.

The Lancers face another enormous task today as they host Southington at 5:15.
