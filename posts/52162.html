Eastern boys soccer shuts out Platt on Tuesday, 5-0

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52162 Published on 2024-10-10 21:15:52

In a Tuesday evening match, Bristol Eastern (2-6-3) beat visiting Platt (2-10), 5-0, in Bristol. Leading the way for the Lancers were Leslie Adzo with two goals and Alex Szynal with two assists.

 A Lucas Tylicky perfectly placed corner kick into the far-right corner of the Platt goal at 29:50 got Eastern on the board. 

The Lancers added to their lead at 19:23 as Szynal connected with Adzo who cut down the middle and notched his second goal of the year. 

At 16:56, Szynal once again won a ball, sent a pass to Adzo, who hit the ball into the left corner of the Platt net for a 3-0 lead into halftime.

"It was great to see us put pressure on Platt early and finish our shots," said Eastern coach Bill Sweet. "We didn't allow Platt to get into their passing game and we put ourselves in good position to score.  The deep four backs, Szynal, Landon Gawel, Drew Viens, Sam LaMar, and two central midfielders, John Saraceno  and Eli Emmert, cut off Platt's attack."

Eastern upped the score to 4-0 in the second half as Gawel intercepted a pass and sent a ball to a cutting Matt Cabrera.  At the top of the Platt penalty area, Cabrera cut to his left and sent a hard shot into the Platt net at 27:31. 

Matias Basantes finished Eastern's scoring at 33:42 when he lofted a high shot into Platt's goal.

Eastern's goalies,  Connor Murtaugh (1) and Chris Duplessis (2), had just three saves, while Platt's combination of Joel Colmenares (4) and Jordan Colmenares (6) had 10 saves.

The Lancers played at Berlin today in a 6 p.m. contest.
