Bristol tax bills are on the horizon

  • Tax bills will be mailed out on June 28.
  • Payments must be mailed by Aug. 1.
  • The 2024 mill rate is 31.85 per $1,000 of assessment on Real Estate and Personal Property.
  • The Motor Vehicle mill rate is 32.46.
