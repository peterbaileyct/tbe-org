School Street closed after stabbing last evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=29655 Published on 2023-08-11 08:54:50

Police are encouraging motorists to find alternative routes around the areas between School Street between West Street and North Main Street for their morning commute as there is an ongoing investigation of a stabbing and a disturbance that involved shots fired late last night.

Officers responding to a report of a disturbance on School Street Thursday evening found evidence of a serious assault, according to a release from the Police Dept. Police were notified soon afterward that a 17-year-old stabbing victim had been admitted at a local hospital.

The stabbing victim was transferred by LifeStar to a trauma hospital, has undergone surgery and remains in critical but stable condition, according to the release. The name of the victim is not being released.

The investigation is ongoing. Additional information will be released at a later time.

The Bristol Police are requesting that anyone with any information concerning the incident call (860)584-3011.
