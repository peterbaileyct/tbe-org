What battered motorists pay at the pump has increased for a variety of reasons affecting supplies and ultimately consumers. Using apps like GasBuddy or Gas Guru to locate stations with the lowest prices in the area will help alleviate some of the agony felt at the pump.

The GasBuddy App can be found either on Apple's App Store on on Google Play.

Google Play is also home to Gas Guru.
