Rockwell Park concert series begins next Tuesday, June 21

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14075 Published on 2022-06-15 07:00:00

The Bristol Park Department is again offering a free weekly Summer Concert Series at the Rockwell Park Amphitheater this summer.

This year's series features 8 weeks of outstanding performances every Tuesday Night from 6:00 - 8:00 pm beginning June 21st. Opening acts perform from 6:00 - 6:20 & main acts go on 6:30 p.m..

The concerts will feature a variety of musical performances including Pop, Funk, Rhythm & Blues, Country & Classic Rock music and more. Bring your family & friends, blankets & chairs, and even grab dinner at the food trucks, and enjoy this free night of entertainment each week.
