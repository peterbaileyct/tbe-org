Bristol Sports Hall of Fame announces 2022 Inductees

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=15545 Published on 2022-08-02 16:00:00

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame has recently announced its nine inductees for 2022.

The newest members of the BSHOF are: Leon and Gussie Breen, Mario Marrero, Tracy Pomerenke Regan, Clem Roy, Reinaldo Soto, Chris Strahowski, Kim Corbin Varasconi, and Pete Wininger.

Bob Kalat, vice president of the Bristol Sports Hall of Fame and chairman of the selection committee, made the announcement and congratulated the new members.

"The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame is honored to name the following individuals as the BSHOF Class of 2022. They will now join the impressive list of all past inductees and continue the tradition of representing the best of Bristolâ€™s sports heritage."

The Induction Ceremony will be held on Friday, Nov. 18 at the DoubleTree in Bristol. More information will be available in the near future and on the Hall of Fame website at bshof.org.
