Back to School Bash set at BAIMS Monday evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49988 Published on 2024-08-24 07:00:00

The Bristol Public Schools' Back to School Bash will be held Monday, Aug. 26, from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. at the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School.

An invitation to the event, for families and students, was among the messages included in a post on ParentSquare from Acting Superintendent Iris White on Friday.

"This year promises to be one filled with exciting achievements and memorable moments," White writes, including a reminder about Monday's event.

The bash will feature games and activities, food and refreshments, time to meet teachers and staff and school tours.

There will be face painting, raffles and prizes, food trucks and free backpacks for the first 250 students. All children must be accompanied by an adult.

The 2024-25 school year begins Thursday, Aug. 29. This year's theme is "Together We Will Thrive in '25."

"Let’s work together to make this year a wonderful one," White says. "Here’s to a year full of growth, inspiration, and joy for our students, staff, and families!"

White expresses her gratitude from the community for its support and enthusiasm, and she shares that staff is excited about building on past successes.

"Your involvement and support are vital to our shared success," White states, "and we are committed to finding new ways to help you stay connected and engaged throughout the year."
