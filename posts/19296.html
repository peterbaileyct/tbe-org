Bristol Democratic Town Committee to host campaign rally at Firefly Hollow on Thursday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=19296 Published on 2022-11-01 15:10:30

The Bristol Democratic Town Committee has announced they will be hosting a campaign rally at Firefly Hollow Brewing, 139 Center St. on Thursday, Nov. 3, from 6 to 8 p.m.

The event is open to the community and will feature local and statewide candidates for public office. It is free to attend, but donations for the Bristol DTC will be accepted. The Bristol Democrats invite you to come for a night of food, fun and friends.

The event is being held five days prior to Election Day, Tuesday, Nov. 8, and serves as a reminder of the importance of voting and having a plan to vote either in person at the polls or by absentee ballot for those eligible.

The town committee has also announced that they will provide rides to the polls on Election Day. If you need a ride, contact the Bristol DTC at (860) 506-4020.
