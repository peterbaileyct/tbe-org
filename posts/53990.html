BristolWORKS! to host hiring event Nov. 21

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53990 Published on 2024-11-17 07:00:00

Local collaborative workforce development training center BristolWORKS! Will be hosting several workshops and events this fall.  

ClarkDietrich will be holding a Hiring Event on Nov. 21.

Available positions include Manufacturing Team Member, Manufacturing Production Manager, Maintenance Technician, and more. No registration is required for this event. 

  • Thursday, Nov. 21
  • 4:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.
  • BristolWORKS! 430 North Main St.

M&T Bank is holding Build Your Financial Confidence Workshop on Dec. 5

We’re here to help with what’s important, no matter where life takes you. With our no cost financial wellness workshops and M&T’s Financial Education Center, you can get access to information on a variety of topics like budgeting, credit management, building wealth & retirement, and more. It’s like having the expertise and knowledge of an M&T Banker anywhere you go. 

  • Thursday, Dec. 5
  • 5:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m.
  • BristolWORKS! 430 North Main St.

For more information, visit https://bristolworksct.org
