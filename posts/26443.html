Is it Friday already? That can’t be possible.

Friday weather

Another great day, with sunny skies and highs in the upper 60’s. It will be windy again coming from the Southwest at 10-15 m.p.h.

But the good news is that it will rain on Saturday, so you won’t have to work in the yard.

Trivia answer

The only last place MLB team with a winning record is the Boston Red Sox (24-20 as of Thursday night). So those of us who are proud Sox fans can boast of the best worst team in baseball.

Friday events

Gov. Lamont visits Bristol at Muzzy Field at 1:30 p.m.

Dairy Queen on Farmington Avenue will have a big sale -- two handfuls of dirt for the price of one. Whipped cream is extra.

On this day

In 1946, Andre the Giant is born.

In 2018, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle are married.

Adios

It is now time to say goodbye. In the words of the immortal Roy Rogers, "Happy trails to you, until we meet again.”
