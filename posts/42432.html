Single car accident results in no injuries, minor property damage

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42432 Published on 2024-03-11 09:45:00

A single car, motor vehicle accident occurred earlier this evening around 5:20 p.m. in the area of 998 Farmington Ave., Bristol Police report. The driver was arrested for operating under the influence. Minor property damage occurred. No one was injured during the accident.
