Bristol American Legion defeats Torrington in slugfest, 17-12

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48587 Published on 2024-07-21 07:35:00

Bristol American Legion defeated Torrington 17-12 at Torrington on Wednesday. Caleb Molinsy knocked in four runs and Jose Feliciano added three more, including one home run, to lead Bristol's offense. Connor Cyr went five for five with one RBI and scored three runs. Jonny Montgomery, Rocco Spirito, Ryan Maglio and Molinsy each had two hits. Montgomery scored four runs.
