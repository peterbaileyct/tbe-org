Platt deals Bristol Eastern volleyball a four set defeat Monday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18664 Published on 2022-10-19 07:00:00

Bristol Eastern travelled to Meriden for a volleyball match with Platt Monday evening, and they returned home with their fourth loss of the season.

The Panthers jumped out quickly with a first set victory at 25-19. They then dominated set two for 25-12 decision.

Eastern was able to stay alive by edging Platt in the third set by a score of 25-23.

Whatever hope the Lancers had was quickly snuffed out as Platt easily clinched the match with a fourth set 25-13 margin.

Platt was led by Eva Dudley with 20 kills, 7 digs, and 3 aces. The Panthers’ Emily Hart had 23 assists, 15 digs and 4 aces.

The Lancers’ Lilli Lowe had 4 kills, a block and 2 digs in a losing cause.

BE’s record now stands at 10-4.

Platt’s record is now 11-5.

Bristol Eastern gets back home for A 6:00 match tonight against Conard.
