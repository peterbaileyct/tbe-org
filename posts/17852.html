Bristol police issue silver alert

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17852 Published on 2022-10-03 21:20:18

On Monday morning, the Bristol Police Department issued the following Silver Alert.

The Bristol Police Department is seeking the public’s help in locating a missing juvenile. 17-year old Gracie (Gray) Aiudi was last seen at home in Bristol on 10/02/22 at approx. 2130hrs. She is described as a white female with pink and blond hair, blue-green eyes, approx. 130lbs, last seen wearing a black Panic at the Disco t-shirt with red lettering, no further clothing description. Gracie has no car or cell phone and has not shown up at her place of employment. 

Anyone having any information concerning Gracie’s whereabouts or who may have seen her are encouraged to call the Bristol Police Department at 860-584-3011.
