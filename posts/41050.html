BE wrestlers continue perfect record in CCC South with 58-21 decision over Plainville Wednesday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41050 Published on 2024-02-03 10:09:51

The Lancer wrestling team continued their March through the CCC South Wednesday night with a convincing win over Plainville.

Eastern’s Yussuf Bah, Juan Florentino, Luke Viens, Gabe White, Adoni Fernandez, Rocco Barbino, Owen Broyles, Aiden Davies, and Rio Fernandez all won by pin.

Soph Um won a major decision to add Four points to the Lancer’s total.

Eastern’s record is now 14-2, 5-0 in CCC South

The CCC tournament is this Friday and Saturday, which will feature all teams from the four CCC conferences . 

The Lancers’ last conference and regular season match will be at home against Bristol Central next Wednesday.

Then Eastern will be competing in the Class M tournament Feb. 16th and 17th.
