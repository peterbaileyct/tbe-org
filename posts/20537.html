Birthdays

On this day, in 1908, "Baby Face" Nelson was born. He was an American bank robber and gangster who killed more FBI agents than any other criminal. He was born in Chicago, Ill., the cradle of American gangsters.

All loyal N.Y. Giants fans know it is Andy Robustelli's birthday. A great defensive end, he weighed in at 225 pounds, a little smaller than today's behemoths. He was born in Stamford, Conn., in 1925.

And for all you cricket fans, Peter Willey, an English cricketer of the late 70's early 80's, was born in Sedgefield, County Durham, England, in 1949.
