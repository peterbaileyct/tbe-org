D.J. Hernandez arrested after ESPN incident

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24416 Published on 2023-03-28 08:00:00

WFSB reported earlier this morning that D.J. Hernandez, brother of the late Aaron Hernandez, was arrested last week after allegedly tossing a brick, with a note attached, on to ESPN property.

The arrest follows another episode earlier in the month in Cheshire in which Hernandez was wanted for attempting unauthorized use of a credit card belonging to a family member.

The WFSB report includes information that Hernandez might be experiencing extreme behavior, citing a bipolar condition. A screen shot of the note, attributed to TMZ, appears in the WFSB account. For the report, click here.

Regarding the Bristol incident, which took place on March 23, Hernandez was arrested for breach of peace and is scheduled to appear in court next week.

The incident at ESPN has been reported in TMZ, Sports Illustrated and the New York Post.
