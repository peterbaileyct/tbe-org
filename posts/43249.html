Early voting resumes this morning at 10 a.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43249 Published on 2024-03-30 06:00:00

Early voting resumes this morning, March 30, at 10 a.m. and runs through 6 p.m. this evening in city chamber chambers.

On Tuesday, the vote count was 52, Wednesday around 50, and by noon on Thursday, 13, according to moderator Amy Wernicki. There was no voting yesterday, on Good Friday.

The deadline for first-time registration to vote in person in this year's primary on Tuesday, April 2, is noon on April 1 at the registrar’s office.

For the April 2 primary, there is one change in polling places–those who voted at Bristol Elks Club will now be voting at the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School.
