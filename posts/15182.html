Bristol Park Department crowns 2022 Men’s Spring Softball League Champions

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=15182 Published on 2022-07-22 07:00:00

Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services recently announced the champions of its 2022 Men’s Spring Softball Leagues: Fancy Bagels & the Kookies.

The 3-seeded Fancy Bagels claimed the Men’s 40-and-Over Spring League championship, winning all three of their games in the single-elimination playoff tournament.

The top-seeded Kookies, who went 17-1 in the regular season, claimed the Men’s 18+ Spring League championship.


“Both leagues are looking forward to another exciting fall season,” League Director Shawn Mirmina said. “Many of the same teams will be returning, and we always look forward to welcoming new teams to join the league.”

The Fall Softball League is scheduled to begin in the coming weeks, with the Men’s 18+ Weeknight League scheduled to run from August 8-October 28, and the Men’s 40-and-Over League scheduled to run from July 31-November 6.

For Fall Softball League team registrations, register online at www.BristolRec.com. For individuals looking to join a fall team, contact League Director Shawn Mirmina at shawnmirmina@bristolk12.org.
