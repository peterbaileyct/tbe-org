Eastern boys soccer suffers tough 1-0 loss to Avon Friday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50930 Published on 2024-09-14 14:14:34


Avon (3-1-1) used a Rayhan Shameen goal to defeat Bristol Eastern (1-2-1), 1-0, in a   Friday evening contest at Bristol Eastern.

   "It was a close contest, with us carrying the early momentum and Avon getting energy and a goal late in the first half," said Eastern coach Bill Sweet.   "We had many free kicks and corners, but couldn't capitalize on them."

   With twelve minutes left in the first half, Avon's Diego Garza sent a ball into the Eastern penalty area that resulted in a collision between Shameen and Lancer goalie, Connor Murtaugh.  The ball fell loose to the ground and Shameen was able to knock it into the net for the game's only score.

    Both sides had chances to score in the second half, but each side's defense was able to thwart the opponent's attack.  Murtaugh ended with 7 saves while Avon's Jordan Beaudoin made 10 saves.

Eastern travels to Wethersfield on Monday for a 3:45 game and returns home to host Southington on Thursday at 6 p.m.
