Pedestrian struck by car on Pine Street

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47564 Published on 2024-06-24 11:00:00

A pedestrian was struck by a vehicle early Monday morning on Pine Street

The accident occurred around 4:45 a.m. near Cumberland Farms, at the intersection of Pine and Emmett streets.

A witness on the scene stated that the pedestrian was a homeless woman.

“She was crossing the street with her cart, when a car came and hit her in the middle of the road. The driver went through the red light," he said.

"I went into the road to block any other cars from hitting her again because I didn’t know if they could see her on the ground, and I didn’t want her to get hit again.”

Police are currently on scene. The road is closed, and Dept. of Transportation is encouraging drivers to seek alternative routes.
