West End Association to Host Annual Holiday Meeting and Party

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54130 Published on 2024-11-25 19:00:00

BRISTOL- The West End Association will host their annual holiday meeting and party on Thursday, Dec. 5, from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. at the Bristol Bazaar, located at 67 Race St.

The festive event will feature a mix of business and celebration, as the association reviews its achievements from the past year and discusses plans for the upcoming year. In addition to the meeting, attendees can enjoy holiday food, drink and socializing with neighbors.

“We will have a brief official meeting with a summary of 2024 and plans for 2025 followed by drink and merriment,” said West End Association Vice President Jesse Jablon. “Food & Wine provided. Additional beverages available at Yellow King with cash bar.”

The West End Association is a community-driven organization that works to improve the quality of life in the West End neighborhood, fostering a sense of community and helping to support local businesses and initiatives.

Prior to the annual holiday meeting and party, the Bristol Bazaar will be holding a Gingerbread making contest onsite. Those interested in entering their own gingerbread house or as a member of the West End Association should contact The West End Association. 

Those interested in attending the West End Association annual holiday meeting and party should RSVP to westendbristol@gmail.com
