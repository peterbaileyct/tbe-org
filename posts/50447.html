Max out in September with Season 16

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50447 Published on 2024-09-05 07:00:00

By Drew a.k.a ZypherFusion

Hello Trainers!

September is time to get maxed out! The new season is upon us, and it means for new shake ups on Pokémon Go. Don’t miss your start to go big on Season 16 for this event!

Max Out is Pokémon GO’s 16th in-game starts on Tuesday, Sept. 3, at 10 a.m. During this Season, the Dynamax phenomenon from Pokémon Sword and Shield will be featured in Pokémon GO. Additional Pokémon from the Galar region will also be added to the game.

For the raids, here are Pokémon and the times for each listed below, along with their locations:

Times

  • Mega Absol Sept. 3 – Sept. 14
  • Mega Houndoom Sept. 14 – Sept. 26
  • Mega Gardevoir Sept. 26 – Oct. 4
  • Kyogre Sept. 3 – Sept. 14
  • Groudon Sept. 14 – Sept. 26
  • Zacian Sept. 26 – Oct. 4

Locations

  • Federal Hill Green, 33 Queen St.
  • Page Park, 651 King St.
  • Rockwell Park, 238 Jacobs St.

Raid Hours take place every Wednesday from 6 to 7 p.m., and you won’t have to worry about time zones as it will always be your local time. This is the perfect chance to get out there and catch yourself a Legendary Pokémon.

Since the Galarian region is being featured, a variety of new Pokémon debuts will be happening throughout the entire Season of Max Out.

Galar region Starters: Grookey, Scorbunny, Sobble, and their evolved forms will debut in Pokémon GO at the start of the GO All Out event. So far, Skwovet, Wooloo, Falinks, and Zacian have been confirmed to have their shiny debuts in Pokémon GO during September. Shiny variant Pokémon from the Galarian region to be added throughout the season.

Dynamax Bulbasuar, Dynamax Charmander, Dynamax Squirtle, Dynamax Skwovet, and Dynamax Wooloo debut in Pokémon GO during the GO Big event.

Zacian and Zamazenta will be appearing in Five-Star Raids during the season. Dreepy (and its evolved forms) will debut during the GO All Out event. More Pokémon debuts are speculated later in September and throughout October and November (including Halloween-themed Costumed Pokémon).

The Dynamax Phenomena will first appear on Sept. 10, in Pokémon GO, and select Pokémon will make their Pokémon GO Dynamax debuts in one-star Max Battles.

Be sure to gear up for next week as we look forward to September! For more information, visit www.pokemongolive.com. Until next time Trainers!
