Reno Dumond

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12086 Published on 2022-03-27 20:12:58

Reno Dumond, 82, of Bristol, beloved husband of Rolande (Caron) Dumond, died on Friday, March 25, at St. Francis Hospital.

Reno was born in St. Agatha, Maine, on July 10, 1939, and was a son of the late Alsime and Leda (Lagasse) Dumond. He was raised in St. Agatha before enlisting to serve in the United States Army. He and Rolande married and moved to Bristol in 1967 where he would enjoy a career as a union carpenter with Local 24 until retiring in 2002. He was known for his sense of humor; his laugh could light up a room. He was a parishioner of St. Gregory the Great Church, Bristol.

In addition to his wife of 54 years, Reno is survived by two daughters: Michele Winiarski and husband, Tom of Bristol, and Dawn Moore and husband, Tim Rogers of Maine; one brother: Roland Dumond of Windsor; a sister: Nancy Berube and husband, Donald of Bristol; five grandchildren: Leah, Luke, Lyle, Lucy Winiarski, and Tyler Moore and wife, Elizabeth; and many nieces and nephews.  He was predeceased by six brothers:  Norman, Lawrence, Gilbert, Gilman, Gerald, and Emelio Dumond, and three sisters: Edwina Dufour, Lena Hebert, and Alphena Chase.

Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, March 30, at 10:30 a.m. from Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol, to St. Gregory the Great Church, 235 Maltby St., Bristol, for a Mass of Christian Burial at 11:30 a.m.

Military honors will follow the Mass. Burial will be at the convenience of the family.  Relatives and friends may call at Funk Funeral Home on Tuesday between 5 and 7 p.m. Please visit Reno’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
