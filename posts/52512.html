Bristol Police seek help locating missing juvenile

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52512 Published on 2024-10-18 19:30:00

Bristol Police are currently investigating a missing juvenile complaint. If anyone has any information on the whereabouts of Morgan Ciccio, please contact the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.

Ciccio is a 13-year-old white female, approximately 5 feet 1 inch tall, weighing around 110 pounds with long dark brown hair. Ciccio was last seen wearing a long-sleeved black shirt, black pants, and no shoes.
