Bristol Soccer Club Travel Team tryouts to commence in June

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=26893 Published on 2023-05-31 07:54:09

By Michael Letendre 

The Bristol Soccer Club has a big week of activities and deadlines as the program is looking for athletes and players to join its ranks. 

And there’s plenty going on as tryouts are on the horizon. 

Here’s what is going on with the BSC, starting with travel tryouts. 

Travel Tryout Registration Ends Friday 

Registration is now open for 2023-24 travel tryouts.  

If your player is interested in trying out for the BSC travel program, please register at https://leagues.bluesombrero.com/Default.aspx?tabid=1465102 for the appropriate birth year and gender tryout no later than Friday, May 26 at 5 p.m. 

All players who wish to be considered for a 2023-24 travel team must participate in the tryout process each year for the coming fall's appropriate age division based on birth year, regardless of their spring 2023 participation with BSC.
