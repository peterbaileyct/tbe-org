Bristol Central girls track and field falls to Maloney on Wednesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12807 Published on 2022-04-28 18:17:54

By Michael Letendre 

BRISTOL – The Bristol Central girls track and field team made some gains against the program from Maloney in a CCC South meet on Wednesday. 

And while the Rams dropped a tough 86-60 decision from the track and field at Bristol Central, the home team made a few first places tallies  here and there. 

Central’s Destinee Craft won the long jump with a leap of 15 feet and a-half-an-inch while teammate Kaiya Yuschak netted a six-foot, six inch make in the pole vault to win the event. 

Rebecca Litwinczyk was tremendous in the 200 meters, taking victory in 27.3 seconds. 

And in the 4x400 relay, the team from Bristol Central was able to snare the win away from the Spartans. 

The combination of Litwinczyk, Katie Tran, Francesca Amara, and Lynae Lewis finished in 4:38 for the win. 

Central is back in action next Tuesday on the road against Plainville. 

The event takes place at 3:45 from Tinty Track and Field in Plainville.
