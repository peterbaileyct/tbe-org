1973 NLCS

Monday night at the TBE headquarters, for some unknown reason, the GMB staff found itself discussing the 1973 NLCS between the Cincinatti Reds and New York Mets.

Going into the playoff series, the Reds complied a regular season record of 99-63 with a winning percentage of .633. Meanwhile, the Mets were 82-79 with a winning percentage of .509.

The Reds and Mets squared off 12 times during the regular season with the Reds winning eight of the games.

None of that mattered come October. The Mets won the series 3 games to 2 and outscored the Reds 23-8.
