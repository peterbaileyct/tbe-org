Rumors of Chippanee sale are debunked

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49638 Published on 2024-08-16 12:45:00

The latest Chippanee Country Club weekly news email contains an item dispelling rumors that the club is selling to a developer who would be building condominiums on the property.

"There is a rumor going around that the club is sold or has been sold to a developer looking to build homes on the property," the announcement reads, appearing under the title, "Rumor control..."

"The rumor is NOT TRUE!!! We would not have invested in sprinkler systems, car paths, and all the other infrastructure improvements if the club were up for sale!!!"

The announcement, in the Aug. 13 newsletter, concludes with an appeal to those who are contributing to the rumors to, please, stop.

"If you are part of the rumor mill, please stop as this could hurt our efforts to attract new members, outside events and weddings."
