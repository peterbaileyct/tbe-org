Kayla S. Miner

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11825 Published on 2022-03-14 18:42:28

Kayla S. Miner, 29, of Bristol, passed away on Friday. March 11. She was born on August 22, 1992, in New Britain, daughter of Mitchell and Justine (Chapman) Miner.

Kayla was a graduate of Bristol Central High in 2010 and went on to work at the Summit of Plantsville for several years in the kitchen. She loved to cook and loved working with the elderly and they loved her kind heart. 

Besides her parents Kayla is survived by her Grandmother Justine Ayrton of Salem, her brothers Daryl Davis and wife Sarah of Forestville, Nolan Remillard and spouse Heather Polk of Bristol, Sister Donna Nieves and husband Rey of Bristol, her nieces Brianna Thibeault of Bristol, Tyna Nieves of Plainville, Nephew Zackery Nieves and wife Kelly of Bristol and great-niece Averly Nieves of Bristol and many Aunts, Uncles, and cousins.

Calling hours will be held on Friday, March 18, at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave., Bristol starting at 3 p.m. until a 5 p.m. service will begin.

To leave an online message of condolence, share a memory or a photo, please visit Kayla’s tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
