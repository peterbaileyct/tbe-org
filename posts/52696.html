15-year-old is missing, Silver Alert issued

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=52696 Published on 2024-10-22 10:00:00

The Bristol Police Department is asking for the public's help in locating a missing juvenile. Denzel Pinet-Sepulveda was last seen Monday, Oct. 21, at around 2:45 p.m. when he walked away from his parent on foot.

Sepulveda is a 15-year-old 6-foot-1-inch Hispanic male weighing 250 pounds with black hair, brown eyes, and a light complexion. He was last seen wearing a dark olive-green hoodie, red plaid pajama pants, black shoes, and glasses.

A Silver Alert has also been issued. Pinet-Sepulveda is not believed to be endangered.

Anyone having information on Sepulveda's whereabouts is asked to contact the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.
