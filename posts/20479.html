Good morning, Bristol!

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20479 Published on 2022-12-05 07:00:00

Hi, all. How was your weekend? The staff of GMB went to Christmas parties and some flew away for a short vacation. Details to follow.

Weather

Today will be a great day for a walk. There will be partial sunshine with a high of 47 and the winds will be from SSW at 5-10 m.p.h.

Since some staff have abandoned the ship this week, it will be up to the rest of us to soldier on.

Party central

At the above-mentioned Christmas party, the entertainment was provided by two Bristol legends, Al Caruso and Rich Theriault. At the age of 95, Caruso still plays the keyboard like a pro. And Rich Theriault, long-time city music teacher and conductor of the Bristol Brass and Wind Ensemble, combined with his friend to cover the entire Christmas music catalogue with flair.

The food was excellent as usual, and the desserts were awesome. There were so many leftovers that there was not enough room in the fridge, so some dessert had to be stored on the back porch. A certain neighborhood resident tried to steal a cheesecake, and the police have been notified.

He claims he is innocent, but we caught him red-handed, or red-pawed, if you prefer. Here he is casing out the situation.

Caught in the act

No further evidence is needed. Let the wheels of justice turn.

19 shopping days until Christmas

Today is Dec. 5, and there are only 19 shopping days till Christmas. Please do your part to keep the economy humming.
