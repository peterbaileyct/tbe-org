Bristol Eastern boys track team falls at Plainville on Tuesday, 98-52

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12434 Published on 2022-04-13 12:06:41

By Michael Letendre

PLAINVILLE - The Bristol Eastern boys track and field team stepped up against an extremely strong squad from Plainville on Tuesday.

And the Blue Devils took the meet over a 98-52 triumph in a CCC South entanglement from Tinty Track and Field at Plainville High School.

The Lancers won two of the three relay events as Eastern dominated both the 4x800 and 4x400 races.

In the 4x800, the combination of Logan Crowley, Aidan Tartarelli, Dominic Tartarelli, and Carter Harris earned first place in 8:44.4 and then in the 4x800, Harris was a winner once again.

This time, Carter joined Axel Agustin Santiago, Edward Ansah, and Preston Poirier in the 4x400 - seizing the winning time in 3:49.34.

But Crowley and Harris weren’t finished.

Crowley nabbed first place in the 1600 (4:54) and 3200 meters (10:27) while Harris won the pole vault (8-06) and 800 meters (2:09).

In seven days…

The Lancers are back in action this coming Tuesday as Eastern travels to Meriden for a CCC South confrontation against Maloney - starting at 3 p.m.
