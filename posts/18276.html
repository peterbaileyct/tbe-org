Resident Describes What He Heard in Deadly Ambush of Bristol Police

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18276 Published on 2022-10-13 18:56:55

Ken Krawiec was playing
tennis with some friends Wednesday night at Page Park, when he heard a sudden
burst of gunfire. "The shots came quickly, pop, pop, pop, about twelve in
a row," said Krawiec. Then there was a pause of ten to fifteen seconds and
another burst of gunfire erupted, another 10-12 shots.

 

The next thing Krawiec
heard was the sound of sirens everywhere. He got in his car and followed the
parade of emergency vehicles heading to Redstone Hill Road. As he drove down
Middle Street, he came to the ESPN North campus, and in the parking lot there
already appeared to be around twenty police cars parked in the lot, with
officers everywhere. He finally got as close as the intersection of Metro
Street and Redstone, and from there everything was blocked off. While he was
watching, the State Police Crime Lab van arrived.

 

What Krawiec heard was
something we think couldn't happen in our hometown. Two young police officers
were killed and one wounded in what the state police have called a deliberate
ambush.
