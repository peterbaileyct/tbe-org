Chorale holds its bake sale fundraiser at BAIMS polling site

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53481 Published on 2024-11-05 15:15:00

Treats await voters at the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School as the Bristol Chorale has a tent outside the polling station where they offer home-baked items for sale.

A note on the Bristol Chorale website states: "Overwhelmed by the outpouring of community support and encouragement, The Bristol Chorale will once again hold a bake sale on Election Day at BAIMS. Stop by and say hello, and purchase from a wide selection of yummy treats baked by Chorale members. Volunteers will be there from 6:00 am until we run out."

Among the treats are blondes, homemade chocolate chip cookies, breads and brownies. | JoAnn Moran
