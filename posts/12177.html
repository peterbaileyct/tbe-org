Mildred Boisvert

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12177 Published on 2022-03-30 20:28:23

Mildred (Miller) Boisvert, 75, of Bristol, beloved wife of Robert A. Boisvert, died on Tuesday, March 29, at home surrounded by her family. 

Mildred was born on Oct. 21, 1946, in Bristol and was the youngest of three daughters of the late Edward and Elizabeth (Manke) Miller. A lifelong Bristol resident, she graduated from Bristol Central High School in 1963 and worked at Iseli Company. She enjoyed spending time with family, going to the casino, playing cards, and was known for her crocheting and hand stitching.

In addition to her husband of 56 years, Mildred is survived by two sons: Thomas Boisvert and fiancée, Kimberly Sugrue of Bristol, and Joseph Boisvert of Meriden; two sisters: Linda Gladu and husband, Lionel of Seven Valleys, Pa., and Patricia Gionta and husband, Andrew of Watertown; four grandchildren: Shonda Morrill and husband, Justin, Jordan McLaughlin, Joseph, and Lily Boisvert; three great-grandchildren: Cayleigh, Caleb, and Callen Morrill; and several nephews and nieces.

Funeral services and burial in Peacedale Cemetery will be private and at the convenience of the family. There are no calling hours. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Epilepsy Foundation of Connecticut, Inc., 386 Main St., Middletown, CT 06457.

Please visit Mildred’s memorial web-site at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
