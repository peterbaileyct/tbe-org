Police report shooting at Jerome Ave. and Mix St.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=12803 Published on 2022-04-28 11:40:05

Bristol Police issued the following statement Wednesday afternoon:

Bristol police received a 911 call of shots fired from occupants of one vehicle into the rear of another vehicle. The occupants were not injured. 

A very short time later, a second unrelated victim, who was following the suspect vehicle, called 911 reporting that his windshield was struck by gunfire from the same suspect vehicle. Again, no one was injured. 

The suspect vehicle is described as a silver sedan, unknown make/model. 

These two incidents both occurred in close proximity and time in the area of Jerome Ave. and Mix St. Anyone with potential video surveillance or witnesses are asked to call the Bristol police department at 860-584-3011. Suspect vehicle pictured below:
