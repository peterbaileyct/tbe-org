Pequabuck River Duck Race takes place this Sunday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17612 Published on 2022-09-28 07:00:00

The Pequabuck River Duck Race returns this year -- with a new host.

The City Arts & Culture Commission and the Parks, Recreation, Youth & Community Services Department is thrilled to be taking over this annual community event. 5,000 ducks will splash and swim in the Pequabuck River.

You can also have a chance to win over 25 prizes (the first-place prize is $1,500 cash).

Before & during the race, there will be a block party, complete with children's games, crafts, music, vendors & food.

The festivities will start on Sunday at 11 a.m., and the ducks will be released at 1 p.m. Ducks/tickets are on sale at the BPRYCS main office at 51 High St. until Friday, Sept. 30. Each ticket is $10.

If you have any questions, you can call the BPRYCS office at860-584-6160.
