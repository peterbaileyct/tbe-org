St. Paul girls explode in 4th quarter to win going away over Watertown

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21995 Published on 2023-01-18 13:39:29

St. Paul entered the 4th quarter nursing a seven point lead in what had been a close contest at Watertown Tuesday night, but the Falcons pulled away with an 18 point explosion that turned the contest into an runaway victory by a score of 57-40.

Audrey Tice and Rebecca Kelly led the Falcon attack with 22 points apiece.

Casey O’Toole led Watertown with 16, while Arellys Velez scored 11.

St. Paul improved their record to 6-4 on the season. They host Oxford Friday night in a 6:30 tip-off.
