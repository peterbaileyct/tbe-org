This year's Mum-a-thon has Halloween theme

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=36198 Published on 2023-10-26 07:00:00

This year's Mum-a-thon event included a mile-long Monster run for children, a wheelchair event, 5K walk and the 8K road race, all beginning and ending at St. Paul Catholic High School. A new course returns to a more traditional Mum-a-thon race, running through Bristol's northeast section.

This year the race had a Halloween theme, with contestants of all ages dressing for the occasion.

The Mum-A-Thon Road Race benefits Veterans Strong Community Center, an information and resource center located in Bristol. The Mum-A-Thon, which began in 1970, has been benefitting VSCC for the last five years!
