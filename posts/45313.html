BCHS' Tom Pirog pitches no-hitter in leading the Rams over Middletown Wednesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45313 Published on 2024-05-10 07:00:00

Bristol Central High School Rams' Tom Pirog tossed a no-hitter against Middletown Wednesday to lead the Rams to a 10-0 victory, improving their record to 12-3 on the season.

In picking up the win, Pirog struck out 12 and walked two Middletown batters.

Offensive Leaders for the Rams:

Aiden Lopez (2 for 3, 3 RBIs)

Joe Gratta (1 for 3, 2 RBIs)

Rocco Spirto (2 for 2). 

The Rams hosted Plainville yesterday at Muzzy Field.
