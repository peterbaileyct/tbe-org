Bristol Eastern boys and girls track teams defeat Maloney on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43807 Published on 2024-04-11 07:00:00

Both Bristol Eastern Track teams earned win number one on the season Tuesday at Maloney. The boys turned in a dominant performance in a 92-49 victory.

And the Eastern girls were also victorious in a much closer meet, winning by a score of 79.5 to 69.5.

Below were the victorious performances for both teams.

Boys Track and Field

Bristol Eastern: 92

Maloney: 49

Cole Beland: 1st place in the 400m (53.9), 800 (2:26), 4x800, and 4x400

Jase Koczur: 1st place in the 1600m (5:08), 3200m (11:51), 4x800, and 4x400

Jacob Kingsley: 1st Place in 110 Hurdles (17.8)

Jonathan Casarola: 1st Place in 300 Hurdles (55.5), 4x800, and 4x400

Lincoln Bashaw: 1st in Shot Put (39-02)

Isaiah Lawrence-Bynum: 1st place in Triple Jump (41-01.5)

Jacob Rogers: 1st Place in Pole Vault (9-06) and Javelin (120-09)

4x400 (Cole Beland, Jonathan Casarola, Kobe Senethong, Jase Koczur): 1st place (3:51)

4x800 (Jase Koczur, Cole Beland, Kyle Kelley, Jonathan Casarola): 1st place (9:25)

Bristol Eastern boys move to 1-1 on the season and are back in action next week Tuesday against Lewis Mills!

Girls Track and Field

Bristol Eastern: 79.5

Maloney: 69.5

Princess Priscilla Dilone: 1st place in the 100m (13.1), 200m (27.2), and 4x400

Kaelyn Gonzalez: 1st Place in the 800 (2:46), 4x800, and 4x400

Amanda Noel: 1st Place in the 300 Hurdles (51.4)

Ryan Braccia: 1st place in High Jump (5-02)

Marjorie Moliengo: 1st place in Pole Vault (7-06)

4x400 (Kaelyn Gonzalez, Punya Mohan, Madison Dixon, Princess Priscilla Dilone): 1st place (4:27)

4x800 (Kaelyn Gonzalez, Punya Mohan, Lauren Ayotte, Calliope Gallagher): 1st place (11:24)

Bristol Eastern girls are 1-1 on the season and back in action next week Tuesday against Lewis Mills!
