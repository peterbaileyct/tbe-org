Lake Compounce to hold drone extravaganza this weekend

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50248 Published on 2024-08-31 10:30:00

A dazzling display of more than 300 drones will fill the skies over Lake Compounce over this Labor Day weekend, the park has announced in a recent release.

The historic park will hold its first-ever drone show offering the drone extravanganza where hundreds of drones will create unique designs mimicking "beloved park staples" beginning at 8 p.m. on both Saturday, Aug. 31 and Sunday, Sept. 1.

Hundreds of drones will fill the sky over Lake Compounce this weekend as part of a drone extravaganza. | Firefly Drone Shows

The theme park and water park will be open all weekend long with the last chance for guests to splash in Crocodile Cove this Saturday. Aug. 31 – Monday, Sept. 2 from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.

In addition, the park is offering special discounts available online, and is planning its Phanton Fall Fest. Click here for more information.
