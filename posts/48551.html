World War II veterans to be honored at opening of Bristol Blues game this evening at Muzzy Field

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48551 Published on 2024-07-19 16:44:31

World War II veterans Joe Caminiti and Dick Fitz will be honored at the start of tonight's Bristol Blues game at Muzzy Field.

The game begins at 6:30 p.m. The Blues will face the North Adams, Mass.
