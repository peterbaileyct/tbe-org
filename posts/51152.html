Local POW/MIA Remembrance Day ceremony to be held on Memorial Boulevard Friday morning

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51152 Published on 2024-09-19 07:00:00

A local POW/MIA Remembrance Day Ceremony will be held at the new POW/MIA monument on Veterans Memorial Boulevard and Park Friday, Sept. 20, at 11 am.

The ceremony, in conjunction with National POW/MIA Remembrance Day, is being hosted by American Legion, Post 2 with assistance from the Bristol Veterans Council.

Among those being honored are Edward Riccio who was a POW for a short time and the brother of Bob Barnet who was killed in a POW Camp during the Korean War.

The ceremony is open to the public.
