World AIDS Day commemorated Wednesday at City Hall

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9710 Published on 2021-12-03 14:46:16

World AIDS Day was commemorated on Wednesday evening at City Hall.


Mayor Jeff Caggiano and Alice Ferguson, chairperson of the Mayor’s Task Force on HIV/AIDS/HCV, both shared thoughts and memories of those gone before and of hope for the future.

Members of the Bristol Mayor’s Task force on HIV/AIDS/HCV and guests also attended.
