Farmers market today from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m., adds new sponsor to this year's list

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48819 Published on 2024-07-27 07:00:00

Bernie's Frame Shop is the latest sponsor of the Bristol Farmers Market, the nonprofit that runs the seasonal farmers market announced on Facebook.

This week's Bristol Farmers Market will be held from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. at the 10 Main St. parking lot.

Sponsors help to fund marketing, family-friendly entertainment and cooking demonstrations among other events.

Vendors at each week’s market include Better Baking by Beth, Continuum Distilling, Chicken Frosty, Gresczyk Farms, CBG Gurus, Amazing Love, Soothing Ways, Ridge Runner Soaps, the Ancient Flavors, Perkins Pure Maple Sugar House, Pure Goodness Bakery, Tonn’s Marketplace and Twin Pines Farm.

The venue features free musical entertainment, accepts Senior Farmers Market Nutrition Vouchers and WIC as well as Double Snap and EBT.
