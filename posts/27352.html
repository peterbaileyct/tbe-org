Good morning, Bristol!

Seeing the crowd gathered at the new pickle ball courts at Seymour Park, the staff of TBE decided to give the game a try, and we are writing this while awaiting our x-ray results. Words of advice -- don’t dive on a concrete court to reach a shot whizzing by you.

Tuesday’s weather

Patchy fog with a chance of showers in the morning. Partly sunny with a slight chance of showers in the afternoon. Highs in the mid 70’s. Winds from the west at 5 m.p.h. Chance of rain -- 40 percent.

Tuesday events

If you are having trouble sleeping, you can catch tonight’s City Council meeting on zoom. It begins at 7 p.m.

If you have any idea where the Upper Valley Nighthawks are from, you can travel to see the Bristol Blues tonight. If you’re not back by midnight, we will organize a search party.

This date in history

  • On June 13, 1777, the Marquis de Lafayette lands in the U.S. (Within two years, the entire Continental Army is fluent in French.)
  • In 1913, the Yankees win their 13th game of year after losing 36 games. (Then the Red Sox gave them Babe Ruth.)
  • Comedian Tim Allen was born on this date in 1953.
