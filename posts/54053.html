Holiday Tea Party at the clock museum slated for December 8

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54053 Published on 2024-11-18 09:34:29

Teas the Season: A Holiday Tea, will be held at the American Clock & Watch Museum on Sunday, December 8th from 3:00-5:00.

Held in conjunction with the museum's annual “Mantels for the Season,” the event is planned as a great way for attendees to relax with friends over a delightfully elegant afternoon tea, while viewing the beautiful mantel displays.

Unlike the museum's annual New Year's Madhatter tea for children that is held to usher in the New Year, this event is for adults looking for an extra-special way to usher in the holiday season.

Catered by Cravings of Bristol, the menu will include traditional tea sandwiches, scones, desserts, and tea, of course!

In addition to the festive decorations and delightfully delicious tea, there will be a teacup drawing and the museum shop will be filled with unique gifts, to help guests get some shopping done, too!

Tickets to A Holiday Tea are $50 per person, ages 12+. They can be purchased by calling the museum—860-583-6070--or by stopping by the museum, Wednesday through Sunday, 10-5. Please note: Due to the nature of the event, we regret that we will not be able to accommodate food allergies. Tickets to the event are not refundable.
