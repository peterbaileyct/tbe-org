Mum Festival auto show, a treat

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51602 Published on 2024-09-30 07:00:00

The Mum Festival Auto Show drew a number of classic vehicles to the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School on Memorial Boulevard during this year's Mum Festival celebration.

The show was held Saturday, Sept. 28, and the classic cars, several of which are presented here in photos by Ron Tessman, provide a sampling of the variety and quality of this year's entrants
