BE girls track team drops close decision to Maloney on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25242 Published on 2023-04-23 20:29:10

By Michael Letendre  
 

BRISTOL - The Maloney girls track and field team did well against Bristol Eastern last Tuesday from Alumni Field in Bristol. 

In a CCC South entanglement against the Lancers, the locals hung around against the Spartans but in the end, Maloney came away with a 90-57 victory. 

Four athletes from Eastern were able to tally first place finishes against Maloney. 

Princess Priscilla Dilone did extremely well over the short sprinting events for Eastern. 

In the 100 meters, Dilone snared the win in 13.64 seconds and in the 200 meters, she was a winner again, this time finishing in 27.82 seconds.  

Also for the Lancers, Julia Armington won the 400 meters in 64.9 seconds.

Next Tuesday, Eastern has its final home meet in April as the Lancers host Lewis Mills in CCC South action. 

Meet time is 3:45 p.m. from Alumni Field.
