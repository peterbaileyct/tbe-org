Small but mighty girls track & field squad from Bristol Central hangs in against Maloney but ultimately falls 94-55 in CCC South meet from Meriden

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25476 Published on 2023-04-27 18:20:11

By Michael Letendre  

MERIDEN – Bristol Central’s girls track and field program had several athletes perform well at Maloney in a CCC South attraction on Tuesday afternoon but by the completion of the meet, the Spartans were able to wrangle up a 94-55 decision in Meriden to stave off Central in the end.  

The Rams won five events that day which included victories in the 4x100 and 4x400 (4:19) meter relays.  

Central also had success in the 400 meter race as Lynae Lewis nabbed first place with a finish of 1:01.8.
In the long jump, Kylee Barnes successfully cleared 16 feet, four inches while the pole vault event saw Rayiah Reinhard nab the win with a make of seven feet even.  

Looking to get back to its winning ways, Central battles Plainville next Tuesday at 3:45 p.m. from the track and field at BCHS in a CCC South tilt.  

The meet commences at 3:45 p.m.
