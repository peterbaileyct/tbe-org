Bristol Central finishes CCC South with 14-0 record after great showing at CCC Championship meet on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45992 Published on 2024-05-23 07:00:00

After a long and very warm day at Willowbrook Park on Tuesday, the Bristol Central Boys Track and Field Team completed an undefeated season in the CCC South, including their 7-0 regular season.

In New Britain, BC won the South Division at the CCC Championships yesterday to finish the season 14-0.

The Rams were also 4th overall in the entire CCC, one of the toughest conferences in the state! All results can be found on athletic.net. Here are some highlights for Bristol Central:

  • 4x800 CCC Champions and All-Conference in the blazing time of 8:01.48

Rowan Hennessey, Julieen Lopez, Patrick Wininger, Isaiah Fabrizio

  • 4x400 CCC South Champions, All-Conference, 6th CCC
