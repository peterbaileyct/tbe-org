Hydrant flushing continues on Redstone Hill Road and surrounding area

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=42364 Published on 2024-03-11 08:08:26

The Bristol Water and Sewer Department will continue hydrant flushing for 2024 today, with activity ending at 2:30 p.m. in the following areas:

  • Redstone Hill Road
  • Vincent P. Kelly
  • Commerce Drive
  • Benham Street
  • Emmett Street Broderich Road
  • and surrounding areas.

Low pressure is to be expected during flushing and for short periods afterwards.

The Water and Sewer Department recommends putting off washing clothes, and after the flushing ends, to run cold water in an outside faucet or bathtub until the water runs clear.
