Bristol Central to hold Powder Puff football fundraiser for Make-A-Wish Friday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54114 Published on 2024-11-20 07:00:00


This Friday, November 22, from 6-8 p.m. on the turf at Bristol Central, the 26th annual Powder Puff Fundraiser to benefit the CT Make-A-Wish Foundation will be held. Tickets are $5 to enter. There will be Dunkin' Donuts, coffee and hot cocoa on sale for $1. Any extra donation is gladly accepted.

In last year's 25th annual Powder Puff Fundraiser, $2,000 was raised. That brought BC’s lifetime total to $50,000 raised...that is the equivalent of granting five life-changing wishes for local CT children. What an incredible accomplishment!
