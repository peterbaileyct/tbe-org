2024 Rockwell Festival today from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49683 Published on 2024-08-17 07:00:00

This year's Rockwell Festival will feature almost 200 vendors 14 food booths, the Kids Zone and Art District, according to the West End Association vice president.

“We have our mix of civic organizations, nonprofits, crafters, retailers, 14 food booths, the Kids Zone, Art District, car show and pie eating contest,” Jesse Jablon said.

He said there are over 35 booths that are first-time vendors this year.

The festival is also expanding is school supply give-away program. Details are still being worked out, he said, but two new sponsors have come on board — Price Chopper and Liberty Bank.
