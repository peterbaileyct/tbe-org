Bristol Police investigating Sunday morning shooting incident

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11703 Published on 2022-03-07 09:17:02

At 1:00 a.m. Sunday morning, Bristol police responded to a call involving a shooting at Spare Time Bowling on Farmington Avenue.

It was determined that a fight that began between two females inside the establishment continued out in the parking lot and grew into a larger disturbance between two groups. During the incident a shot was fired.

The two groups then left the area in two separate cars, a black Honda and a gray Hyundai. More shots were then fired as one car tried to cut off the other.

Shell casings and other evidence were collected at the scene. There was no indication that anyone was injured in the incident.

Police are asking for assistance from anyone with knowledge of this incident. Please call The Bristol Police Department at 860-584-3011, the Anonymous Tips Line at 860-585-8477, or email BPDCrimeWatch@BristolCT.gov.
