Good morning, Bristol!

"Staff" is under the weather this morning, so his second cousin, “Stuff” is handling this morning’s greetings.

Where to start?

Rumor has it they are digging a big hole at Centre Square. Supposedly, the plan is to fill the hole with the bodies of all politicians who promised to get something done there over the years.

Shamrock Run

The Shamrock Run is scheduled to go off on March 18. “Stuff” has been training hard for the big day. So far, he is up to a 150-yard stroll, followed by a six-yard sprint to the refrigerator. His confidence is sky high, as is his belt size.

“Stuff” is sure new projects are being planned. He anxiously awaits the big announcements.

So far

So far, the Red Sox are undefeated in Spring Training. Reserve your spot now for the big Duckboat parade in November!

Enough

Enough “stuff.” Have a beautiful day, Bristol.
