Paving to start the next three weeks on Memorial Boulevard and Riverside Avenue

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16556 Published on 2022-08-31 11:22:43

Mayor Caggiano recently announced on his Facebook page that milling and paving will take place the next three weeks on both the Boulevard and Rte. 72 (Riverside Avenue).

The work being done and the schedule are explained below.
