Main Street Community Foundation seeking scholarship applicants

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11030 Published on 2022-02-02 14:28:14

The Main Street Community Foundation is now accepting scholarship applications for the 2022-2023 academic year. A full list of scholarships available, their eligibility criteria and links to the online applications are available on the Foundation website, www.mainstreetfoundation.org.

The deadline for all scholarship applications is March 11, 2022.

A total of $275,000 in scholarships will be awarded from 80 different funds this year. These scholarships are available to graduating seniors, current college students and adults returning to school who are or will be attending two or four year colleges as well as technical schools and career certification programs. There are also a few scholarships available for students attending elementary and secondary private schools in Bristol.

Each scholarship has unique eligibility criteria established by the donor. Donors are individuals, families, businesses or other organizations that seek to assist students in pursuing their education and career goals and dreams; they have a belief that helping students ease the financial obligations of education is an investment in the future.

Check out the Scholarship FAQs page on the Foundation’s website or contact Samantha Rajotte, Scholarship & Program Officer, at samantha@mainstreetfoundation.org or 860.583.6363 to request more information.
