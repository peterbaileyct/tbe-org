The Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce are combining their annual Health Fair, Business Expo and Career fair into one all-inclusive event -- "The Everything Expo."

"We absolutely love being able to offer up an opportunity for the public to come and explore their health options, business services or job opportunities and at the same time also showcase the amazing business we have here in the region," said Cindy Bombard in a press release announcing the expo.

Bombard is the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce President & CEO.

The Everything Expo will be held March 28 at the DoubleTree by Hilton from 2 to 7 p.m. Attendance is free to the public, and those who attend will have a chance to enter to win two roundtrip tickets from Hartford to Dublin, Ireland, from Aer Lingus.

It is the goal of the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce to assist in making the connections needed to improve the quality of life for area residents and to continue to support business growth.

For more information on the Central Connecticut Chambers of Commerce, visit www.CentralCTChambers.org, or call 860-584-4718.
