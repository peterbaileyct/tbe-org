Bristol Sports Hall of Fame 25th Anniversary Induction Dinner will be held on November 19

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9042 Published on 2021-11-02 14:50:45


The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame will be holding its 25th Anniversary Induction program on Friday, Nov. 19 at the DoubleTree in Bristol.

Ceremonies start at 7 p.m. with registration beginning at 6 p.m.

This year the Hall of Fame will be honoring the Class of 2020 which includes Jim Danis, John Dauphinee, Todd Hasler, Dr. Albert Jabs, Hank Lodge, Karin Przygocki, Stacy Mills Rivoira, and Tony Savino(deceased).

Tickets are $40 and can be puchased by contacting Pat Parente at 860-919-8087 or online at bshof.org. 

No tickets will be sold at the door.
