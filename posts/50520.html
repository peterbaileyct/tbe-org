Fall leaf bag collection to begin Oct. 7

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50520 Published on 2024-09-06 12:30:00

The city's fall leaf bag collection will begin Monday, Oct. 7 and continue through Friday, Nov. 29, Bristol Public Works announced earlier this week.

The following guidelines apply:

  • Leaf bags must be placed curbside and not within the residence’s property for proper collection.
  • Loose leaf piles will not be collected.
  • Leaves are only collected in brown, recyclable, paper leaf bags that are available at most home and garden stores.
  • There is no maximum limit to leaf bag collection at a residence.
  • Place bags at the curb as soon as they are full, as there is no specific pick-up day.
