CT election results will be available Tuesday night at ctmirror.org

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53457 Published on 2024-11-05 10:30:00

by Stephen Busemeyer November 5, 2024 @ 4:57 am

The Connecticut Mirror will provide live town-by-town election results Tuesday night for all of the state House and Senate seats, as well as results for the U.S. House races and for U.S. Senate.

The CT Mirror will also have town-by-town results for the presidential election and for the constitutional amendment on absentee voting.

All of the results will be provided by the Associated Press. They will automatically update on the CT Mirror website as they become available after the polls close.

The CT Mirror’s reporters will provide live news and analysis from key races and locations throughout the day.

Read all of CT Mirror’s coverage of the 2024 election campaigns here.

The CT Mirror’s Voter Guide offers details on candidates, their platforms, district representation and more.

Results will appear here after polls close.
