Speaking of radio

In 1952, Brooklyn Dodgers games were broadcast on Bristol's WBIS. That year the Dodgers certainly "tore up the pea patch" going 95-57-2, capturing the NL pennant. But "Da Bums" lived up to their reputation and dropped the Series in seven to the Yanks.

It happened

In 1971, Mayor Longo threatened a photographer and forbade the WBIS news director from attending a press conference. Imagine that? Can't imagine such silliness going on today. Can you?
