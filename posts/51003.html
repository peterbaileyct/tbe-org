Bristol Central boys cross country team takes 5th place in the Harry Geraghty Invitational on Friday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51003 Published on 2024-09-16 16:40:24

The boys from Bristol Central placed fifth on Friday in the Harry Geraghty Invitational. The Rams were paced by Noah Pellerin, who finished 16th in a strong field with a time of 19:14.37.

Emersyn Mazzei ran a great race in the girls competition with a 10th place finish in a time of 22:24.

And AJ Olivero Mulligan had an fantastic 2nd place finish in the Boys JV bracket in 20:31.
