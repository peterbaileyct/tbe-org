Wednesday Little League regionals update

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=15757 Published on 2022-08-10 09:00:00

On Wednesday, in the New England Region bracket, Massachusetts faces New Hampshire at 1 p.m. The winner will face Maine in the region finals on Thursday, Aug. 11, at 5 p.m.

On Thursday, in the Mid-Atlantic bracket, Washington, D.C., will face Pennsylvania. The winner will square off against Delaware in the regional finals on Friday, Aug. 12, at 3 p.m.

In Tuesday's, Mid-Atlantic contest Delaware defeated Pennsylvania, 5-4.

Also on Wednesday, In the Metro Region bracket, New Jersey will face Connecticut, who lost to New York (7-5) on Monday in a game that had two rain delays. The winner will play New York in the Metro Region final on Friday, Aug. 12, at 7 p.m.

All games are broadcast on ESPN.
