City announces holiday closures for Christmas

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21101 Published on 2022-12-22 07:00:00

The City of Bristol has announced that City Hall will be operating on a half-day schedule on Friday, December 23, 2022 in observance of Christmas Eve. Most departments* will be open from 8:30 a.m. to 12:15 p.m.Â 

*The Public Works Department, Building Department, and Comptroller's Office will be open from 8:00 a.m. to 11:45 a.m. on Friday, December 23rd. 

The Bristol Transfer Station will be open and operating under regular hours (7:15 a.m. to 2:45 p.m.) on Friday, December 23rd. The Transfer Station will be closed on Saturday, December 24th.

All City Hall Departments and the Bristol Transfer Station will be closed on Monday, December 26, 2022 in observance of Christmas Day. 

There are no service schedule changes. All curbside collections (rubbish, recycling) will be completed on their regularly scheduled days.
