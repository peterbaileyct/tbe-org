Eastern wrestlers get huge win over Berlin in contest that goes down to final match, hang on for 34-33 decision

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=40832 Published on 2024-01-28 18:23:43

Bristol Eastern outlasted Berlin last Wednesday in a match between the last two undefeated teams in the CCC South. The Lancers won the seat-squirmer by a single point, 34-33, and emerged with the lead in the division.

Yusuf Bah pinned his opponent to put the Lancers ahead 34-30 with one match remaining.

Berlin’s Collin Sullivan then tried desperately to pin Eastern’s Andrew Brown, but Brown was able to stay off his back despite a close call with about twenty seconds remaining, and the Lancers emerged with the one-point win.

Besides Bah, Eastern wrestlers Luke Viens, Rocco Barbino, and Owen Broyles also pinned their opponent.

Gabe White got a major decision for Eastern worth five points, and Soph Um and Rio Fernandez won decisions in their matches.
