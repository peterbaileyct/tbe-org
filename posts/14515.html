Registrars start sending polling location changes July 1

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14515 Published on 2022-07-01 08:05:00

The Registrars of Voters will begin mailing notifications to some residents whose polling place has changed as a result of redistricting. Redistricting takes place every 10 years based on the results of the census.

If a resident's polling place has changed, the resident will receive an informational letter and magnet with their new polling location and precinct. The mailing should be completed by July 18.  

Contact the Registrars of Voters Office with any questions, 860-584-6165.
