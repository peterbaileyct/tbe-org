Bristol Hospital announces no-visitation policy beginning Dec. 31

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10138 Published on 2021-12-30 19:31:19

Due to the continued spike in COVID cases across the state, Bristol Health is taking precautionary measures to keep its patients and staff safe by implementing its no visitation policy, which will go into effect on Friday, Dec. 31st. 

"Due to the increasing numbers of COVID-19 cases in our communities, we are revising our visitation policy," said Donna Morris, Bristol Health's Manager of Infection Prevention and Regulatory Programs. "In an abundance of caution to keep our patients, staff and community members safe, we are limiting visitation and access to the hospital to those considered essential to operations and those requiring hospital services and care."

The policy restricts visitation for hospital inpatients, the Emergency Department and outpatient procedures at Bristol Hospital. Exceptions to the policy can be granted for humanitarian reasons and will be on a case-by-case basis.

To read the full No Visitation Policy, visit bristolhealth.org/visitation.
