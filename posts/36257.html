Spooktacular at BAIMS field is a Halloween treat

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=36257 Published on 2023-10-26 22:36:04

A Mummy Special Effects Makeup demo, stilt walkers, over 30 vendors, face painters and a Witch's Brew Tent with fall beverages are among the offerings at the Halloween Spooktacular at the field at Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School from 3 to 10 p.m. Saturday.

A pumpkin carving contest encourages participants to bring their best carved pumpkin and a battery powered light to illuminate the carving to the Bristol Arts and Culture booth upon arrival. The winner will be announced at 8:30 p.m. Winners will be displayed at the Bristol Parks Recreation Youth and Community Services building on High St.

While separate from the Spooktacular, tickets are still available for Downtown Live's Rocky Horror Picture Show with live shadow-cast by RKO Army, in the Rockwell Theater. The pre-show begins at 6:15 p.m.

Bristol Parks Recreation, Youth and Community Services, sponsor of the Spooktacular will provide candy to each vendor and a bag for anyone who wants to trick-or-treat. Vendors will include food trucks, hot cocoa and coffee baristas, delectable treats, face painters, hands-on activities and arts and crafts!
