Bristol Central boys track soundly defeats rival Bristol Eastern, 117-33

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45596 Published on 2024-05-16 07:00:00

Bristol Central’s boys track and field team had a great day on Tuesday by dominating most events to defeat Eastern 117-33 to finish the regular season with a record of 7-0.

Bristol Eastern boys move to (4-3) on the season.

The next event for both teams is the CCC Championship next Tuesday.

The Rams are virtually certain to win the South Division outright and will get to see how they stack up against some very tough CCC teams heading into the Class L Championship!
