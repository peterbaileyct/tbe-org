Anna D. Grossi, 98

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49557 Published on 2024-08-15 07:00:00

Anna D. Grossi, 98, lifelong resident of Bristol, passed away on Monday, Aug. 12. She was born on June 19, 1926, daughter of the late Donato and Palma (Lanuzza) Decrisantis.

Anna was the loving wife of the late John Grossi whom passed in 2013. Together Anna and John would enjoy boating every weekend. She also enjoyed sewing and spending time with her family.

Anna is survived by her daughter Paula Pompei and her husband Joseph; grandson Jospeh Pompei; great-grandsons Anthony and Landen Pompei and her sister Lucy LaMonico.

Besides her husband and parents, Anna is predeceased by a grandson Jay Pampei and sister Domenica Doty.

Services will be held privately.

To leave an online message of condolences, please visit Anna’s tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
