Ann Marie Albert, 94

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=21565 Published on 2023-01-06 07:00:00

Ann Marie (Soucy) Albert, 94, of Bristol, widow of Gerard A. Albert, died on Saturday, Dec. 31, 2022, at Ingraham Manor.

Ann Marie was born on June 3, 1928, in Van Buren, Maine, and was one of nine children of the late Arthur and Jeanne (Bosse) Soucy. She was a longtime Terryville resident where she worked for O-Z/Gedney Company before moving to Bristol in 1999.

She was a parishioner of St. Gregory the Great Parish in Bristol.

Ann Marie is survived by five children: Paul Albert of Torrington, Cindy Bosse of New Britain, Gary Albert of Huddleston, Va., Mark and Jeannine Albert of Greenville, S.C., and Lori Borysewicz and husband, Anthony of Bristol; a brother: Philip Soucy of Cary, N.C.; six grandchildren; seven step-grandchildren; nine great-grandchildren; 20 step-great-grandchildren; and many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by seven siblings.

Family and friends are invited to gather on Monday, Jan. 9, between 9:30 and 10:30 a.m. at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Avenue, Bristol.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at 11 a.m. at St. Gregory the Great Church, 235 Maltby St., Bristol. Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.

The family has arranged Mulligan Grill at Pequabuck Golf Club for the lunch after. Please visit Ann Marie’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
