BC Cross Country opens the season with a good showing at Stratton Brook Invitational

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50667 Published on 2024-09-09 13:11:54

Bristol Central’s Cross Country teams had a good first day on the course at the Stratton Brook Invitational in Simsbury on Saturday.

This will be a building year for Central with a young team. In fact the girl’s team is so young that only one girl ran in the varsity race.

Both the boys and girls teams are led by sophomores. The boys team all ran personal records on the course at Stratton Brook and sophomore Emersyn Mazzei had a super strong first showing for the girls.
