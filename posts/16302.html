Josephine Goggi, 99

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16302 Published on 2022-08-23 16:00:00

Josephine (Leone) Goggi, 99, wife of the late George L. Goggi ,Sr., passed away peacefully on Sunday, Aug. 21, at home. She was a lifelong resident of Bristol, born on July 4, 1923, and was one of six daughters and a son born to the late Anthony and Antoinette (Gianotti) Leone.

She was predeceased by her siblings, brother John Leone Sr. and her sisters Angie Willis, Connie Trelli, Sadie Segretario, Rose Parent, and Mary Hummelman. Her life, like the memorable day she was born on, will be remembered not only for what she did with her family and friends as well as those she never met.

She will be best remembered for her homemade fudge and pizzelles during the holidays, and especially her knitting and crocheting. Over the years she gave many of her handmade creations of baby blankets, winter hats, gloves, scarves and potholders to family, friends and strangers, donating 25 newborn hats in 2018 to the Pediatric Ward at Bristol Hospital.

She is survived by her son, George L. Goggi, Jr., as well as many nieces, nephews, family as well as and the friends she made during her 99 years she shared her long life and talents with.  

There will be no calling hours. A Memorial Mass will be held on Monday, Aug. 29, at 10 a.m. at St. Anthony Church followed by a burial at St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.

To leave an online condolence, please visit Josephine's Memorial here at http://www.Obrien-FuneralHome.com.
