All are welcome -- Saturday at this year's Mum Festival in pictures

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51654 Published on 2024-09-30 15:30:00

Bristol photographer Ron Tessman of BristolCT.net spent Mum Festival weekend slinging his Nikon camera from event to event and capturing some photos for the ages--all ages including one of grandpa lending a hand with some arts and crafts, another of that ride on a horse--or a hay wagon, how about that petting zoo and what about a photo with some pirates.

Well, if we try this, it might work, perhaps? | Ron Tessman

And then, perhaps, this is under control, when it comes to riding a four-legged friend, but then why not let someone else do the driving and what is a bunch of goats called, anyhow? | Ron Tessman

Between the slide and lines, someone has to decide what's next. | Ron Tessman

Arrr, matey, smile for the camera; a little music on a perfect night while the crowds ebb and flow with more and more people. | Ron Tessman
