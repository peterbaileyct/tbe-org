West Hartford shuts out Bristol Legion on Monday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47348 Published on 2024-06-18 18:51:40

Not much went right for the Bristol American Legion baseball team Monday night when it travelled to West Hartford. For the second game in a row, the bats were silent, and the problems were compounded when West Hartford pounded out eleven hits and scored the same number of runs.

West Hartford, the defending state champions, appears to have another strong outfit this year, and have jumped out to an early first place position in Zone 1 with a 3-0 record.

Bristol was held to three hits and Connor Cyr collected two of them, while Brayden Schenck got the other.

West Hartford had four doubles and two triples in their dominant performance.

Bristol drops to 4-2 in Zone 1. They next play on Wednesday, hosting Meriden at Muzzy Field in a 7:45 start.
