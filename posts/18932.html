Page Park

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18932 Published on 2024-11-20 09:47:40

On Wednesday, October 12, Dr. Josh Medeiros of the Bristol Park Department gave an update on the big Page Park restoration project which is projected to be completed in stages over the next four years. In a zoom presentation lasting about an hour, Medeiros covered all the details of the improvements, including a tentative time schedule.
