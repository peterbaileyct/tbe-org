American Clock & Watch Museum announces Mad Hatter's Tea Party for Aug. 27

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16271 Published on 2022-08-23 11:20:56

Join the fun at the American Clock & Watch Museum on Saturday, Aug. 27 from 2 to 4 p.m. for a children's Mad Hatter's tea party.

There will be crafts, and snacks, and tea, of course!  The ticket price for the event is the same for adults and children--$5 per person. Seating is limited.  Pre-registration is required.  Call the museum to reserve your child's spot at the tea table--860-583-6070.   

Information is also available on the website  www.clockandwatchmuseum.org. This event is sponsored by the Bristol Rotary Club.
