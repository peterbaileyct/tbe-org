Hydrant flushing moves to Stafford Avenue area

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51349 Published on 2024-09-23 07:00:00

Starting today, Monday, Sept. 23, the Bristol Water and Sewer Department will continue its 2024 hydrant flushing in the following areas:

  • Country Lane
  • Boulder Drive
  • Stafford Avenue
  • Driftwood Road
  • Dogwood Lane
  • Beechwood Lane
  • Old Farm Road
  • Carriage Road
  • Plank Hill Road
  • Lufkin Lane
  • Strawberry Hill Road
  • Cherry Hill Drive
  • Stevens Street, and
  • surrounding areas.

Activity will begin at 7 a.m. and end around 2:30 p.m.

Low pressure and discolored water is to be expected during flushing and for short periods afterwards. The Water and Sewer Department recommends refraining from washing clothes during this time. Afterwards, the department recommends running an outside faucet or cold water in a bathtub until the water runs clear.

The activity will continue until the next area is posted. Refer to http://www.bristolct.gov//CivicAlerts.aspx?AID=1493.
