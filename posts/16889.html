Bristol Trivia Answer:

Rac Slider was the first manager of the Bristol Red Sox.

His '73 BriSox team posted a record of 62-77.

Slider managed in the Red Sox farm system for 21 consecutive seasons from 1965-1985, including eight straight seasons in Winter Haven, Fla., with the Sox Single A affiliate.

He eventually made it to Boston in 1986 as their bullpen coach.
