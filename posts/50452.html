Eastern volleyball opens season Tuesday with loss to South Windsor

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=50452 Published on 2024-09-05 06:54:55

Bristol Eastern’s volleyball team got off to a good start in their season-opening match with South Windsor, winning the opening set 25-21.

After the first set, however, the Bobcats took control, winning three straight sets by scores of 25-17, 25-12, and 25-12.

South Windsor’s Kailie Girard posted 9 kills and three digs, while Katie Bullock had twenty one assists, four aces and five digs.

For Eastern, Jamayrie McRae Arroyo had seven kills and three digs, and Kamila Mateo-Rivera added nine kills, seven digs and an ace.

South Windsor 3, Bristol Eastern 1
(at Bristol Eastern)
Game scores: BE-SW 25-21, 17-25, 12-25, 12-25


Records: SW 1-0; BE 0-1
