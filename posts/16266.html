Bristol crowns 2022 Pony League baseball champions

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16266 Published on 2022-08-23 11:05:39

The City of Bristol Department of Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services wrapped up the 2022 Pony League season last week. The Grizzlies defeated the Eagles under the bright lights at Muzzy Field to take home the 2022 Pony League championship. The Grizzlies (3-seed) battled in the single elimination tournament, taking down the 6-seed, the 2-seed, and ultimately the 1-seed to win this year's title.

The Grizzlies were led by pitcher Noah Molinsky, who went the distance for the Grizzlies in the title game, surrendering just 4 hits and striking out 9 in a complete-game effort. 

Additionally, the following Scott/McCooey Pony League players were recognized by their coaches and
league directors for their accomplishments on the field this season.


 Don Titus Rookie of the Year: Hunter Dudzikowski
 Jim Kilduff Gold Glove Award: Alexander Maldonado
 George Scott Cy Young Award: Jonathan Montgomery
 Bill Glines MVP Award: Gabriel White
 William McCooey Sportsmanship Award: Matthew Harkey
 Jim Kilduff Gold Glove Award: Tyler Ryan
 Jim Kilduff Gold Glove Award: Richard Montanez
 Monahan Silver Slugger Award: Caleb Molinsky
 Jim Kilduff Gold Glove Award: Adam Newton

For more information, or to get involved please contact League Director Shawn Mirmina at
shawnmirmina@bristolk12.org or visit www.BristolRec.com.
