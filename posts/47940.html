Tonight's Rockin' Out at Rockwell summer concert features Billy Joel tribute band

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47940 Published on 2024-07-02 12:00:00

Tonight's Rockin' Out at Rockwell summer concert features River of Dreams, a Billy Joel tribute band, from 6:30 to 8 p.m. at Rockwell amphitheater.

Attendees are encouraged to bring family and friends, blankets and chairs, and to grab dinner at one of the food trucks, organized by the West End Association. For this evening, Dem Boyz and Greer's Cluck food trucks will be on hand, along with Rich Farm Ice Cream.

The 2024 concert series is sponsored by the West End Association, Xfinity, Thomaston Savings Bank and RV Enterprise LLC.

For updates, visit BristolRec.com.
