Railroad crossing on Center Street to be closed Tuesday morning through Wednesday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=43316 Published on 2024-04-01 12:30:00

Center Street from North Main Street to Summer Street will be closed from 7:30 a.m. Tuesday, April 2, to 4:30 p.m. Wednesday, April 3, while a construction project is underway at the railroad crossing, according to a release from the Bristol Police Dept.

Police officers will be present during hours of construction, including overnight.

Motorists are being asked to find an alternative route during this closure. The detour for Center Street, east of the construction area, can be accessed from either Federal Street or Main Street.

Access to the businesses, factories and Prospect Methodist Church will be available on Center Street by Summer Street.

The construction project is a joint effort of Bristol Public Works and Berkshire and Eastern Railroad.
