Bristol Middle School Cross Country Champions were crowned on Wednesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53583 Published on 2024-11-07 09:13:26

The Bristol Middle School Cross Country Championships were held at Page Park on Wednesday.

It was a great event with runners from five schools participating.

Boys Two Mile Race

In the Boys race, Michael Bastiaanse Of West Bristol was the top finisher with a time of 13:11.80.

He was followed Thomas Zabawa, who was only two seconds behind at 13:13.12. Alexander Szczygiel finished third at 13:57.27.
