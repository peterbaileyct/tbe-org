Bristol Eastern cruises to volleyball win over Middletown Friday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18371 Published on 2022-10-15 10:32:41

After several matches against some of the best volleyball powers in Connecticut, the Bristol Eastern volleyball team got a bit of a breather as they easily defeated an overmatched Middletown group Friday night.

The Lancers won in three straight sets, 25-10, 25-8, and 25-3.

Eastern coach Stefanie Reay was able to put many different combinations on the floor as every member of the Lancer team got a chance to play.

Eastern was paced by Emily Valentine, who served up 12 aces and had 5 digs. Emma Cratella had an ace, a kill, and a dig. And Jamayrie McRae-Arroyo chipped in with a kill and a dig.
