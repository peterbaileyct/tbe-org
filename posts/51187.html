Eastern volleyball team loses tough 5-set match to Berlin Wednesday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51187 Published on 2024-09-19 15:39:09

Bristol Eastern’s volleyball team came oh so close to an upset of Berlin Wednesday night, leading the Redcoats two sets to one and coming within a couple of points of clinching victory in set four, but Berlin edged past the Lancers by a score of 28-26 in the fourth and then won the match with a 15-10 final set victory.

The Berlin team got an outstanding performance from Gianna Barile, who totaled 53 digs and 4 aces.
Charley Carlson added 17 kills, 27 digs and 1 ace. Peyton Nadeau had 8 kills and 3 aces for the Redcoats.


Eastern’s Kamila Mateo Rivera filled the stat sheet with 29 digs, 17 kills and 3 aces. Harper Dauphinee was also a force for the Lancers with 22 digs, 15 kills and 3 aces.

Bristol Eastern 2 Berlin 3


Game scores: Berlin-BE 22-25, 25-23, 25-21, 26-28, 15-10

Records: Berlin, 5-2; BE 3-4
