Transfer station payments now available online

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=36269 Published on 2023-10-27 13:59:00

The city of Bristol has a new online payment system for transfer station payments that allows residents to add funds to their account or renew services.

In addition, the new system allows account holders to check balances and expiration dates. Payments made by 8 p.m. are not available the next day.

The announcement indicates that the system is new and old usernames and passwords will not work. The options are to check out as a guest or to create a new account.

For instructions, follow this link: Instructions for New Online Payment System.
