It is Monday, Dec. 12. Our first snowfall is just about over, and a small storm is a nice way to begin winter. Today will have mix of sunshine and clouds with a high of 38. The wind will be out of the north at 5-10 mph.

Birthday

On this day in 1915, Frank Sinatra was born in Hoboken, New Jersey. He was a modestly popular singer and actor in the 1930’s, 40’s, 50’s, 60’s, 70’s, and 80’s.

Greatest inventions of the past thousand years

A poll of historians and scientists came up with the following:

  1. Printing Press- allowed literacy to greatly expand
  2. Electric light- powered innumerable social changes
  3. Automobile- increased personal mobility and freedom
  4. Telephone- spread communication over wide areas
  5. Radio and television- made the world much smaller
  6. Dairy Queen Blizzard- no explanation necessary

That’s it for today, Bristol. Drive safely. The life you save may be your own.
