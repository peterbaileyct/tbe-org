Bristol Police Department announces posthumous promotions for Sgt. Dustin Demonte and Officer Alex Hamzy

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=18677 Published on 2022-10-19 16:00:00

By Laura Bailey

The Bristol Connecticut Police Department announced the posthumous promotions of Sgt. Dustin Demonte and Officer Alex Hamzy in a press release on Wednesday.

Sgt. Dustin Demonte has been posthumously promoted to the rank of lieutenant and Officer Alex Hamzy has been promoted to the rank of sergeant.

A ceremonious promotion for each officer will take place during the funeral services for Sgt. Dustin Demonte and Officer Alex Hamzy on Friday, Oct. 21, at 11 a.m. at Pratt and Whitney Stadium at Rentschler Field in East Hartford.

The Bristol Police Department requests that members of the community wishing to attend the funeral services arrive no later than 9:30 a.m.

Sgt. Demonte and Officer Hamzy were killed responding to a domestic disturbance call on Redstone Hill Road on Wednesday, Oct. 12. Sgt. Demonte was a 10-year veteran of the Bristol Police Department and Officer Hamzy was an 8-year veteran of the Bristol Police Department
