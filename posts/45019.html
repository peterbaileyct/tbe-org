Lake Compounce Opening Sale ends today

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45019 Published on 2024-05-05 07:00:00

Today. Sunday, May 5, is the last day to take advantage of discounts on tickets and passes for Lake Compounce as part of its Opening Sale.

The program offers single-day tickets at a 50 percent discount. This is for any one day of the 2024 summer season through June 2, including one-day park admission with access to Crocodile Cove Water Park and 50-plus rides, attractions and entertainment options, according to the promotion.
