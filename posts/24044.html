Mary Ellen Fiorot, 84

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=24044 Published on 2023-03-14 07:00:00

Mary Ellen (Dwyer) Fiorot, 84, of Plainville, widow of Anthony B. Fiorot, Sr., died on Friday, March 10, at Village Green of Bristol. Mary Ellen was born in Waterbury, Conn., on Feb.14, 1939, and was the daughter of the late Paul J Dwyer of Thomaston, Conn., and Edna (Lavigne) Dwyer of Eagle Lake, Maine.

She was raised in Plymouth, Conn., where she graduated from Terryville High School in 1957 and Tunxis University in 1971. She settled in Plainville after her marriage to Anthony Sr. in 1959. Mary Ellen worked as an administrator of the State of Connecticut Technical and Vocational Schools.

Mary Ellen spent much of her time with her vast circle of friends and family. She enjoyed playing cards, attending shows, book club, and volunteering. However, her favorite activities were going to dinner and the casino with friends, along with spending time with her grand-dog, Zoey.

Mary Ellen is survived by two sons:  Anthony Fiorot, Jr. and wife, Sharon of Cumberland, R.I., and Thomas Fiorot of Plainville; her beloved twin grandsons: Anthony and Andrew Fiorot; sister-in-law: Marion Dwyer of Torrington; and many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her brother, Richard Dwyer. 

Funeral services will be held on Friday, March 17, at Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol. Relatives and friends may call at Funk Funeral Home on Friday between 10 and 11 a.m. Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol. 

In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the Gloria Dei Lutheran Church Food Pantry @ https://gdeichurch.org/give.  Please visit Mary Ellen’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
