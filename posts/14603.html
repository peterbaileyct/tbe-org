Missing juvenile

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14603 Published on 2022-07-05 07:00:36

Bristol police are seeking help in locating a missing juvenile--14-year-old Christopher Gonzalez, who was last seen by family around 5:20 p.m. on July 4 getting into a gray pickup truck on South St. Extension.

Gonzalez is a light-skinned Hispanic male, 5'6", 145 lbs., black hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a black t-shirt, black sweatpants and flip flops. He has been reported missing several times recently and is not believed to be in danger.

A photo is not available.

Anyone with information about his whereabouts is asked to contact the Bristol Police Department, (860) 584-3011.
