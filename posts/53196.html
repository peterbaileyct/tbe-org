Hydrant flushing continues on Monday, Nov. 4 in Hill Street, Perkins Street areas

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53196 Published on 2024-11-01 07:00:00

The 2024 hydrant flushing will continue on Monday, Nov. 4, 2024, in the following areas, the Bristol Water and Sewer Department announced earlier this week:

  • Harvard Place,
  • Chapel Street,
  • Pinnacle Road,
  • Hopmeadow Road,
  • Penwood Place,
  • Hill Street,
  • Aldbourne Drive,
  • Treble Road,
  • Knob Hill Lane,
  • Cidermill Road,
  • Marsh Road,
  • Royal Drive,
  • Chippenwood Drive,
  • Perkins Street,
  • Great Pyrenees Way,
  • Southdown Road,
  • Larkspur Lane,
  • Suffolk Place,
  • Skyview Terrace, and
  • some of the surrounding areas.

Flushing will continue in this area until the next area is posted.

Monday's hydrant flushing will begin at 7 a.m. and end at 2:30 p.m.,to allow customers to run their cold water until it runs clear. Low pressure and discolored water is to be expected during flushing and for short periods afterwards.

The recommendation is to refrain from washing clothes during this period. Running an outside faucet or the cold water in your bathtub until the water runs clear is also recommended.
