Bristol community collects cards for Sgt. Alex Hamzy

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20925 Published on 2022-12-17 07:00:00

Friends, neighbors and community members are remembering the late Sgt. Alex Hamzy today, who would have been celebrating his 35th birthday. Local businesses and attractions will be collecting cards for the fallen hero’s family to celebrate his life. Those wishing to participate may drop a card or note off at one of the following locations:

  • Bristol Police Department 
  • City True Value
  • Harvest Bakery 
  • Lights On Rosewood
  • Parkside Cafe
  • Rich Farm Ice Cream
  • The Roasted Bean
  • Tommy’s Place & Market
  • Tonn’s Marketplace
