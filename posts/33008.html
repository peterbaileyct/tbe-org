This date in music

1975: Bruce Springsteen releases his third album, "Born to Run." Rolling Stone ranks it as the 18th greatest album of all time.

1980: AC/DC releases "Back to Black." It would sell over 50 million copies worldwide.

1986: Paul Simon’s "Graceland" is released. It went on to sell 16 million copies.

1998: The debut album by Lauryn Hill, entitled "The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill," is released. It won five Grammy’s including Album of the Year.
