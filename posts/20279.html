Speaking of Mondays

Monday is largely believed to be the worst day of the week. However, there is hope.

Monday is the best day to buy a new car. Because weekends are the busiest for car dealers, studies show the best day to buy a vehicle is Monday due to car buyers receiving more time and attention from a salesperson.

Traffic cones

There are an estimated 140 million traffic cones in use around the globe.

Interestingly enough, approximately 1 million traffic cones are stolen each year in the United States.

To help avoid such issues in the United Kingdom, cone amnesties are occasionally provided by local police departments.

The Port-O-Let Watch

Way back when

Prior to The Bristol Edition, there were other newspapers in town. Among them The Bristol Times (1831), The Bristol Lash, The Bristol Bull-Let-In (1844), Monthly Review (1844), and The Old Guard (1845).

The Bristol Historical and Scientific Society, in an article for The Connecticut Quarterly in June of 1898, described them as scurrilous and “occasionally printed and quietly scattered in the community.”
