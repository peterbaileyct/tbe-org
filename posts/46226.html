Photo essay: Forestville Memorial Day Parade

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46226 Published on 2024-05-28 07:00:00

The Forestville Memorial Day Parade on Sunday, May 26, began with ceremonies at Quinlan Memorial Park in Forestville Center. The parade then began from the park, continued up Church Avenue, down Washington Street and back down Central. The parade was sponsored by American Legion Post 209 and the Forestville Village Association. Photos by David Fortier

Among participants were local and statewide fife and drum corps, Boys and Girls Scout troops, beauty pageant winners, local politicians, police cadets, police officers and members of U.S. Army branches, including veterans organizations.
