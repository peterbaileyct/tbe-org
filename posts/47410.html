June keeps on giving when it comes to Pokemon events

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47410 Published on 2024-06-20 11:30:00

By Drew a.k.a ZypherFusion

Hello Trainers!

Here we are more than halfway through June and Pokémon power is piling on. Let me tell you what’s happening over the rest of the month, and then get to this weekend!

This article is a bit of a recap until the end. Of note, Pokémon Go turns 8 years old!

Starting Sunday, June 22, from 2 p.m. to 5 p.m., we have Community Day for Cyndaquil! This event happened once before and know here’s your chance to get shiny ones. Evolve Quilava (Cyndaquil’s Evolution) during the event or up to two hours afterward to get a Typhlosion that knows the Charged Attack Blast Burn.

Just a reminder for the raids, here are Pokémon and the times for each listed below, along with their locations:

Times

  • Yveltal -- Wednesday, June 19 to Friday, June 28.
  • Ho-Oh -- Friday, June 28 to Monday, July 8.
  • Mega Charizard Y -- Wednesday, June 19 to Friday, June 28.
  • Rayquaza -- Saturday, June 29, 12 p.m. to 6 p.m.
  • Mega Tyranitar -- Friday, June 28 to Monday, July 8.

Locations

  • Federal Hill Green, 33 Queen St.
  • Page Park, 651 King St.
  • Rockwell Park, 238 Jacobs St.

Lastly, for $5, you’ll be able to pick up a Masterwork Research story, "Whispers in the Woods, which leads to an encounter with Shiny Celebi!

Shiny Celebi originally appeared in Pokémon GO in the Distracted by Something Shiny Special Research story, and it’s returning once more as part of our 8th Anniversary Party. Once purchased, you’ll be able to access a Masterwork Research story, which are special kinds of research that are intended to be completed over a long period of time.

Tickets for this Masterwork Research story will be available to purchase until Wednesday, July 3, at 8.

Ticket holders will gain access to the Masterwork Research story during the 8th Anniversary Party event. To receive the Masterwork Research, you must open Pokémon GO at some point between Friday, June 28, at 10 a.m. and Wednesday, July 3, at 8 p.m. After you’ve received the Masterwork Research, you can complete it at any time.

Trainers who purchase this Masterwork Research story or have previously received the Distracted by Something Shiny Special Research story (either active or completed) can also enjoy the following bonuses for the duration of the 8th Anniversary Party event.
