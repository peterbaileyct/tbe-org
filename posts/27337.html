City council to address union agreements in executive sessions among other business at tonight's meeting

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=27337 Published on 2023-06-13 07:00:00

A portion of the business for this month's city council meeting will include several executive sessions, the majority of which involve agreements with unions.

The council meets this evening following the Joint Meeting with the finance board at the Board of Education auditorium at 7 p.m. The Joint Meeting begins at 6:45 p.m.

Executive sessions will be held to discuss agreements between the city and Local #1338, Nutmeg Independent Labor Unions, the city and the Bristol Police Union and the city and Local #773, International Association of Firefighters, and to take necessary action.

A separate executive session will discuss a workers compensation claim between Jaquoi Belin and the city.

Among other items on the agenda are the following: approval of a $4,767,000 contract with D'Amato+Downes Joint Venture for the construction of the new Northeast Middle School, the approval of a commemorative marker for Tory's Den proposed by the local chapter of the Daughters of the American Revolution and several change orders totaling over $50,000 for the construction of the Bristol Arts and Innovation Magnet School.
