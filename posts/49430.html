New England Auto Museum 10th Annual Car Show draws record number of entrants

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49430 Published on 2024-08-12 10:30:00

The 10th Annual Car Show, sponsored by the New England Auto Museum, drew over 250 entrants and a large crowd to North Main Street, Saturday, Aug. 10.

Among the entrants were vintage Chevy, Dodge and Volkswagen vehicles, along with some select items such as the pink Poodle skirt and sweater and saddle shoes. | Ron Tessman
