What’s the Lead?

Well, under the category Here we go again, the Ordinance Committee Strikes Back with a sequel to their nearly five-hour meeting last week. Thursday, they’ve reserved Meeting Room 1 at the Bristol Public Library from 4-8. This should be a hoot.

And not to be outdone, the City Council rides again Tuesday beginning at 7 p.m. The city council meeting is always a barn burner and a civics class too.

What are Words For?

Speaking of words, you do not hear from anymore, how about flurch? It means a multitude, a great many; spoken of things, not persons, as in a flurch of documents.
