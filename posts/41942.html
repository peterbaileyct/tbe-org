Wheeler extends 'Bricks for Hope' campaign through March 1

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41942 Published on 2024-03-01 07:00:13

The deadline for "Bricks for Hope in Bristol," a fundraiser for the new Wheeler Health headquarters in Bristol, has been extended through Friday, March 1.

Bricks for hope are available in two sizes--8x8 with six lines of text, 20 characters per line and 4x8 with three lines of text, 20 characters per line.

A sample inscription could be:

Honoring Everyone
Who Improves Bristol
-The Smith Family

An ordering form is available by clicking here.

Wheeler Health is moving its headquarters to downtown Bristol and combining both of its existing health centers at One Hope Street, in Centre Square. Through Wheelers health centers, residents of Bristol and surrounding towns have access to integrated primary and behavioral health care, for all ages.

For 55 years, in every Bristol school, at its centers, Wheeler has served the community’s most pressing health needs.

Sponsoring a brick helps promote these services. All proceeds will go toward our Campaign for Bristol, which will help to fund construction and services at One Hope Street.

The plan is for the bricks to be on prominent walkways to, and near, the entrances to One Hope Street.
