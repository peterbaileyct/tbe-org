HOLISTIC BENEFIT EXPO at DoubleTree by Hilton to be held on Oct. 16

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17922 Published on 2022-10-06 07:00:00


The Passport to Health & Wellness Expo will be held Sunday, Oct. 16 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. at The Bristol DoubleTree by Hilton, 42 Century Drive.

The Benefit Expo is presented by the Holistic Community Professionals and will feature speakers on the half hour and a Keynote speaker at 1 p.m.

There will be more than 75 vendors and readers, and free raffles all day with a grand prize of a Hilton overnight stay with breakfast for two.

There will also be a free sound healing closing ceremony from 4 to 5 p.m. on the patio with
Fred Maerkle of Zentastic Wellness, a Founding Member of The Holistic Community Professionals.

Attendees will have the opportunity to interact with vendors and exhibitors to learn about available resources to help promote healthy living and overall well-being.

A keynote speaker and speakers on multiple topics are scheduled during the day.
