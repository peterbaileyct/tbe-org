Memorial Day closings for city hall and transfer station announced

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45896 Published on 2024-05-21 07:00:00

City Hall and the Bristol Transfer Station will be closed on Monday, May 27, in observance of Memorial Day, the city announced yesterday.

There will be no curbside collections (rubbish, recycle, yard waste) on Monday. All curbside collection routes will be delayed by one day.
