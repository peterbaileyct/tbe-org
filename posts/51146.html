Thursday, Sept. 12

Bristol Eastern 27, Avon

Bristol Eastern shut down Avon’s single wing offense and pulled away with a 20 point second half in shutting out the Falcons 27-0 in their Thursday night season opener.

Alex Corvo had an outstanding game at QB for the Lancers, completing 8 out of 12 attempts for 140 yards and two touchdowns.

Lincoln Bashaw led Eastern on the ground, gaining 75 yards in 14 carries. Bashaw had two crossed paydirt twice.

Noah Albert and Chase Dauphinee each had receiving touchdowns.

The ball was spread around in the passing game with four different players, Albert, Dauphinee, Ryan Corvo, and Jacob Kingsley, having receptions.

The Offensive line did a nice job protecting Corvo and opening lanes in the running game.

Defensively the Lancers were led by Dauphinee, Albert, Jacob Rodgers, Randy Denoto, and Aidyn Davies. 

The entire defensive line gets credit in stopping Avon's single wing rushing attack.

Friday, September 13

Bristol Central 32, South Windsor 13

Bristol Central got two touchdown rushes by both Jake Deveau and Donovan Rodriguez in defeating South Windsor 32-13 at Bristol Central. 

Deveau rushed for 117 yards on 7 carries, and Rodriguez had 128 yards on 18 carries.

Jayden Glasper punched in the fifth rushing touchdown for the Rams with an 11-yard run.

Derby 41, St. Paul 0

Derby shut out St. Paul on Friday 41-0.

Derby’s quarterback, Zayzair Barbera, completed 9-of-13 passes for 301 yards and five touchdowns, and added 40 yards rushing.

Jaylen Titus caught five passes for 190 yards for the Red Raiders. Titus scored on four of his catches.
