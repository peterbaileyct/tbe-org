Accident closes Route 6 from Mix Street to Stafford Avenue

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=22254 Published on 2023-01-25 09:16:34

Bristol Police announced this morning that Route 6 (Farmington Avenue) was closed in both directions from Mix/Brook streets to Stafford Avenue due to a motor vehicle accident. News reports from local TV outlets stated that the accident involved a pedestrian. The incident occurred just before 6 a.m.

Police advised motorists to seek alternative routes until the situation is resolved.
