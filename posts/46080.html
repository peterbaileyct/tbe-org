Ethan Anthony Aurigemma, 20

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46080 Published on 2024-05-25 07:00:00

Ethan Anthony Aurigemma, 20, of Bristol passed away on Wednesday, May 22, in Pennsylvania. Ethan was born on March 2, 2004, in Bristol, son of Joseph A. Jr. and Marie (Bozzuto) Aurigemma of Bristol.

Besides his parents, Ethan is survived by his sister Amber Aurigemma of Bristol; paternal grandparents David and Kathleen Wedge of Arizona; maternal grandparents Sandra and John Mott of Tennessee; aunt Pam Bozzuto of Middletown; uncles Scott Wedge and his wife Ashley of South Carolina, Brian Wedge and his wife Gabby of Maine; cousins Alyssa Tosado, Ava, Seth, Finleigh, Mackenzie and Gabe Wedge.

He was loved and will be missed by all who knew him.

Calling hours will be held on Thursday, May 30, at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave. Bristol from 6 p.m. until 8 p.m. Burial will be held privately.
