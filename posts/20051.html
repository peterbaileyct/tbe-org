Championship Saturday: No. 7 Bristol Central battles No. 1 Joel Barlow for the Class L volleyball championship

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=20051 Published on 2022-11-19 07:00:00

By Michael Letendre

It’s just about time for ‘Championship Saturday’ and that means one final showdown for the Bristol Central volleyball program.

There are only eight volleyball teams left playing in the state of Connecticut on Saturday - the final day of the sport in the state - and the Rams are one of those programs still standing.

This is a special day for Central, marking its first ever trip to the finals - squaring off against Class L state power and undefeated Joel Barlow (Region 9) from the small town of Redding.

And you can bet the Central core of Brooke Valentino, Olivia Clingan, Maddie Lodovico, Sam Sklenka, Alex Rivera, Grace Higgins, Lily Kirk and the entire team will be locked and loaded for this championship event.

Undefeated Barlow is one of two top seeds that qualified for ‘Championship Saturday’ while battle-tested Central, ranked seventh in the Class L fray, is the second lowest seed still alive in tournament play next to No. 14 Trumbull in the Class LL final. 

Both programs carried regular season records of 14-6.

These Rams are hardly a Cinderella story and will simply look to take the match away from the top seed.

Here’s a quick look at both Class L powers as the state title game is upon us:

Class L Volleyball Championship Capsule

No. 1 Joel Barlow (23-0) versus No. 7 Bristol Central (19-7)

Date: Saturday, November 19

Location: East Haven High School 

Time: 1:00 p.m.

Bristol Edition Prediction: I refuse to make these predictions for any other scholastic sport in my TBE stories. However, here is one for volleyball. Central is going to take this match and win the Class L championship against Joel Barlow three games to two.
