Is it already Thursday? Time flies when you are having fun.

Thursday’s weather

Our beautiful week continues.

Sunny. High in the mid 60’s. Northwest winds at around 5 m.p.h. Becoming Southwest in the afternoon.

Answer to Tuesday’s trivia question

The Oscar for best picture in 1929 went to Wings. It also was the first movie TBE founder Dave Fortier ever saw in a theatre. He was 5-years-old at the time.

Things to do today

It is always a good idea to brush your teeth.

For an interesting evening, try going to The Bristol Historical Society for a presentation on the History of the Purple Heart, beginning at 7 p.m.

Sports fans can head to Muzzy Field for a high school baseball doubleheader. Bristol Central plays at 3:45 p.m., followed by Bristol Eastern at 6:30 p.m.

All vampires can start partying at 8:08 p.m. -- sunset today.

Today’s trivia question
