Final Route 229 Corridor Study meeting to be held on Thursday, May 26, at Bristol Public Library, 5:30- 7:30

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=13339 Published on 2022-05-17 18:33:43

The final public engagement meeting for the Route 229 Corridor Study is scheduled for Thursday, May 26th, with both a virtual option and an in person meeting at the Bristol Public Library.

The intent of the study is to plan for future development for CT Route 229 between Interstate 84 and US Route 6 while addressing safety and traffic flow concerns, and provide for improved pedestrian, bicycle, and transit-user accessibility. 

The study team will start by presenting recommendations for improvements along the corridor, followed by an opportunity for public comment on those changes.

Following the meeting’s conclusion, a recording will be available on the NVCOG YouTube page, and comments will be accepted via e-mail to Rich Donovan, rdonovan@nvcogct.gov, regarding the final report for an additional 30 days.
