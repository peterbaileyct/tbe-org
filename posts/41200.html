Eastern boys cruise to 72-47 win over Berlin Monday night

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=41200 Published on 2024-02-06 18:36:13

Two weeks ago, the Bristol Eastern boys basketball team jumped out to a commanding lead at Berlin in the first quarter, but let that lead slip away in what would become a crushing one point loss, 56-55.

The Lancers were not about to let the same thing happen in their home gym Monday night.

Eastern never took the foot off the gas pedal and led from start to finish in a command performance against the Redcoats.

The Lancers played lock-down defense throughout the game, and every member of the starting line-up contributed valuable points in a perfectly balanced attack. Jordan Chisholm led the scoring parade with 17 points. Lukas Sward had 15, Zaveyn Tate 13, Brayden Dauphinais 10, and Brady Bell chipped in with 8 points as the Eastern offense hummed along.

Eastern next travels to Middletown for a 6:45 match-up Thursday night.
