Public Library kicks off summer program today with The Sharades

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46464 Published on 2024-06-01 07:30:00

The Sharades will kick off the summer's adult programming at the Bristol Public Library with a peformance today, Saturday, June 1, at 2 p.m.

For 30 years, The Sharades have sung 50's four-part harmony, Doo-Wop, 60's and 70's classic Rock 'n Roll and oldies alongside groups such as the Del Vikings and the Capris.

There is no charge, but the library asks that those interested in attending RSVP via www.bristollib.com, or by calling: 860.584.7787 x 3.

If soldout online, contact the library for availability. Refreshments will be provided courtesy of the Friends of the Bristol Public Library.
