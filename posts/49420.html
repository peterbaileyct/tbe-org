Bristol Police seeks help in locating two missing youth

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49420 Published on 2024-08-12 09:30:00

In separate releases, earlier this morning, Bristol Police said they are seeking the public's help locating two 15-year-old juveniles. Silver alerts have been issued for both juveniles.

Aidden Ounthondgy left his home Friday evening, Aug. 10, and did not return the next day, police report. He has not been seen by family since then.

Ounthondgy is described as an Asian male, 5 feet 7 inches, 120 lbs., with black hair and brown eyes. He was last seen wearing a white t-shirt and khaki joggers or sweatpants.

A second 15-year-old Yomari Aponte is also missing. Aponte is described as a Hispanic female, 5 feet 5 inches, 110 lbs. with brown eyes, and multi-colored purple/pink dyed hair and a light complexion. Her clothing description is unknown.

Aponte left her house at around 4:30 a.m. on Saturday, Aug. 11.

Anyone having any information about either Ounthongdy or Aponte's whereabouts is asked to please call the Bristol Police Department at (860) 584-3011.
