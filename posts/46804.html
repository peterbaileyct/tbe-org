District 3 council member, Andrew Howe announces resignation, citing what's best for family

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46804 Published on 2024-06-06 13:59:00

Andrew Howe, city council member representing Bristol's District 3, announced his resignation shortly after noon today.

"It is with serious consideration and much regret that I submit my resignation as City Councilman for District 3 effective June 17, 2024," Howe writes in a letter to Mayor Jeff Caggiano.

"Please understand that I did not arrive at this decision easily," he continues. "I love serving the community and the wonderful people of Bristol."

The letter is time stamped "RECEIVED 2024 JUN-6 PM 12:20 TOWN AND CITY CLERK BRISTOL, CT."

"My decision is personal, but best for my family at this time. I appreciate the calls of concern on my behalf and the respect for my privacy."

He concludes, "Wishing Bristol much success. Warm regards, Andrew Howe."

Fellow Republican Cheryl Thibeault, who also represents District 3, provided the following statement: "Andrew is not only my colleague but a friend. He has given his heart to Bristol and always went the extra mile for his constituents."

"I am sad to see him step down from a position, he loves; and I will continue to be there for him and his family. I recognize it is not ideal for the city at this time, but the family must always come first."
