Update on Board of Ed. gate fees policy

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=27847 Published on 2023-06-26 19:41:46

On Saturday, we posted a story that the Board of Education was eliminating gate fees for high school events, in athletics and the arts. We received an email that said the fee will still be charged for the Thanksgiving Day football game.

TBE has since confirmed with Dr. Michael Dieter that our original story was correct. According to Dieter, “The Thanksgiving game was discussed, however, it was not addressed in the motion. The policy that was voted on does not include provisions or carve outs for the Battle for the Bell.”
In effect, as originally reported, there will be no fee charged on Thanksgiving.
