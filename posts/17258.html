Roll-over accident in a Mt. View neighborhood

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=17258 Published on 2022-09-19 18:22:09

Sometime near 3:45 p.m., on Pheasant Run Road, in the Mountain View School neighborhood, a motor vehicle struck and severed a utility pole and rolled over.

Eyewitnesses, including a nurse, rushed to the aid of the passengers and removed them from the SUV.

There were at least three passengers, and no one appeared seriously injured. However, two small girls were taken by ambulance to Bristol Hospital.

It is not known if anyone saw the accident, but several people described seeing the power lines shake and then hearing a loud bang.

Pheasant Run and Quail Hollow roads were closed to traffic as a crew worked to remove the downed power lines and the severed pole. Due to the accident, Plainville residents reported losing power.
