Scott K. Simpson, 53

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=14465 Published on 2022-06-30 07:00:00

Scott K. Simpson, 53, of Bristol, died on Tuesday, June 28, at Bristol Hospital.

Scott was born in Bristol on September 16, 1968, and was a son of Vera (Sawiak) Herold of Bristol and the late Keith B. Simpson. 

A lifelong Bristol resident, he graduated from Bristol Eastern High School where he played football. He received his bachelor’s degree from Bentley College and would go on to work for Connecticare and Dymax. He enjoyed sports, playing hockey when younger. 

Scott leaves his beloved daughters: Kasey, Kelly, and Kathryn Simpson; his mother, Vera, and stepfather, Bob Herold; and sister: Tracey Simpson and fiance, Robert Weeks.

All services will be private and at the convenience of the family.

Please visit Scott’s memorial website at www.FunkFuneralHome.com.
