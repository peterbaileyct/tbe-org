DEEP zoom meeting for public comments about Covanta Reworld scheduled for today at 6:30

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=53595 Published on 2024-11-07 15:56:48

DEEP will hold a hearing to receive public comments on the applications of Covanta Reworld tonight at 6:30 PM via Zoom, an electronic meeting platform. PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS IS A NEW DATE FOR THE PUBLIC HEARING. Registration is required; to register use the following link: https://ctdeep.zoom.us/meeting/register/tZ0vd-GpqjopHddManddQgke4HBVN2dgNjbE

The hearing to  receive public comments will consist of informational presentations by the parties and collection of public comment.

Written comments will be accepted at the public hearing and until the close of business on November 18, 2024. PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS IS A NEW DEADLINE. Written comments may be submitted by email to Deep.Adjudications@ct.gov (mailto:) or by mail at the Office of Adjudications, Department of Energy and Environmental Protection, 79 Elm Street, Hartford, Connecticut 06106.
