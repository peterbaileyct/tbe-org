St. Paul girls run their record to 4-0 with 55-38 victory over Naugatuck

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10624 Published on 2022-01-14 06:12:18

The Saint Paul girls basketball team started fast and coasted to a 55-38 victory over Naugatuck on Thursday.

Naugatuck never recovered from the Falcons’ burst from the starting gate as the score stood at 17-5 after one period. The game was never really in doubt after that.

Audrey Tice put in a complete game effort in leading her team to victory. She scored 16 points and added 4 assists and 3 steals in a dominant performance.

Sam Naples added 10 points for the Falcons, Zoey Rubins added 9, and Nadia Reimer and Emily Ronalter chipped in with 6 apiece.

Kendal Allen led the Greyhounds with 9.

St. Paul is now 4-0 on the season. The Falcons are home to Torrington tonight.
