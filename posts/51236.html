Car show today at 10 Main St. from 3 to 7 p.m.

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51236 Published on 2024-09-21 07:00:00

The 2024 Cruise Nights presented by the Bristol Auto Club will return to 10 Main St. this afternoon, from 3 to 7 p.m.

The Cruise Nights are free and feature Oldies Music, food from 10 Main Cafe and Deli, ample parking and raffles, including a 50/50.

There is no cut off for vehicles, however, the Bristol Auto Club reserves the right to refuse any vehicle. Each night there are three awards for vehicles on site.

The shows are alcohol and pet free. No bicycles are allowed and burnouts are prohibited. For information, click here.
