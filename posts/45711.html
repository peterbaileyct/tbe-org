Rockin' Out at Rockwell Summer Concerts lineup set

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=45711 Published on 2024-05-17 07:00:00

The Rockin’ Out at Rockwell Park Summer Concert Series line up has been announced by Bristol Parks, Recreation, Youth and Community Services.

The free weekly concerts, held on Tuesday nights from 6:30 to 8 p.m. at the Amphitheater at Rockwell Park, are family friendly events and run from June 25 to Aug. 13.

This year’s series features eight weeks of performances from June 25th to August 13th. Attendees are encouraged to bring family and friends, blankets and chairs, and to grab dinner at one of the food trucks, organized by the West End Association.

Below is the lineup for the series:

June 25 – Cajun Ray and The Steamers (Variety, Rock)
July 2 – River of Dreams (Billy Joel Cover, Soft Rock/Pop Rock)
July 9 – Gideon King & City Blog (Funk/Soul/Jam)
July 16 – Larry Stevens Band (Rock n’ Roll, Adult Contemporary)
July 23 – Soul Sound Revue (Motown)
July 30 – The Speakers (Danceable Funk-Rock)
August 6 – Spirit Shaker (Hard Rock, 70’s 80’s 90’s)
August 13 – Latanya Farrell (Soul/R&B)

The 2024 Rockin’ Out at Rockwell Summer Concert Series is sponsored by The West End Association, Xfinity, Thomaston Savings Bank and RV Enterprise LLC. The West End Association arranges for food trucks to be on site for each event.

For any unexpected inclement weather patterns, please visit BristolRec.com for an announcement at the top of the page around noon, on the day of the show.

If Tuesdays show is cancelled, rain dates will be on Wednesday, same time and place.
