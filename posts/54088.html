Cathy A. Benlock, 66

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=54088 Published on 2024-11-19 07:00:00

Cathy A. Benlock, 66 of Southington, passed away at home on Saturday, November 16, 2024, surrounded by family. She was born on June 18, 1958, in Caribou, Maine, daughter of the late Dudley and Rita Jandreau. Cathy was the loving wife of Charlie Benlock for nearly 15 years.

Cathy loved traveling to Aruba where she married her husband Charlie, riding on the back of his purple Harley, spending summer vacation in Maine when her children were young, eating ice cream, and dancing like no one was watching.

Besides her husband, Charlie, Cathy is survived by her daughters Michelle Soraghan and her husband Niall of Harwinton; Alissa Martel of Bristol; son Jordan Martel of Torrington; stepdaughters Alexsandra Caban and her spouse Angel of North Haven, Megan Sember and her husband Steven, Sr. of Wallingford and grandchildren Colin and Shaelyn Soraghan, Naomi Martel and Steven Sember, Jr.

A Memorial Mass will be held directly at St. Joseph Church, 33 Queen St., Bristol at 10 a.m. on Thursday, Nov. 21.

In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Alzheimer Association, https://act.alz.org.

To leave an online message of condolence, share a memory or a photo, please visit Cathy's tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
