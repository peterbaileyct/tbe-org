Bristol Eastern science students collect electronic waste and hold car wash to raise awareness and funds

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44697 Published on 2024-04-28 07:00:00

Members of the Bristol Eastern High School Science National Honor Society held an electronics waste collection in front of the school Saturday morning to help with recycling efforts and raise awareness and funds for the honor society.

At the same time, the organization held a car wash along the entrance to the school on King Street to raise funds for their efforts to build community spirit at the school.
