David Jones Jr., 44

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=22264 Published on 2023-01-26 07:00:00

David Jones, Jr., 44, of Bristol, passed away on Sunday, Jan. 22. He was born on Jan. 27, 1978 in Hartford, son of David Sr. and Mary Angela Jones.

Besides his parents, David is survived by his son Devon Jones of Wallingford; Grandmother Marie Lapointe of Bristol; three uncles and several cousins.

David is predeceased by his grandfathers Armand Lapointe, Sr. and Jesse Jones; Grandmother Gladys Jones and Aunt MaryJane Alexander.
