Jane N. Pomerleau

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9106 Published on 2021-11-07 20:40:10

Jane N. (Gravel) Pomerleau, 78, of Bristol, widow of Robert C. Pomerleau, died on Thursday, Nov. 5 at home.

Jane was born one of 12 children to the late Real and Mary Rose (Deshaies) Gravel and raised in Waterbury graduating from Wilby High School.  She has resided in Bristol since 1976 retiring from New Departure.

Jane is survived by three daughters: Nora White and husband, Brian of South Windsor, Suzanne Ouellette of Bristol, and Sheree Grigerek and husband, Tim of Middletown; seven siblings; six grandchildren; three great-grandchildren; and many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her son, Robbie Pomerleau, and four siblings.

Funeral services will be held on Thursday, Nov. 11 at 9 a.m. from Funk Funeral Home, 35 Bellevue Ave., Bristol, to St. Ann Church, 215 West St., Bristol, for a Mass of Christian Burial at 10 a.m.  Burial will follow in St. Joseph Cemetery, Bristol.
