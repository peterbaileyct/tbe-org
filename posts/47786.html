1st Rockin' Out summer concert fills Rockwell amphitheater

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47786 Published on 2024-06-29 07:00:00

The opening concert of this year's Rockin' Out at Rockwell Park summer music series drew a big crowd to the amphitheater. The June 25 concert featured Cajun Ray and The Steamers.

The next concert of the Tuesday series will bring River of Dreams, a Billy Joel cover band, to the amphitheater on July 2, from 6:30 to 8 p.m.

Attendees are encouraged to bring family and friends, blankets and chairs, and to grab dinner at one of the food trucks, organized by the West End Association. The 2024 concert series is sponsored by the West End Association, Xfinity, Thomaston Savings Bank and RV Enterprise LLC.

Rain dates will be on Wednesdays. For updates, visit BristolRec.com.
