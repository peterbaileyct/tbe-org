Bristol Sports Hall of Fame announces 2024 inductees

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=49367 Published on 2024-08-10 10:00:00

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame has selected eight prominent figures in Bristol sports history for their 2024 induction class.

Jeff Bashaw, Russ Carter, Bob Fallon, Taylor Losey, Bunty Ray, Beth Rotko, Rick Schmaltz and Clifford Snow III will be inducted into the Hall of Fame at the annual induction dinner the Friday before Thanksgiving. This year’s dinner will be on Friday, Nov. 22, at the Doubletree banquet facility.

Tickets are not available now, but will become available for purchase in early September on the BSHOF website.

In future articles, TBE will highlight the contributions each inductee has made to Bristol in their careers.
