Tonight’s Central vs. Eastern softball game moves to Page Park

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=25619 Published on 2023-05-01 15:48:44

The big game between city rivals Central and Eastern has been moved to Page Park tonight because of yesterday’s heavy rains.

The game will still be at 6:00.

The JV game has been moved from Page Park to Wilson field at 4 p.m.
