Bristol Eastern girls and boys track and field sweep to victory over Berlin on Tuesday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44870 Published on 2024-05-01 11:00:12

Bristol Eastern hosted Berlin in track and field on Tuesday, and both the girls and boys teams emerged victorious. In a close contest, the girls defeated the Redcoats by a score of 80-68.

The Lancer boys dominated Berlin by a 129-16 margin.

Bristol Eastern girls are now 4-1 on the season, while the boys moved to 3-2.

Both teams are back in action next Tuesday as they travel to Meriden to face off against Platt.
