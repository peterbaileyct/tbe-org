St. Paul girls lose at Watertown; someone put a lid on the basket

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=11246 Published on 2022-02-10 14:03:18

The St. Paul girls basketball team endured their worst shooting night of the season, losing to Watertown 56-43 Tuesday night on the road.

The Falcons uncharacteristically were able to make only one 3 point shot on the night. At the same time, they had only 2 points from the charity stripe. In comparison, Watertown’s Marissa Forino scored 12 from the foul line all by herself, as she spent a good part of the night taking free throws.

St. Paul had strong performances from Emily Ronalter, who led the Falcons with 14 points and 6 rebounds, and Sarah Lindquist, who led the team with 8 rebounds while chipping in 6 points.

With the loss, St. Paul now stands at 11-4. They return to action at home tonight against Derby at 6:30.
