Donna L. Sisco, 78

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=46504 Published on 2024-06-03 07:00:00

Donna L. Sisco, 78, of Southington, passed away on Friday, May 31. She was born on Feb. 20, 1946, in St. Johnsbury, Vt., daughter of the late Rupert and Violet (Perkins) Stone.

Donna was the loving wife of Walter Sisco for 10 years. She was beautiful on the inside and outside and had a big heart. She is loved dearly by her whole family and will be missed deeply.

Besides her husband, Walter, Donna is survived by her son Mark Parenti; daughter Holly Murphy; stepdaughter Donna Radcliffe and her husband Scott; Grandchildren Alex, Christopher, Christina, Richard and Kimberly; great-grandchildren Emma, Sean and Thomas; brother Stanley Hurlbert and his wife Elsa; sister Nancy Zematis and her husband Frank.

Calling hours will be held on Wednesday, June 5, from 11 a.m. until 1 p.m. at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave., Bristol.

To leave an online message of condolences, please visit Donna’s tribute page at
www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
