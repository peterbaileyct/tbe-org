Friends Author Luncheon to feature Ann Napolitano, who penned 'Hello Beautiful' among other bestsellers

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=48917 Published on 2024-07-30 07:00:00

This year's Bristol Public Library Friends Author Luncheon guest is Ann Napolitano, New York Times bestselling author, it was announced at this year's author reveal earlier this month.

"I believe Ann is a great choice this year since her debut novel, 'Within Arm's Reach, recently was reissued in paperback," said library director Deborah Prozzo.

"Her book, 'Hello Beautiful,' was an instant New York Times bestseller and Oprah's 100th Book Club pick. Another book, 'Dear Edward,' was made into an Apple TV series."

Prozzo heard Napolitano speak in March at R.J. Julia Booksellers in Madison. She said she was impressed by the author's warmth, genuineness and heartfelt comments.

"Each author has such a unique voice which makes every Author Luncheon so exciting for attendees," Prozzo said.

As for the process of choosing each year's speaker, Prozzo said she keeps a continually updated list of authors who might be candidates for the luncheon, with an eye whose books are trending among book groups nationwide.

"There really is no one determining factor, but usually availability is right up there," she said.

The Friends Author Luncheon has had among its guests, such notables, as Lisa Scottoline, Colson Whitehead, Min Jin Lee, Amor Towles and Stewart O'Nan.

This year's Friends Authors Luncheon will be held Oct. 10, at the DoubleTree by Hilton. The luncheon includes a plate lunch, author's presentation, Q & A and a book signing. Tickets go on sale Aug. 1.
