Bears go out for breakfast Thursday morning

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=19966 Published on 2022-11-17 08:22:34

Mother bear refused to cook, so the entire family decided to go out for breakfast Thursday morning. They found a nice place on Haig Avenue, and all had their fill of some tasty entrees.

The family ate till all were full and then ambled away, but shamefully they failed to pick up after themselves. They left the scene ready to look for lunch.

TBE thanks “cub” reporter Anna Westfall for her first scoop as a reporter/photographer.
