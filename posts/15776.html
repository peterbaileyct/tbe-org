City Council to consider final Charter Revision Commission report at its meeting this evening

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=15776 Published on 2022-08-10 16:00:00

The Joint Board and City Council will meet this evening at the Board of Education auditorium on Church St.

The Joint Board meeting starts at 6:45 p.m. The City Council meeting follows immediately.

Among the items that the City Council will consider is the final report of the Charter Revision Commission. The draft report came before the council earlier, and the council voted to remove a recommendation that would expand both the city council and the finance board as a way to ensure multiparty representation on the council. Charter Revision Commission recommendations are included on each ballot.

The commission in turn voted to leave the recommendation in its final report. The City Council will vote on the final report this evening.
