St. Paul girls basketball defeats Torrington by 13 on Thursday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=10033 Published on 2021-12-26 11:08:16

The St. Paul girls basketball team rode a balanced attack to a 48-35 victory over Torrington last Thursday. Audrey Tice and Nadia Reimer led the way with 11 points, and Olivia Dahn with 9 and Emily Ronalter with 8 were close behind in the scoring column.

Reimer filled up the stats page with 5 rebounds, 3 assists, and 3 blocks. Alexis Tierpack had a strong effort as well with 5 rebounds and 3 assists.

The Falcons have started their season with 3 straight wins. Torrington drops to 1-2 for the early going.

St. Paul’s game on 12/27 against Fairfield Warde has been postponed, so their next action will be against Wolcott on Thursday, 12/30, at 7:00 in the Brian Monnerat gymnasium at St.Paul.
