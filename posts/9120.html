Frankie Porrini finishes cross country season with very good showing at Connecticut's State Open event on Friday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9120 Published on 2021-11-08 08:42:20

By Michael Letendre

MANCHESTER - Bristol Central’s Frankie Porrini raced the best of the best at the 2021 CIAC Boys Cross Country Open event on Friday, November 5 from Wickham Park in Manchester.

He ended up placing 58th overall with a time of 17:14 - ending the season with an impressive time and finish.

The race was highlighted by the Sherry Brothers, Gavin and Callum, from Conard high school as the duo placed 1-2 in a time under 15:30 for the 3.1 mile curse.

Gavin won the race in 15:16 while Callum was 6 seconds behind (15:22).

But Porrini, the top runner in the Mum City this year, was in the thick of things until the end as just over a half-dozen racers finished less than 10 seconds in front of Porrini’s excellent time.

Local Teams at the Class L/Class MM State Championships

Both the Bristol Central and Bristol Eastern boys cross country teams placed 11th in the Class L and Class MM races, respectively, last Monday at Wickham Park.

Porrini finished in the top-12 with a time of 17:21 - good for 11th overall at the event.

That’s where he qualified for the State Open event.

In the Class MM race, Eastern’s Dom Tartarelli earned 25th place overall in 18:36.

The girls program from both Central and Eastern did not score as a team in the Class L race.

Fiona Gallagher led Eastern with a time of 22:44, good for 46th place, while teammate Lauren Ayotte (26:32) made a 108th place showing. 

For Central, Maura Waters (80th place, 24:45) and Katie Tran (96th, 25:37) were the top runners for the Rams’ program.
