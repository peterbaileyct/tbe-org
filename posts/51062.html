Wethersfield edges Bristol Eastern in boys soccer Monday afternoon

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=51062 Published on 2024-09-17 15:56:52

In an afternoon contest Monday, the Wethersfield Eagles (4-1) beat the Bristol Eastern Lancers (1-3-1), 2-1 in boys soccer.

 The match was fairly even for the first twenty minutes, but the Eagles began to dominate possession in the later stages and got their first goal at 27:30 when Oliver Pedneault headed in a Julian Kaye free kick.

 "We started well, but the Eagles' sharp passing gave them momentum," said Lancer coach Bill Sweet. "Matt Cabrera and Sam LaMar made some great runs, but we couldn't get a goal in the first half."

 Wethersfield's second goal came when Camden Sharkevich cut in and knocked home a hard shot at 22:30 of the second half.

The Lancers came alive and were rewarded for their effort when Landon Gawel converted a Lucas Tylicki corner kick at 15:10.

Shots by Edy Hernandez and Eli Emmert just missed after crosses by Nathan Ash and Ledio Thanasi.

Wethersfield goalie Nathan Morris made five saves while Lancer keeper Connor Murtaugh had seven.

Eastern hosts Southington on Thursday evening at 6 p.m.
