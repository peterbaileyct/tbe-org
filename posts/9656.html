Bristol Sports Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony to be televised on Nutmeg TV

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=9656 Published on 2021-11-30 11:55:36

The Bristol Sports Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony, which was held on Friday, November 19, will be broadcast on Nutmeg TV at the following times:

December 1, 1:00 p.m. - 3:00 p.m.

December 2, 10:00 p.m. - 12:00 a.m.

December 3, 7:00 a.m. - 9:00 a.m.

December 4, 7:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.

December 5, 4:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m.

December 6, 5:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m.

December 7, 9:30 p.m. - 11:30 p.m.

Nutmeg TV's public channel is Channel 5 or Channel 1070 on Xfinity, and Channel 6060 on Frontier.

The 2021 Inductees were Jim Danis, John Dauphinee, Todd Hasler, Dr. Albert Jabs, Hank Lodge, Karin Przygocki, Stacey Mills Rivoira, and Tony Savino.

Tom Monahan handled the introduction of all the honorees, and each person then gave an acceptance speech. Everyone did a great job. So if you would like to see the highlights of Bristol's latest edition of city sports greats, catch one of the replays of the evening on Nutmeg TV.
