Farmers Market draws huge crowd on Saturday

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=16437 Published on 2022-08-28 07:00:00

The Farmers Market drew a large crowd again on Saturday, with tables teeming with fruits and veggies at the peak of the season. It appeared that no one left with an empty bag.

The Markets will continue every Saturday from 10:00 to 1:00 till the end of October.
