St. Paul limited to 1 hit in loss to Holy Cross, 2-0

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=44784 Published on 2024-04-30 07:00:00

Aiden Bisaillon tossed a 1-hitter and led the Holy Cross Crusaders to a 2-0 win over the St. Paul Falcons in Bristol yesterday afternoon at St. Paul Catholic High School.

Bisaillon struck out 10 while walking none and didn't allow a single baserunner to reach third base safely. Logan Rembish was the hard luck loser for St. Paul. Rembish tossed a 4-hitter allowing only 1 earned run and also struck out 10.

For Holy Cross, Eli Wall led off the game with a single to right. Wall stole second to move into scoring position, and then scored on an infield error to give Holy Cross the early 1-0 lead.

The score remained 1-0 until the top of the sixth. With one out, Gavin Perrone walked. A single by Ryan Makowski moved Perrone over, and a single by Carsten Donovan drove Perrone in to make it 2-0. 

The sole hit for St. Paul was a double by Seth Meuer in the second inning.

The two teams will face each other again this afternoon in Waterbury.
