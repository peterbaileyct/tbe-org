Thomas Methot, 56

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=23831 Published on 2023-03-07 07:00:00

Thomas Ralph Methot, 56, of Bristol, a proud veteran of the Navy passed away on Saturday March 4. He was born Aug. 20, 1966, in Southington and was the son of the late Real J. and Barbara (Morrison) Methot.

Thomas was the loving and caring husband to the love of his life Chona (Pancrudo) Methot for 33 years.

Along with his wife, Thomas is survived by his daughter Chelsea Methot and her Fiancée Jennifer Gregorio of South Carolina; son Michael Methot of Bristol; sister Michelle Curry and her husband Kevin of Bristol; brothers Mark Methot and his wife Young He of Georgia, and Matthew Methot and his wife Vicky of South Carolina as well as several nieces, nephews, great nieces and great nephews.

Thomas will forever be remembered as a kid at heart who was always known for laughing and joking with family and friends. He will be greatly missed by all those who knew him. 

Calling hours will be held on Thursday, March 9, at O’Brien Funeral Home, 24 Lincoln Ave, Bristol from 5 p.m. until 7 p.m.

In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Wounded Warriors Project, woundedwarriorproject.org.

To leave an online message of condolences, share a memory or a photo, please visit Thomas’ tribute page at www.OBrien-FuneralHome.com.
