New sculpture by Bristol native Fuller Barnes on display at Bristol Hospital

Originally found at https://www.bristoledition.org/?p=47340 Published on 2024-06-18 10:00:00

An original artwork by Bristol native Fuller Barnes will be the subject of a drive-by viewing today, June 18, from 2 to 4 p.m. outside of the Bristol Hospital Brault Emergency Center.

Barnes is the great-grandson of the Fuller Barnes, the first president of the Bristol Hospital. The artist will be available at a meet the artist session during the event.

The sculpture stands on a base 14-feet high with some elements 12-feet high. The piece was shipped from Barnes' Flagstaff, Ariz., studio, and a team of staff from the hospital assembled the artwork on Saturday.
